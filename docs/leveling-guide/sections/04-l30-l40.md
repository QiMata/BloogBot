# L30-L40 Bracket — Mount Milestone, Plate Armor, Warhorse/Felsteed

The "first mount" bracket and the most economically demanding. The character must accumulate ~100g for **Apprentice Riding (80g) + racial mount (20g)** at level 40, plus class-specific 40-level mount quests for Paladin (Warhorse) and Warlock (Felsteed). Plate armor unlocks for Warriors and Paladins at L40. The bracket also covers the SM full-clear (Library/Armory/Cathedral) and the first 40+ dungeons (Uldaman, Maraudon, Zul'Farrak entry).

---

## Bracket Goals (target end-state at L40)

- [ ] All L30-L40 talent points spent (10 across L31-L40; cumulative 31)
- [ ] **Apprentice Riding bought (80g) + racial mount (20g)** OR class mount quest completed (Pal/Warlock free mount)
- [ ] At least 3 instanced 5-mans cleared in this bracket (SM:Library/Armory/Cathedral, Uldaman entry, Maraudon entry)
- [ ] Hearthstone bound to bracket-end hub (Booty Bay continues, or Tanaris/Gadgetzan if late-bracket)
- [ ] Profession primary skill ~225 (Mining → Mithril, Herbalism → Liferoot/Khadgar's, Skinning → Heavy → Thick Leather)
- [ ] First-aid bandage rank advanced to Mageweave/Heavy Mageweave (skill ~200-225)
- [ ] Plate armor equipped (Warrior/Paladin); intermediate-mail unlocked (Hunter/Shaman) at L40
- [ ] Gold reserve 5-15g post-mount (training reserve for L40+ skills)

## Bracket Milestones — Per Level

| Level | Mandatory action | Why it matters |
|-------|------------------|----------------|
| 30 | Priest: Mind Control trained | Combat utility; instance/raid pull-tool |
| 30 | Shaman: Ancestral Spirit (resurrection) trained | Group-play essential |
| 30 | Rogue: Sap rank 2 trained | CC duration matters for instance pulls |
| 32 | Hunter: Pet skill point cap +1 | Build flexibility for pet specialization |
| 35 | Warrior: `The Affray` chain → Brutal Armguards reward | Ironforge/Hillsbrad chain; alternate path to Whirlwind Sword (SM Armory drop) |
| 36 | Druid: Faerie Fire (Feral) at Feral 5-pt talent | Improved threat + armor reduction debuff |
| 38 | Mage/Lock/Priest: rank 4-5 caster spells trained | Bracket-end DPS/healing parity |
| 40 | **Apprentice Riding 80g + racial mount 20g (~100g total)** | Mount = 60% speed = bracket-changing |
| 40 | Paladin (A): `Tirion Fordring` / `Verdant Stones` chain → free Warhorse | Free mount, bypasses 100g cost; ~30-min chain in Cathedral of Light |
| 40 | Warlock: `The Binding` continuation → Felsteed (free) | Free mount via 4-step ritual chain (cities + Felwood) |
| 40 | Warrior/Paladin: Plate armor auto-trained | +20% effective HP via Strength/Stamina plate gear |
| 40 | Hunter/Shaman: Mail armor auto-trained | Hunter/Shaman major DPS/Stam upgrade |
| 40 | Mage: Portal: Stormwind/Org/etc trained | Major group-utility, reagent: 1 Rune of Portals |
| 40 | All: 2nd talent tier capstone (31-pt talent reachable) | Tree-defining ability (Bloodthirst, Mortal Strike, Vengeance, Bestial Wrath, etc.) |

## Alliance Zones (30-40)

### Stranglethorn Vale (lvl 30-45) — continues

See [03-l20-l30.md](03-l20-l30.md#stranglethorn-vale-lvl-30-45-top-of-bracket). Bracket continues from Booty Bay; Hemet Nesingwary chain has highest XP/min in this band. Bloodsail Buccaneer chain gives reputation grind for Booty Bay vendor.

### Arathi Highlands (lvl 30-40)

See [03-l20-l30.md](03-l20-l30.md#arathi-highlands-lvl-30-40). Witherbark Village + Boulderfist Hall chains take both factions to ~L37. Stromgarde elite ruins are L40+, post-bracket.

### The Hinterlands (lvl 30-45) — A side

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Continent | Eastern Kingdoms |
| Capital hub | **Aerie Peak** (innkeeper, FP to Refuge Pointe/Light's Hope/Stormwind; **Wildhammer Clan** rep grind) |
| Sub-hubs | Quel'Danil Lodge (high elf outpost, neutral), Plaguemist Ravine (post-bracket prep) |
| Dungeon | None native; **Sunken Temple** (lvl 50-55) accessed via Hinterlands FP after attune chain |
| Faction enemies | Witherbark trolls (Witherbark Village shared with Arathi), Vilebranch trolls (Jintha'Alor — major elite outdoor area), Owlbeasts (Skulk Rock), Saltwater Snapjaws (coast) |
| Race fit | Wildhammer dwarves are Alliance-aligned but neutral-faction; rep grind via Revantusk-killing for Alliance |
| Quest chains | Falstad Wildhammer's Quel'Danil chain, **Jintha'Alor Vilebranch raid** (long chain, lvl 40-45 culmination), Seradane (Emerald Dragon shrine — green dragon spawns lvl 60 world boss) |

**Key NPCs:**
- Falstad Wildhammer — Aerie Peak ruler, Wildhammer rep gateway
- Primal Torntusk — Revantusk leader (Horde-aligned hub on coast)
- Vile Priestess Hexx — Jintha'Alor elite questline endpoint
- Jin'rohk the Breaker — Vilebranch troll elite

- Hammertoe Grez — gnome inventor inside Uldaman, intermission turn-in point

**Decision-engine cue:** Badlands is the **first zone with no Alliance hub**. Alliance must travel from Loch Modan or fly via Refuge Pointe. The Reliquary of Purity / Sunken Temple attune chain starts here for both factions.

## L40 Mount Acquisition

### Standard mount cost (1.12 patch, all classes except Pal/Warlock)

| Item | Cost (Friendly home-city rep) | Cost (Honored) | Cost (Exalted, max discount) |
|------|------------------------------|----------------|------------------------------|
| Apprentice Riding skill | 90g (undiscounted) | 81g | 72g |
| Racial mount | 10g (undiscounted) | 9g | 8g |
| **Total** | **~100g** | **~90g** | **~80g** |

**Note:** Numbers above reflect **undiscounted at vendor reputation Friendly**. Source: 1.12.0 patch notes restructured pre-1.12 fixed pricing into a rep-discount model. Most levelers buy at Friendly = ~100g total. `[verify pass 3 — exact discount tiers]`

### Class mount paths (free, quest-only)

| Class | Quest chain start | Steps | Reward |
|-------|-------------------|-------|--------|
| Paladin (A) | **Lord Grayson Shadowbreaker** at Cathedral of Light, Stormwind | 4 steps: Tome of Valor (L20 prereq), Verdant Stones, Manna-Enriched Horse Feed | Summon Warhorse (free mount) |
| Warlock | **Tannysa** in Stormwind (A) / **Strahad Farsan** in Ratchet (H) | 4 steps: Imp delivery to Tannysa+Strahad+Stormwind+Ratchet | Summon Felsteed (free mount) |

**Decision-engine rule:** Paladin/Warlock should use the class mount quest at L40 instead of buying. Banks 100g for L60 epic mount (1000g+100g).

### Race-mount quest waivers (1.12 specific)

- **Tauren** can buy a War Kodo at Bloodhoof Village (no extra cost; same prices as other mounts)
- **Night Elf** can buy a Saber from Dolanaar (same)
- All other races buy at home-city mount vendor

### Faction discount practical recommendation

Pre-L40 bracket: complete **3 quests in your home city** (e.g., Stormwind quest hub turn-ins) to push to Honored rep. Honored gives ~10% discount = saves ~10g on the 100g sticker price.

## Bracket Exit Conditions

When all of the following are true, engine should mark bracket complete and transition to L40-L50:

- [ ] `Level >= 40`
- [ ] `Talents.PointsSpent == 31` (cumulative)
- [ ] `Snapshot.Mounted == true OR Class IN {Druid (Travel Form OK), Shaman (Ghost Wolf OK)}` — Druid/Shaman can defer mount
- [ ] `Dungeons.Cleared.CountOfL30toL40 >= 3` (any 3 of: SM:Lib/Arm/Cath/RFD/Uldaman/Mara)
- [ ] `Hearthstone.Bind ∈ {Gadgetzan, BootyBay, Theramore, Brackenwall}` (next-bracket-ready)
- [ ] `Profession.Primary.Skill >= 200`
- [ ] `Gold >= 5g` post-mount (training reserve)

If `Snapshot.LevelDelta(start, end) < 10 && Snapshot.WallClock > 14h`, engine flags **bracket stall** — common causes:
- Insufficient gold for mount at L40 (most common)
- Profession lag (Mining/Herbalism not at 200)
- Stuck in SM full-clear loop without group progression
- World PvP camp on STV/Hinterlands/Tanaris road

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **At L30 turn-in**, dispatch class-quest priority (covered iter 36): Travel Form, Felhunter, Air Totem, Berserker Stance.
2. **Gold accumulation rule**: from L30 to L40, every quest reward gold + every gray-vendor proceeds + Mining/Herbalism gathered raw = enforce `Snapshot.Gold` projection >= 100g by L40 ding.
3. **At L40**, dispatch mount-acquisition priority. Paladin/Warlock take class quest (free); others buy at home-city mount vendor.
4. **Plate armor rule (L40)**: Warrior/Paladin should immediately re-equip to plate (auto-trained, no quest). Engine should detect `Equipment.ChestArmor.Type != "Plate" && (Class == Warrior || Class == Paladin) && Level >= 40` and route to AH or vendor for plate transition.
5. **Mail armor rule (L40)**: Hunter/Shaman auto-trained mail. Same logic as plate rule but for mail.
6. **SM full-clear sequence**: engine should run SM:Library → Armory → Cathedral in order across L32-42. Lockout is **per-wing** in 1.12 (not unified — Cataclysm change).
7. **Reliquary of Purity / Insignia trinket**: from Reliquary of Purity quest (Theldurin/Hammertoe chain ending in Uldaman). Engine should always pick up the chain in Badlands before entering Uldaman.
8. **Hemet Nesingwary chain bonus**: STV "Big Game Hunter" chain awards a **Hunter-only quest helm** at completion — Hunter bots should always complete this chain before leaving STV.
9. **Polymorph Pig book** `[verify pass 3]`: Mage chain in Hillsbrad Foothills (Bartolo Ginsetti A-side; Magus Wordeen H-side). Cosmetic Sheep variant.
10. **Profession specialty pickup**: at L40 with `Profession.Skill >= 200`, engine offers Blacksmithing specialty (Armorsmith vs Weaponsmith) and Leatherworking specialty (Dragon/Tribal/Elemental). User-config gate, not automatic.
