# L20-L30 Bracket — Mid-Range Zones, First Pet Stable, World PvP Hotzones

The "expansion" bracket. The character has finished their starter zone funnel and now branches out across **3-4 simultaneous quest zones** rather than a single zone-per-bracket. World PvP becomes a routine concern (Hillsbrad, Stranglethorn). The first **stable** opens for Hunters at L30, the second **demon pet** (Felhunter) for Warlocks at L30, **Travel Form** for Druids at L30, and **Air Totem** for Shamans at L30. Berserker Stance for Warriors at L30 is the first stance-quest with a small but non-zero failure window.

---

## Bracket Goals (target end-state at L30)

- [ ] All L20-L30 talent points spent (10 points across L21-L30; cumulative 21)
- [ ] Class quest L30 completed (Felhunter for Warlock, Air Totem for Shaman, Travel Form for Druid, Berserker Stance for Warrior, Hunter pet stable claimed)
- [ ] At least 2 instanced 5-mans cleared (BFD/Stockades/Gnomer/RFK/SM:GY all in scope)
- [ ] Hearthstone bound to a centrally located lvl-30 hub (Stormwind/Orgrimmar most flexible; Booty Bay if STV-bound)
- [ ] First-aid bandage rank advanced to Mageweave (skill ~150)
- [ ] Profession primary skill ~150 (Mining → Iron, Herbalism → Goldthorn, Skinning → Heavy Leather, Engineering → Mithril prep, etc.)
- [ ] Gold reserve ~25-50g toward L40 mount fund (target ~100g by L40)
- [ ] All bracket-appropriate flight paths attuned (Booty Bay/Grom'gol mandatory for both factions)

## Alliance Zones (20-30)

### Duskwood (lvl 18-30)

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Continent | Eastern Kingdoms |
| Capital hub | **Darkshire** (innkeeper, FP to Sentinel Hill/Lakeshire/Stormwind) |
| Sub-hubs | Raven Hill (Watchers' worgen camp), Twilight Grove (lvl 60 world boss spawn — emerald dragon) |
| Dungeon | None native (Stranglethorn boats from Lakeshire/Booty Bay) |
| Faction enemies | Worgen (Nightbane pack), undead (Ebonlocke crypt), Twilight cultists, Stitches (lvl 35 elite, walks Raven Hill→Darkshire), Mor'Ladim (lvl 32 elite roaming) |
| Race fit | All Alliance — bridge zone from Westfall/Redridge to STV |
| Quest chains | Madame Eva fortune-telling (links to Mor'Ladim), Sven Yorgen worgen, Watcher Backus, **The Legend of Stalvan** (long multi-zone, ends in elite Stalvan in Manor) |

**Key NPCs:**
- Lord Ello Ebonlocke — Darkshire mayor (no quests himself, atmosphere only)
- Lieutenant Sgt. Hartman — Darkshire guards
- Madame Eva — fortune-teller, chains into Mor'Ladim
- Watcher Backus — Stitches alarm questline
- Sven Yorgen — worgen / Worgen Manor chain
- Calor — Twilight Grove cultist quest (lvl 25-28)

**Decision-engine warning:** Stitches (lvl 35 elite zombie) periodically walks the Raven Hill→Darkshire road and **one-shots any L25-L28 questing solo**. Engine should detect path collision and reroute.

**Decision-engine cue:** STV is the **first multi-faction quest zone**. Booty Bay accepts both factions; quests there are neutral. The Venture Co. + Tigers + Hemet expedition are the bracket's highest XP-per-quest density.

| Bracket end | Alliance bind | Horde bind |
|-------------|---------------|------------|

**Decision-engine rule:** at L30, both factions converge on **Booty Bay** as the optimal hearth-bind for the L30-L40 bracket (Hemet hunting, ZG eventually, Tanaris approach). Engine should rebind there immediately upon L30 ding if STV quests are still available.

## Travel Speed Considerations

- **Druid Travel Form @ L30** (40% land speed, free, indoors-restricted) — bracket-end power spike
- **Shaman Ghost Wolf @ L20** (40% speed, indoors-OK) — bracket-start power spike
- **Hunter Aspect of the Cheetah @ L20** (30% speed, daze on hit cancels) — situational
- **Hearthstone CD** still 1 hour
- **STV Booty Bay boat** to Ratchet (5 min) connects EK ↔ Kalimdor without zeppelin
- **Theramore boat** from Menethil (5 min) — Alliance-only landing in Dustwallow Marsh L35-50 zone

**Decision-engine cue:** for Druids, post-L30 zone-rotation should prioritize the **largest map zones** (STV, Arathi, Hillsbrad) since Travel Form makes long traversal cheap. Other classes should still cluster quest hubs by hearthstone CD.

## Bracket Exit Conditions

When all of the following are true, engine should mark bracket complete and transition to L30-L40:

- [ ] `Level >= 30`
- [ ] `Talents.PointsSpent == 21` (cumulative)
- [ ] `ClassQuests.L30Complete OR Class NOT IN [Warrior, Druid, Shaman, Warlock]`
- [ ] `Dungeons.Cleared.CountOfL20toL30 >= 2` (any 2 of: Stockades/SFK/BFD/RFK/Gnomer/SM:GY)
- [ ] `Hearthstone.Bind ∈ {BootyBay, Hammerfall, Stormwind, Orgrimmar}` (next-bracket-ready hubs)
- [ ] `Profession.Primary.Skill >= 150`
- [ ] `Gold >= 10g` (Travel Form / training reserve)

If `Snapshot.LevelDelta(start, end) < 10 && Snapshot.WallClock > 12h`, engine flags **bracket stall** — common causes:
- Missing class quest at L30 (Druid Travel Form, Warlock Felhunter, Shaman Air Totem, Warrior Berserker Stance)
- Profession lag (skill < 150)
- Stuck in Stitches/Mor'Ladim corpse-camp on Duskwood road
- World PvP camp on Hillsbrad or STV road

## World PvP Hotzones (L20-L30)

| Zone | Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------|-----------|
| Hillsbrad (Tarren Mill ↔ Southshore) | Very high — open road faction conflict | Quest in caves/coast/Dun Garok, avoid the road |
| Stranglethorn Vale (Grom'gol ↔ Booty Bay) | High — both factions converge | Travel by boat from Ratchet to Booty Bay if possible |
| Ashenvale (Astranaar ↔ Splintertree) | Medium — NPCs on road provide some safety | Use FP rather than road traversal |
| Arathi Highlands (Refuge Pointe ↔ Hammerfall) | Medium — central road shared | FP-hop; avoid Galen's Fall central road |
| Desolace (Shadowprey ↔ Nijel's Point) | Low — map size + multiple routes | Generally safe |

**Decision-engine rule:** if `Snapshot.PvPEnabled == true && Position ∈ {HillsbradRoad, STVMainRoad}`, engine should set `RouteAvoidanceWeight = High` and prefer flight-path traversal even at gold cost.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **At L20 turn-in**, dispatch class-quest priority (Warlock Succubus, Shaman Water Totem, Druid Cat Form trainer) before any zone quest hub.
2. **At L30 turn-in**, dispatch class-quest priority (Druid Travel Form is highest-priority — 40% speed boost is bracket-changing).
3. **Hunter at L30** should visit a stable master to open slots 2 + 3, then re-tame for build diversity (e.g., one Boar for high-armor tanking, one Cat for high-DPS).
4. **Rogue at L24** must start Lockpicking via Ravenholdt trainer; ongoing skill grind through L30+.
5. **Stitches collision rule (Duskwood)**: engine reads `Snapshot.NearbyMobs.Has("Stitches")` and forces Hearthstone retreat if `Snapshot.Health < Snapshot.MaxHealth * 0.5 && DistToStitches < 30 yards`.
6. **STV at L30 entry**: prefer Booty Bay binding immediately on entry; FP from Lakeshire (A) / Tarren Mill via Grom'gol (H, after L35 chain) is the standard route.
7. **Centaur reputation choice (Desolace)**: engine reads existing `Reputation.Magram` or `Reputation.Gelkis` if pre-existing on alt account; otherwise default to **Magram (Horde-allied)** for Horde, **Gelkis** for Alliance — purely flavor in 1.12, no mount/recipe gates exist.
8. **Cross-zone walk through Wetlands dragonkin** (L25 char, lvl 30 Drakes): forbidden. Use Menethil boat or Ironforge → Loch Modan → Wetlands FP.
9. **Bracket-end gold reserve**: 10g minimum at L30 (Travel Form quest costs 0g; talents cost is 0; respec cost is 1-5g). Save the surplus for L40 mount fund.
10. **PvP-server world buffs**: at L29 (about to ding 30), ensure `Snapshot.HonorLevel > 0` to qualify for first-rank PvP gear vendor (Friendly title). Ranks 1-2 trivial, no decay penalty.
