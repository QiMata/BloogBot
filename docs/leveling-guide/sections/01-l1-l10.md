> **Pass 11.** This file replaces the prior pass-1 stub with concrete quest hubs and class-quest seeds across all 6 starter zones. Goal: by ding 10, character has cleared starting zone, picked up first major class kit, bound hearthstone to a bracket-2 hub, and is ready for Westfall / Loch Modan / Darkshore (Alliance) or Barrens / Silverpine / Stonetalon (Horde).

## Bracket goals

By **lvl 10** the character should have:

- ✅ Cleared starter zone (one of 6: Northshire, Coldridge Valley, Shadowglen, Camp Narache, Valley of Trials, Deathknell)
- ✅ Picked up first major class kit (e.g., Hunter's pet, Druid's Bear Form chain triggered, Warlock's Voidwalker chain triggered, Shaman's Earth Totem)
- ✅ Hearthstone bound to a logical inn (typically the racial capital's city inn or a hub matching planned 10-20 zone)
- ✅ Both bag slots filled with whatever 6-slot bags drop / are available
- ✅ ~1 silver minimum on hand (for rank-1 spells from trainer)
- ✅ Weapons-class characters: at least one weapon-skill at value ≥ 5

## Alliance starter zones

### Human — Northshire Abbey → Elwynn Forest (1-10)

**Northshire Abbey** is the lvl 1-5 starter zone, an isolated valley in northeastern Elwynn Forest. After lvl 5, players are funneled into the broader **Elwynn Forest** to continue questing through Goldshire (lvl 5-10 hub) toward Westfall (lvl 10-18) at the bracket end.

| Lvl | Hub | NPCs / Quests |
|---|---|---|
| 1-5 | **Northshire Abbey** (small valley NE Elwynn) | Marshal McBride (general quests), Brother Sammuel (Pal/Priest racial trainer), Falkhaan Isenstrider (Hunter — n/a, no Human Hunters in 1.12), Brother Wilhelm (Pal lvl-12 quest start NPC, located in Goldshire actually), Deputy Willem, Father Korin |
| 5-7 | **Goldshire** (Elwynn central hub) | Innkeeper Farley (hearthstone bind), Marshal Dughan, Smith Argus, Brother Wilhelm (Paladin Tome of Divinity start at lvl 12), Stoneskin/Bandit/Kobold quests |
| 7-9 | **Eastvale Logging Camp** (eastern Elwynn) | Supervisor Raelen (lumber + bandit quests), Eagan Peltskinner (Skinning trainer at Goldshire) |
| 9-10 | **Crystal Lake / Maclure Vineyards** (mid-southern Elwynn) | Farmer Furlbrow (Defias intel), William Pestle (Cookie's Stirring Rod quest tied to Deadmines later) |
| 10+ → | **Westfall** (lvl 10-18) | Cross south on Lions Pride Inn road; bind at Sentinel Hill |

### Dwarf / Gnome — Coldridge Valley → Dun Morogh (1-10)

**Coldridge Valley** is the lvl 1-5 starter zone, a snowy valley walled off from the main Dun Morogh. Both Dwarves and Gnomes start here. After lvl 5, players exit via the cave to Anvilmar / Frostmane Hold area in main Dun Morogh, eventually hitting Kharanos for lvl 5-10 questing.

| Lvl | Hub | NPCs / Quests |
|---|---|---|
| 1-5 | **Coldridge Valley** (snowy starter valley) | Hands Springsprocket, Sten Stoutarm, Brewmaster Drohn, Felix Whindlebolt (Rockjaw Trogg quests), Tannok Frosthammer (intro chain) |
| 5-7 | **Anvilmar / Frostmane Hold** (lower Dun Morogh) | Frostmane Trolls, Dwarf district intro |
| 7-10 | **Kharanos** (central hub) | Innkeeper Belm (hearthstone bind), Senir Whitebeard, Loslor Rudebelt (Frostmane troll quests), Granis Swiftaxe (Warrior trainer chain), Azar Stronghammer (Pal lvl-12 Tome of Divinity start) |
| 9-10 | **Steelgrill's Depot** (south central Dun Morogh) | Razzeric (Engineering quest seeds), Theodrus Frostbeard (Gnome chain), Razzashi raptor / Iceflow Lake quest tendrils |
| 10+ → | **Loch Modan** (lvl 10-20) | Cross east via the Stonewrought Pass road; bind at Thelsamar |

### Night Elf — Shadowglen → Teldrassil (1-10)

**Shadowglen** is the lvl 1-5 starter zone on Teldrassil. After lvl 5, players exit south to the broader Teldrassil zone, hitting Dolanaar (lvl 5-10 hub) toward Darnassus or onward to Darkshore for lvl 10-20.

| Lvl | Hub | NPCs / Quests |
|---|---|---|
| 1-5 | **Shadowglen** (small NE Teldrassil grove) | Conservator Ilthalaine, Tarindrella, Tenaron Stormgrip, Melithar Stormrage (intro), Dirania Silvershine (Druid trainer area), Athridas Bearmantle (Druid Tome of Divinity-equivalent racial start) |
| 5-7 | **Dolanaar** (central Teldrassil hub) | Innkeeper Keldamyr (hearthstone bind), Syral Bladeleaf (Druid-related quests), Tallonkai Swiftroot (Skinning + Hunter pet area), Iverron (Webwood Spider chain, Bashal'Aran ruins) |
| 7-9 | **Pools of Arlithrien / Bashal'Aran** | Webwood Spider Egg Sac quests, Bashal'Aran ghost quests |
| 9-10 | **Darnassus** (capital, accessible by foot or via Teldrassil tree path) | First trip for class trainer rank-ups + auctioneer |
| 10+ → | **Darkshore** (lvl 10-20) | Take Rut'theran Village → Auberdine boat south to Darkshore zone; bind at Auberdine inn |

## Horde starter zones

### Orc / Troll — Valley of Trials → Durotar (1-10)

**Valley of Trials** is the lvl 1-5 starter zone shared by Orcs and Trolls (Trolls don't have a separate starting zone in 1.12.1 — Sen'jin Village is a low-level Troll hub but starting is in Valley of Trials). After lvl 5, players head north to Sen'jin Village and Razor Hill in main Durotar.

| Lvl | Hub | NPCs / Quests |
|---|---|---|
| 1-5 | **Valley of Trials** (south Durotar enclosed valley) | Kaltunk (intro), Hana'zua, Galgar (Cactus Apple quests), Yarrog Baneshadow (Warlock starter — Hum-only? actually Voidwalker starts here for Orc Warlocks), Frang (Warrior trainer), Kor'mul (Orc Shaman starter chain quest at lvl 4 — Earth Totem chain), various racial trainer NPCs |
| 5-7 | **Sen'jin Village** (south coast, Troll-themed) | Innkeeper Grosk (hearthstone bind), Master Vornal, Shoja'my, Tai'jin (Mage trainer for Trolls; Priest trainer; etc.), Lar Prowltusk (Hunter trainer for Trolls/Orcs). Quest hub for lvl 5-8. |
| 7-10 | **Razor Hill** (mid-Durotar hub) | Innkeeper Kaylisk (hearthstone bind alt), Orgnil Soulscar, Takrin Pathseeker (Hunter-related), Kranal Fiss (Shaman Call of Fire chain start at lvl 10), Tarshaw Jaggedscar (warrior racial), Klannoc Macleod IS in **Barrens** for lvl-30 Affray quest, not here |
| 9-10 | **Tiragarde Keep** (south Durotar, lvl 10-12) | Lieutenant Benedict (Daggerspine pirates), repeatable Daggerspine quests |
| 10+ → | **The Barrens** (lvl 10-25) | North on the road from Razor Hill → Crossroads (~12-13); bind at The Crossroads inn (Innkeeper Boorand Plainswind) |

### Tauren — Camp Narache → Mulgore (1-10)

**Camp Narache** is the lvl 1-5 starter zone in southwestern Mulgore — a small tribal village. After lvl 5, players exit north through the Quilboar pass to Bloodhoof Village (lvl 5-10 hub) and onward to Thunder Bluff or Barrens for lvl 10-20.

| Lvl | Hub | NPCs / Quests |
|---|---|---|
| 1-5 | **Camp Narache** (SW Mulgore enclosed canyon) | Greatmother Hawkwind, Eyahn Eagletalon, Chief Hawkwind, Ruul Eagletalon, **Brave Strongbash / Brave Cloudmane** (Brave's Pilgrimage class-related), Skorn Whitecloud (Druid Bear Form starter — `[verify pass 3]`), various racial trainer NPCs. Camp Narache is small — typically completed in 1-2 hours of /played. |
| 5-7 | **Bloodhoof Village** (central Mulgore hub) | Innkeeper Kauth (hearthstone bind), Harken Windtotem, Ahanu (Hunter pet trainer area), Kennah Hawkseye (Quilboar quests + Tauren Mill chain), Brave Lightninghorn, **Bena Winterhoof** (Alchemy trainer), various Quilboar / Kodo Hide / Plainstrider quests |
| 7-9 | **Red Cloud Mesa / The Venture Co. Mine** (eastern Mulgore) | Quest tendrils with Venture Co. mining operations + Kolkar Centaur warbands |
| 9-10 | **Thunder Bluff** (capital — accessible via lift in Bloodhoof Village) | First city trip for class trainer rank-ups + auctioneer |
| 10+ → | **The Barrens** (lvl 10-25) — Crossroads via fly-or-foot east through Mulgore | bind at Crossroads (most common Horde lvl-10 hub) |

### Undead — Deathknell → Tirisfal Glades (1-10)

**Deathknell** is the lvl 1-5 starter zone in southwestern Tirisfal — a Forsaken village. After lvl 5, players exit to broader Tirisfal Glades, hitting Brill (lvl 5-10 hub) toward Undercity / Silverpine for lvl 10-20.

| Lvl | Hub | NPCs / Quests |
|---|---|---|
| 1-5 | **Deathknell** (SW Tirisfal Forsaken village) | Mordo Thunderhoof — wait that's Tauren. Deathknell NPCs: Caretaker Caice, Shadow Priest Sarvis, Novice Elreth, Joshua Kien, **Dannal Stern** (Warlock racial start), David Trias (Rogue trainer area chain), Magistrate Sevren (intro to Brill quests at level 7+), Rupert Boch (Mage trainer for Forsaken — Tirisfal location), Father Lankester (Priest racial start) |
| 5-7 | **Brill** (Tirisfal central hub) | Innkeeper Renee (hearthstone bind), Coleman Farthing, Magistrate Sevren, Apothecary Johaan (apothecary quest chain — Cold Hearth Manor / Solliden Farmstead). Quest hub for lvl 5-9 Tirisfal. |
| 7-9 | **The Bulwark** / **Cold Hearth Manor / Solliden Farmstead** | Various Scarlet Crusade leadup + Worgen quests; The Bulwark quest hub at southeast Tirisfal (gateway to Western Plaguelands lvl 50+ much later) |
| 9-10 | **Undercity** (capital — Royal Quarter, Magic Quarter, etc.) | First city trip for class trainer rank-ups |
| 10+ → | **Silverpine Forest** (lvl 10-20) | South via the Tirisfal-Silverpine road; bind at The Sepulcher (Innkeeper Bates) |

## Profession pickups in this bracket

| Profession | Optimal pickup | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Mining | Lvl 5+ | Bring up alongside questing in Dun Morogh (copper veins) / Mulgore / Durotar |
| Herbalism | Lvl 5+ | Peacebloom + Silverleaf in starter zones; pairs with Alchemy |
| Skinning | Lvl 5+ | Leather drops from beasts in starter zones; pairs with Leatherworking |
| Cooking | Lvl 1+ | Free vendor recipe from any innkeeper; useful for stamina-buff food |
| First Aid | Lvl 1+ | Free vendor recipe; bandages from cloth (linen at lvl 5+) — out-of-combat heal |
| Fishing | Lvl 5+ | Long-term grind; secondary skill for Cooking + Hydraxian Pure Water at 60 |

**Engine planning**: At lvl 5-7 in starter zone, dedicate ~30 min to first-profession pickup + Cooking + First Aid (3 secondary professions). All free recipes from city vendors.

## Hearthstone bind strategy

By lvl 9-10, hearthstone should be bound to:

| Faction | Race | Lvl 10 hearth target |
|---|---|---|
| Alliance | Human | Goldshire (Elwynn) → re-bind at Sentinel Hill (Westfall) at lvl 12 |
| Alliance | Dwarf/Gnome | Kharanos (Dun Morogh) → re-bind at Thelsamar (Loch Modan) at lvl 12 |
| Alliance | Night Elf | Dolanaar (Teldrassil) → re-bind at Auberdine (Darkshore) at lvl 12 |
| Horde | Orc/Troll | Razor Hill → re-bind at Crossroads (Barrens) at lvl 12 |
| Horde | Tauren | Bloodhoof Village → re-bind at Crossroads at lvl 12 |
| Horde | Undead | Brill → re-bind at The Sepulcher (Silverpine) at lvl 12 |

**Engine rule**: re-bind hearthstone whenever the planned lvl-10-20 hub differs from current bind; cost is just a small fee to the inn-keeper.
