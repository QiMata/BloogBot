# Western Plaguelands — Contested L51-58 Zone (Scholomance, Argent Dawn Entry)

Eastern Kingdoms post-apocalyptic Lordaeron zone — **Contested** with Argent Dawn-aligned hubs. Sub-zones: **Chillwind Camp** (Alliance hub, SW), **The Bulwark** (Horde hub, SE Tirisfal border), **Andorhal** (4-tower scourge city central), **Felstone Field** (necromancer farm), **Dalson's Tears** (poisoned well), **Caer Darrow** (Scholomance dungeon entrance island), **Hearthglen / Mardenholde Keep** (Scarlet Crusade fortress), **Sorrow Hill** (Pamela Redpath ghost), **Northridge Lumber Camp**, **Whispers Hill / Plaguemist Ravine**. **Scholomance** dungeon (lvl 58-63) at Caer Darrow. Notable mobs: **Scarlet Crusade** (Hearthglen + Mardenholde Keep — neutral-faction enemies), **Cult of the Damned** (Andorhal), **Stitched Plague Beasts** (Caer Darrow), **Mossflayer trolls** (north). Iconic chains: **The Battle of Darrowshire** (Pamela Redpath multi-zone), **Tirion Fordring** chain (cross-Argent Dawn).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: WPL is the **L51-58 contested zone with primary Argent Dawn rep grind**. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L57.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Chillwind Camp (A) / Bulwark (H) Inn is the bracket-6 default for Plaguelands grind.
3. **AD rep priority**: WPL + EPL is the **fastest AD-Honored grind** in vanilla 1.12. Engine should always-loot Scourgestones + Necromancer Skulls.
4. **Tirion Fordring chain**: pickup at L53+ but defer EPL completion to next bracket.
5. **Battle of Darrowshire chain**: iconic Pamela Redpath multi-zone — sad story, completionist value.
6. **Hearthglen Scarlet party**: at L52-55, Hearthglen Scarlet Crusade kills require party-pull invariant for elite officers.
7. **Scholomance dungeon prep**: at L58+, plan group queue. Skeleton Key chain pre-requisite.
8. **Cross-zone to EPL**: at L55+, head east to Light's Hope Chapel for L53-60 bracket.
9. **Profession early grind**: Chillwind/Bulwark sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman. Capital cities for advanced.
