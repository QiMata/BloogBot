# Arathi Highlands — Contested L30-40 Zone (AB BG Entry)

Eastern Kingdoms grassland zone — **Contested** with major settlements for both factions. Sub-zones: **Refuge Pointe** (Alliance hub), **Hammerfall** (Horde hub), **Stromgarde Keep** (lvl 40+ elite ruins — Syndicate-controlled), **Boulderfist Hall** (ogres), **Witherbark Village** (trolls), **Drywhisker Gorge** (kobolds), **Galen's Fall** (central PvP road), **Thandol Span** (Wetlands border bridge). **Arathi Basin BG entry zone** at southern road. No native dungeon — Stromgarde is open-world elite zone. Notable mobs: **Witherbark Trolls**, **Boulderfist Ogres**, **Syndicate** (Stromgarde, former Alteraci nobles), **Drywhisker Kobolds**, **Forest Trolls** (eastern), **Highland Boars**.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Arathi is the **L30-40 contested zone** for both factions. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L38.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Refuge Pointe (A) / Hammerfall (H) Inn is the bracket-4 default.
3. **Stromgarde Keep elite**: lvl 40+ open-world elite zone. Engine should defer to L38+ + party-pull.
4. **AB BG queue priority**: at L30+, queue AB via southern road. Cross-BG honor accumulation.
5. **PvP avoidance Galen's Fall**: contested central road. Engine should defer or use FP-hop.
6. **Cross-zone to Hinterlands**: at L35+, head east via Aerie Peak FP. Standard route.
7. **Profession early grind**: Refuge Pointe/Hammerfall sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman. Stormwind/Orgrimmar for advanced.
