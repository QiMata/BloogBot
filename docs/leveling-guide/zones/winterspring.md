# Winterspring — Contested L53-60 Zone (Everlook, Wintersaber Trainers, Mazthoril)

Kalimdor frozen forest zone — **Contested** with neutral **Everlook** goblin hub. Sub-zones: **Everlook** (neutral goblin main hub, center), **Frostsaber Rock** (**Wintersaber Trainers** — Alliance NE-only mount grind), **Mazthoril** (Blue Dragonkin lair), **Owl Wing Thicket** (atmospheric), **Ice Thistle Hills** (Yetis), **Demon Fall Canyon** (Demons + Lord Lokholar reagents), **Frostmaul Plateau** (Frostmaul Giants), **Frostwhisper Gorge** (Cult of the Damned), **Timbermaw Hold tunnel** (from Felwood, rep-gated). No native dungeon. Notable mobs: **Yetis**, **Frostsaber Cats** (Wintersaber rep), **Demons** (Demon Fall Canyon), **Frostmaul Giants** (rare elite), **Winterfall Furbolgs** (Timbermaw-hostile), **Blue Dragonkin** (Mazthoril). Iconic content: **Wintersaber Trainers reputation** (NE-only mount grind), **Lorax** Highborne chain.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Winterspring is the **L53-60 contested zone with Wintersaber/Timbermaw rep + Lorax chain**. Engine should plan ~7-10 hour completion through L58.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Everlook Inn is the bracket-6 default for cross-faction L53-60 questers + Wintersaber/Timbermaw farming.
3. **Wintersaber Trainers grind (NE-Only)**: 30-60 hour dedicated grind. Engine should pre-flag NE alts only.
4. **Lorax Highborne chain priority**: iconic L60 multi-step. Engine should pickup at L58+.
5. **Frostmaul Berserker party-pull**: rare elite at Lorax chain endpoint. Engine should encode group invariant.
6. **Demon Fall Canyon Lord Lokholar reagents**: AV BG preparation. Engine should plan reagent farming during AV weeks.
7. **Cross-Felwood tunnel**: at Timbermaw Friendly+, prefer tunnel route over outdoor.
8. **Profession early grind**: Everlook sufficient for limited primary professions; capital cities for Master.
