# Thousand Needles — Horde-Leaning Mid-Bracket Zone (L25-35)

Kalimdor canyon zone — **Horde-leaning** (Freewind Post is Horde) but accessible via The Great Lift to/from Barrens. **Pre-Cataclysm**: dry canyon (post-Cataclysm flooded). Sub-zones: **Freewind Post** (Horde main hub atop mesa), **Whitereach Post** (Horde sub-NE), **Mirage Raceway** (goblin engineer hub south), **The Great Lift** (central elevator to Barrens), **Highperch** (north harpies), **Darkcloud Pinnacle** (Grimtotem tauren), **Razorfen Kraul approach** (south, RFK dungeon adjacent), **Splithoof Heights** (centaur). No native dungeon — RFK is technically in southern Barrens border but accessed from this zone. Notable mobs: **Galak centaur** (Whitereach), **Roguefeather harpies** (Highperch), **Grimtotem tauren** (Darkcloud Pinnacle), **Razormane Quilboar** (RFK approach).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Thousand Needles is the **L25-35 Horde-leaning zone**. Engine should plan ~7-10 hour completion through L33 (less dense than Barrens).
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Freewind Post Inn is the bracket-3 default for Horde alts in this bracket.
3. **The Great Lift route**: primary cross-zone access between canyon-floor and Barrens. Engine should plan elevator-timing.
4. **Cliffwatcher Longhorn Grimtotem chain**: iconic multi-step. Engine should pickup at L27+.
5. **Mirage Raceway Engineering chain**: Goblin Engineering recipes + Tanaris setup. Engine should pickup at L30+.
6. **Highperch Wyvern elite**: rare elite hunt. Engine should party-pull at L30+.
7. **Cross-zone to Tanaris**: at L33-35, head south via Mirage Raceway. Standard route.
8. **1.12-specific dry canyon**: post-Cataclysm zone is flooded; engine should NOT use post-1.12 zone data.
