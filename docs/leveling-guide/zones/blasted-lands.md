# Blasted Lands — Contested L45-58 Zone (Lord Kazzak World Boss, Dark Portal Closed)

Eastern Kingdoms tainted demon-corrupted zone — **Contested** with Alliance Nethergarde Keep + small Horde camp. Sub-zones: **Nethergarde Keep** (Alliance hub, NW), **Dreadmaul Hold** (Horde camp, small FP-only), **Tainted Forest** (Tainted Furbolg + Wretched), **Dark Portal** (visual only — **closed in 1.12.1**, opens in TBC), **Lord Kazzak spawn plains** (south, lvl 60 world boss), **Serpent's Coil** (south coast). No native dungeon. **Quest density medium-low**. Notable mobs: **Tainted Furbolg**, **Wretched** (broken-Eredar demon-orcs), **Felwood-tier demonkin**, **Black dragon scouts**, **Doomguard** patrols. **Lord Kazzak** lvl 60 world boss with **Aura of Madness** AoE wipe mechanic — major raid coordination target.

**Iconic Dark Portal stone arch** — atmospheric placeholder, **closed in 1.12.1**.

### Lord Kazzak Spawn Plains (South — World Boss)

**Southern open plains** — Lord Kazzak world boss spawn area.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Lord Kazzak** | Lvl 60 world boss (Doomguard variant) |

**Boss mechanics:**
- **Aura of Madness** — AoE damage that **wipes pugs at low gear**
- 30+ raid group required for kill
- 16-hour respawn timer
- Drops Twilight Trappings (Brood of Nozdormu rep), tier-set items, weapon drops

**Decision-engine cue:** Lord Kazzak is **lvl 60 world boss**; engine should plan 30+ raid coordination + spawn-timer tracking.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Blasted Lands is **quest-light at L50+** — the L45-50 quests are bracket-prior. Engine should NOT plan dedicated leveling here.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Nethergarde Keep Inn (A) is viable; Dreadmaul Hold (H) lacks innkeeper — Horde alts should use Tarren Mill alternate.
3. **Lord Kazzak farming priority**: at L60, Kazzak fight requires 30+ raid group. Engine should never solo-queue Blasted Lands at L60 questing without first checking `RaidGroup.Active == true`.
4. **Dark Portal warning**: closed in 1.12.1; engine should NOT plan Outland traversal.
5. **Cross-zone to Swamp of Sorrows**: at L48+, head north for Sunken Temple prep.
6. **Wretched cleansing chain**: standard L48-52 quest content.
7. **Profession early grind**: Nethergarde Keep sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman. Capital cities for advanced.
