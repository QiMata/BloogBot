# Searing Gorge — Contested L43-50 Zone (Thorium Brotherhood, Blackrock Mountain Gateway)

Eastern Kingdoms volcanic zone — **Contested** with **Thorium Point** as the only zone hub (neutral Dark Iron splinter faction). Sub-zones: **Thorium Point** (neutral Thorium Brotherhood hub), **The Cauldron** (Dark Iron-controlled excavation), **The Slag Pit** (Cauldron elite extension), **Pyrox Blaze** (fire elementals), **Blackrock Mountain entrance** (gateway to BRD/UBRS/MC/BWL), **Sentinel Hill** (Cauldron-adjacent). No native dungeon — **Blackrock Depths** (lvl 52-60) accessible via Blackrock Mountain. Notable mobs: **Dark Iron Dwarves** (Cauldron + Blackrock Mountain), **Incendosaurs**, **Golems**, **Fire/Lava elementals**, **Bael'Gar** (lvl 50 elite). **Thorium Brotherhood reputation** primary grind starts here. **Mining-rich** zone (Mithril/Truesilver/Thorium dense).

**The Lvl 43-50 cross-faction quest hub** — Thorium Brotherhood splinter outpost.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Master Smith Burninate** | Mining-themed quest hub leader (fiery flux chain) |
| **Hansel Heavyhands** | Hostile fauna kill quests (incendosaurs, golems) |

**Quests (L43-50):**
- **Master Smith Burninate fiery flux chain** — Mining-themed multi-step

**Decision-engine cue:** Thorium Point is **the** Thorium Brotherhood rep entry hub. Engine should always-pickup all 6 quests on first visit.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Searing Gorge is the **L43-50 contested zone** with neutral Thorium Point hub. Engine should plan ~7-10 hour completion through L48.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Thorium Point Inn is the bracket-5 default for Mining/BRD-bound characters.
3. **Thorium Brotherhood rep priority**: pickup all Thorium Point quests on first entry. Bracket-defining for Sulfuron Hammer chain (BS).
4. **Mining-rich zone**: dense Mithril/Truesilver/Thorium veins. Engine should batch Mining + quests.
5. **Bael'Gar party-pull**: lvl 50 fire elemental rare elite. Engine should encode group invariant.
6. **Cauldron chain**: long multi-step ending in Slag Pit elite. Engine should plan 2-3 hour quest block.
7. **Cross-zone to Burning Steppes**: at L48+, head east for L50-58 bracket. Standard route.
8. **Blackrock Mountain dungeon prep**: at L52+, plan BRD group queue. Spectral Chalice quest pickup at Searing Gorge for Mining Smelt Dark Iron.
9. **Profession early grind**: Thorium Point sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman Mining. Capital cities for advanced.
