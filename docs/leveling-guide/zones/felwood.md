# Felwood — Contested L48-55 Zone (Timbermaw Tunnel, Cenarion+Timbermaw Dual Rep)

Kalimdor demon-tainted forest zone — **Contested** with Druid-aligned neutral hub (Emerald Sanctuary). Sub-zones: **Talonbranch Glade** (Alliance Sentinel FP camp, no innkeeper), **Bloodvenom Post** (Horde camp, no innkeeper), **Emerald Sanctuary** (Druid-only access; **Cenarion Circle** quest hub), **Timbermaw Hold** (long tunnel — rep-gated travel between Felwood and Winterspring), **Felpaw Village** (Furbolg-themed), **Jaedenar** (Shadow Council demon area), **Deadwood Village** (Deadwood Furbolg — hostile to Timbermaw), **Shatter Scar Vale** (corrupted treant). No native dungeon. **Densest single zone for L60 endgame Herbalism** (Sungrass + Blindweed + Gromsblood + Golden Sansam + Dreamfoil + Mountain Silversage all spawn). **Cenarion Circle + Timbermaw Hold dual rep grind** zone.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Felwood is the **L48-55 contested zone with dual Cenarion+Timbermaw rep grind**. Engine should plan ~7-10 hour completion through L52.
2. **Hearthstone bind warning**: Talonbranch Glade and Bloodvenom Post have **NO innkeepers** — engine should NOT bind here. Use Auberdine (Alliance) or Crossroads (Horde) alternates.
3. **Timbermaw hostility pre-grind**: ~25 Deadwood Furbolg kills at Deadwood Village to clear initial Hostile standing. Engine should plan ~30-45 minutes before tunnel approach.
4. **Cenarion Circle rep priority**: Felwood is **co-zone with Silithus** for Cenarion Circle rep accelerator. Engine should always-pickup Emerald Sanctuary chains.
5. **Lord Banehollow Jaedenar elite**: party-pull invariant. Engine should encode group invariant for Jaedenar final.
6. **Druid Pristine Hide chain**: Druid alt-specific Class chain. Engine should pre-flag Druid alts.
7. **Tunnel travel route**: at Friendly+ Timbermaw, prefer tunnel over outdoor routes for Felwood ↔ Winterspring.
8. **Profession gathering priority**: Felwood is **densest single zone for L60 endgame Herbalism**. Engine should plan Mining + Herbalism farming rotation.
