# Silithus — Contested L55-60 Zone (Cenarion Hold, AQ40 Attune Gateway, Silithyst 1.12 World PvP)

Kalimdor desert zone south of Tanaris — **Contested** with **Cenarion Hold** as the primary **AQ40 attune gateway** + Cenarion Circle rep grind hub. Sub-zones: **Cenarion Hold** (neutral both-faction hub), **Hive'Ashi** (silithid hive south), **Hive'Regal** (silithid hive east), **Hive'Zora** (silithid hive north), **Twilight Base Camps** (multiple cultist locations), **Bronzebeard Encampment** (Alliance researcher camp), **AQ20 + AQ40 raid entrances** at southern gate, **Silithyst Mounds** (**1.12 world PvP**). Notable mobs: **Silithid** (Stormers/Drones/Reavers — Hive'Ashi/Regal/Zora), **Twilight's Hammer cultists** (Twilight Lexicon Tome drops for AQ40 attune), **Sand Storm Rumblers**, **Anubisath Defenders** (lvl 60 elite, AQ40 prep). Iconic 1.12 content: **Silithyst world PvP** (collect crystals from Mounds, deliver to Cenarion Hold (H) / Bronzebeard Encampment (A) for honor + buff).

## Sub-Zone Breakdown

### Cenarion Hold (Neutral Both-Faction Hub)

**The Lvl 55-60 cross-faction quest hub** — Cenarion Circle HQ + AQ40 attune gateway.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Highlord Demitrian** | Cenarion Hold quest hub leader |
| **Bor Wildmane** | **Cenarion Circle quartermaster** (rep accelerator + AQ40 attune NPC) |
| **Master Hyacinth Sungazer** | Master Herbalism trainer (Artisan tier) |
| **Commander Mar'alith** | Night Elf NPC (atmospheric) |
| **Innkeeper (Cenarion Hold)** | Hearthstone bind (CRITICAL — bracket-defining default) |
| **Hippogryph Master / Wind Rider Master** | Flight master (FP to Gadgetzan/Cenarion Refuge/Moonglade/Marshal's Refuge) |

**Quests (L55-60):**
- **Cenarion Circle quest hub** — 10+ multi-step quests
- **Twilight Cultist counter-quests** — Cult of the Damned counter-faction kills
- **Anubisath Defender hunt** — pre-AQ40 elite scouts
- **Silithyst Mound collection** (1.12 world PvP) — honor + buff turn-ins
- **AQ40 attune chain** — Cenarion Circle Friendly gate + Twilight Lexicon Tome turn-ins

**Decision-engine cue:** Cenarion Hold is **THE** L55-60 Cenarion Circle rep hub. Bracket-defining default Hearthstone bind.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Silithus is the **L55-60 contested zone with primary AQ40 attune chain + Cenarion Circle rep grind**. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L60.
2. **Hearthstone bind PRIORITY**: **Cenarion Hold Inn is THE BRACKET-DEFINING DEFAULT BIND** for L55-60 Cenarion Circle rep + AQ raid prep.
3. **Cenarion Circle rep priority**: Friendly is **AQ40 attune unlock**. Engine should plan ~10-20 hour grind via Silithus quests + Twilight Cultist turn-ins.
4. **AQ40 attune at Friendly**: Bor Wildmane NPC. Engine should plan visit at L57+.
5. **Twilight Cultist farming**: Encrypted Twilight Texts (~20 rep) + Cultist Robe set (~75 rep). Engine should always-loot.
6. **Silithyst world PvP**: opportunistic pickup during questing. Engine should toggle Silithyst-bearer mode when not in combat or in group of 1.
7. **Anubisath Defender party-pull**: lvl 60 elite; engine should encode group invariant.
8. **Profession early grind**: Cenarion Hold sufficient for Master Herbalism (Master Hyacinth Sungazer); capital cities for other Master tiers.
9. **AQ20/40 raid prep**: at L60, plan group queue for AQ raids via southern gate.
