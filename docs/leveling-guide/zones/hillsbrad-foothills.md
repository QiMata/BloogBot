# Hillsbrad Foothills — Contested L20-30 Zone (PvP Hotzone)

Eastern Kingdoms contested zone — **Both factions have major settlements** (**Tarren Mill** Horde, **Southshore** Alliance). The Tarren Mill ↔ Southshore road is **the most-contested PvP corridor in 1.12.1**. Sub-zones: **Tarren Mill** (Horde hub), **Southshore** (Alliance hub), **Hillsbrad Fields** (worker NPCs), **Dun Garok** (lvl 30 dwarf undead — contested), **Daggerspine Coast** (south naga), **Sludge Fen** (south orc-goblin), **Yeti caves** (mountain). No native dungeon. Notable mobs: **Forsaken** (Tarren Mill side), **Yetis**, **Daggerspine Naga**, **Syndicate** (Alterac border), **Mudsnout Gnolls**, **Hillsbrad Farmers + Officers**, **Vile Fin Murlocs**. Iconic **Battle of Hillsbrad** Horde quest chain.

## PvP Hotzone Strategy

The **Tarren Mill ↔ Southshore road** is the most-contested PvP corridor in vanilla 1.12.1.

| Time of day | PvP risk |
|-------------|----------|
| Off-peak (2-6 AM server) | Low risk |
| Peak (7-11 PM server) | Very high — mass-PvP encounters common |
| Weekend events | Highest — Battle of Hillsbrad-themed PvP raid groups |

**Decision-engine rule:** if `Snapshot.PvPEnabled == true && Position ∈ {Tarren Mill ↔ Southshore Road}`, engine should set `RouteAvoidanceWeight = High` and prefer flight-path traversal (Aerie Peak/Refuge Pointe/Bulwark FP-hop).

---

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Hillsbrad is the **L20-30 contested zone** — both factions grind here. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L25.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Tarren Mill (H) / Southshore (A) Inn is the bracket-3 default.
3. **PvP avoidance**: Tarren Mill ↔ Southshore road is highest-risk PvP corridor. Engine should defer Hillsbrad to L25+ and prefer FP routes.
4. **Battle of Hillsbrad Horde chain**: kills flag PvP. Engine should ensure group support + PvP gear before triggering.
5. **Polymorph: Pig book** (Mage cosmetic): pickup at L25+ for Mage alts. Cosmetic-only, same mechanic as base Polymorph.
6. **Cross-zone to Western Plaguelands**: defer until L51+. The Bulwark is gateway but mobs there are gear-restricted.
7. **Helcular's Rod cross-faction**: both factions have parallel quests. Engine should pre-flag faction-side.
8. **Profession early grind**: Tarren Mill (H) / Southshore (A) sufficient for advanced professions at L20-30.
