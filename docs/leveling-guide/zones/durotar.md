# Durotar — Orc + Troll Starter Zone (L1-10)

Kalimdor desert/savannah zone — **Orc + Troll shared Horde starter zone**. Sub-zones: **Valley of Trials** (Lvl 1-5 Orc starter — enclosed), **Razor Hill** (Lvl 5-10 main hub), **Sen'jin Village** (Troll-themed sub-hub, south coast), **Echo Isles** (south island), **Tiragarde Keep** (north L12-15 quest area). Capital adjacent: **Orgrimmar** (north exit). Notable mobs: **Razormane Quilboar** (Barrens prep), **Burning Blade Cultists** (cross-zone), **Mangy Worgs**, **Plain Strider**. Class identity quests at L10: Hunter/Warlock/Priest racial spells; **Shaman Earth Totem** chain at L4-5 (Orc/Troll only).

## Sub-Zone Breakdown

### Valley of Trials (L1-5 Orc Starter)

**Enclosed Orc-only starter area** — first 5 levels. Located at the Valley of Trials cave entrance.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Kaltunk** | Quest hub leader |
| **Foreman Thazz'ril** | Quest giver (cave/trogg theme) |
| **Various class trainers** | Class-specific intro |

**Quests (Lvl 1-5):**
- **Cutting Teeth** — kill scorpid + worgs in Valley of Trials
- **Sting of the Scorpid** — collect scorpid stingers
- **Galgar's Cactus Apple Surprise** — quest cooking-themed
- **Vile Familiars** — Burning Blade purification

**Decision-engine cue:** Valley of Trials is the **enclosed L1-5 Orc starter**. Engine should plan ~1-1.5 hour completion through to L5 + Razor Hill arrival.

### Razor Hill (L5-10 Main Hub)

**The Lvl 5-10 quest hub** — most quests originate here.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Innkeeper Grosk** | Hearthstone bind (Razor Hill Inn) |
| **Orgnil Soulscar** | Quest hub leader |
| **Galgar** | Cooking quests |
| **Misha Tor'kren** | Skinning + Leather quests |
| **Various class + profession trainers** | Standard tier |

**Quests (L5-10):**
- **Burning Blade Cleansing** — Burning Blade Cultist kill quests
- **Mangy Worg Pelts** — Worg kills + Skinning intro
- **Razormane Quilboar** — Quilboar kill quests (cross-zone Barrens prep)
- **Plain Strider** — Strider kills
- **Speak with Sen'jin** — cross-zone introduction

### Sen'jin Village (Troll-Themed Hub)

**Troll-specific quest area** in southern Durotar.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Master Gadrin** | Troll racial quest hub |

## Class Quests (L1-10)

### Shaman (Earth Totem L4 + Fire Totem L10 Chain)

| Step | Source NPC | Reward |
|------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Kaltunk (Valley of Trials) | First Shaman racial quest |
| 2 | Various Valley + Razor Hill quests | Earth Totem at L4-5 |
| 3 | Travel to Razor Hill / Sen'jin Shaman trainer | Fire Totem chain at L10 (Telf Joolam mountain trip in Durotar) |
| 4 | Defeat Minor Manifestation of Fire | Fire Totem trained |

**Decision-engine rule:** Shaman is **Orc/Troll/Tauren** racial; Tauren version routes through Mulgore. Orc/Troll version routes through Durotar/Sen'jin. Engine should pre-flag for Shaman alts.

### Hunter / Warlock / Priest (L10 Racial)

| Class | Trainer | Quest |
|-------|---------|-------|
| **Hunter** | Razor Hill / Sen'jin / Orgrimmar | **Tame Beast** quest chain (lvl 10) |
| **Warlock** | Razor Hill / Sen'jin / Orgrimmar | **Summon Voidwalker** quest chain (lvl 10) |
| **Priest** (Troll only) | Sen'jin / Orgrimmar | **Hex of Weakness** racial spell (Troll-only) |
| **Priest** (Undead only) | Tirisfal Glades — not Durotar | (cross-faction) |

**Decision-engine rule:** at lvl 10, Orc/Troll character should travel to Orgrimmar for class trainer + class quest. Engine should plan Razor Hill→Orgrimmar path via north road.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Starter zone gate**: Valley of Trials (L1-5 Orc) → Razor Hill (L5-10) → Orgrimmar (class quests at L10) → Barrens (L10-25 transition).
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Razor Hill Inn (Innkeeper Grosk) is the bracket-1 default. Engine should bind here after first quest from Razor Hill.
3. **Shaman Earth + Fire Totem chain**: Orc/Troll Shaman must complete at L4-10. Engine should plan Telf Joolam mountain trip in Durotar for Fire Totem.
4. **Class quest dispatch (L10)**: Hunter/Warlock/Priest must travel to Orgrimmar for class trainer + class quest. Engine should plan north road path.
5. **Troll Priest Hex of Weakness**: race-locked; Sen'jin Village trainer source. Engine should pre-flag Troll Priest alts.
6. **Barrens transition**: at L8-10, head northwest to Crossroads via Razormane Quilboar zone. Standard route.
7. **Profession early grind**: Razor Hill sufficient for L1-15. Orgrimmar for advanced.
8. **Burning Blade Captain elite**: lvl 11 elite at Burning Blade camp. Engine should party-pull or solo at L12+.
9. **Sen'jin Village vs Razor Hill priority**: Troll alts grind quest mix at both; Orc alts grind Razor Hill primary.
