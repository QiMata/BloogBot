# Tanaris — Contested L40-50 Zone (Gadgetzan, Zul'Farrak Dungeon, Anachronos AQ40 Chain)

Kalimdor desert zone — **Contested** with neutral **Gadgetzan** goblin hub. Sub-zones: **Gadgetzan** (neutral goblin main hub), **Steamwheedle Port** (eastern coast goblin sub-hub), **Caverns of Time entrance** (Anachronos NPC), **Zul'Farrak entrance** (NW Sandfury pyramid), **Thistleshrub Valley** (resource gathering), **Land's End Beach** (south coast), **Lost Rigger Cove** (Bloodsail pirates), **Sandsorrow Watch**. **Zul'Farrak** dungeon (lvl 44-54) at NW pyramid. **Anachronos NPC** at Caverns of Time entrance — **the AQ40 Atiesh chain step 3 turn-in NPC** (Brood of Nozdormu rep). Notable mobs: **Sandfury trolls** (ZF area), **Hyenas**, **Scorpids**, **Basilisks**, **Silithid** (south, mid-bracket transition zone), **Pirates** (Lost Rigger Cove).

## Sub-Zone Breakdown

### Gadgetzan (Neutral Goblin Main Hub)

**The Lvl 40-50 cross-faction quest hub** — Steamwheedle Cartel goblin city.

|-----|------|

| **Narain Soothfancy** | Goblin mage + chain |

- **Zul'Farrak attune** (Hydromancer Velratha pre-quest) — cross-dungeon

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Tanaris is the **L40-50 contested zone** with neutral Gadgetzan hub. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L48.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Gadgetzan Inn is the bracket-5 default for cross-faction L40-50 questers + ZF/Atiesh chain alts.
3. **Marin Noggenfogger Elixir priority**: pickup at L40+. Random transformation effect = niche utility.
4. **ZF dungeon prep**: at L44-50, plan group queue. Sandfury troll suppression + Carrot on a Stick chain are pre-quest support.
5. **Anachronos AQ40 chain priority**: Druid/Mage/Priest/Warlock alts pursuing Atiesh must visit at L60 + Brood Neutral.
6. **Steamwheedle Port Mining**: at Mining 200+, visit Galvan the Ancient for Artisan unlock.
7. **Steamwheedle Cartel rep**: parallel rep grind via goblin port quests + Bloodsail Buccaneer kills (mutually exclusive).
8. **Cross-zone to Un'Goro**: at L48+, head south to Un'Goro Crater. Standard route.
9. **Profession early grind**: Gadgetzan sufficient for advanced primary professions at L40-50.
