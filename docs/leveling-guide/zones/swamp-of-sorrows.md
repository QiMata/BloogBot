# Swamp of Sorrows — Horde-Leaning Mid-Bracket Zone (L35-45, Sunken Temple Gateway)

Eastern Kingdoms swamp zone — **Horde-leaning** (Stonard is the only zone hub, no Alliance native settlement). Sub-zones: **Stonard** (Horde main hub, only zone hub), **Misty Reed Strand** (south naga), **Stagalbog** (cave with Bog Beast elite), **Sunken Temple** (Temple of Atal'Hakkar, lvl 50-60 dungeon entrance), **The Pool of Tears** (Atal'ai-themed cult area). **Sunken Temple** dungeon (lvl 50-60) — bracket-defining 5-man at central temple. Notable mobs: **Atal'ai trolls** (Sunken Temple guards), **Green whelps + Dragonkin** (Sunken Temple summoning grounds), **Murlocs** (Misty Reed), **Bog Beasts**, **Crocolisks**. Iconic content: **Sunken Temple attune chain** (Crescent Key required from DM East Pusillin chase), **Atal'ai Exile** chain.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Swamp of Sorrows is the **L35-45 Horde-leaning zone** with **Sunken Temple gateway**. Engine should plan ~7-10 hour completion through L42.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Stonard Inn is the bracket-4 default for Horde alts. Alliance alts must use Booty Bay alternate.
3. **Atal'ai Exile chain priority**: at L40+, pickup pre-Sunken Temple chain. Engine should plan Crescent Key acquisition first (DM East Pusillin).
4. **Sunken Temple dungeon prep**: at L50+, plan group queue via Crescent Key. Atal'ai Exile chain pre-quest mandatory for full reward.
5. **Bog Beast party-pull**: rare elite at Stagalbog. Engine should encode group invariant.
6. **Cross-zone to Blasted Lands**: at L45+, head south for L45-58 bracket.
7. **Profession early grind**: Stonard sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman. Capital cities for advanced.
