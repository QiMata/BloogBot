# Badlands — Horde-Leaning Mid-Bracket Zone (L35-45)

Eastern Kingdoms desert zone — **Horde-leaning** (Kargath is Horde, no Alliance hub native). Sub-zones: **Kargath** (Horde main hub, NW), **Camp Kosh** (south quest hub), **Lethlor Ravine** (dragonkin lair, lvl 38-42), **Camp Cagg** (NPC adventurer hub), **Uldaman entrance** (center of zone), **Apocryphan's Rest** (Theldurin the Lost area), **Dustbowl** (black dragon area). **Uldaman** dungeon (lvl 41-51) — bracket-defining 5-man at zone center. Notable mobs: **Black dragonkin** (Lethlor Ravine — Mother Smolderweb area), **Stonevault troggs**, **Dustbelcher ogres**, **Buzzards**, **Coyotes**, **Dark Iron Dwarves** (cross-Searing Gorge). Iconic **Reliquary of Purity** quest chain culminating at Archaedas in Uldaman.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Badlands is the **L35-45 Horde-leaning zone**. Engine should plan ~7-10 hour completion through L42 (less dense than Stranglethorn).
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Kargath Inn for Horde. Alliance must use Loch Modan/Refuge Pointe alternates.
3. **Reliquary of Purity priority**: pickup at L35 entry — provides bracket-defining Insignia trinket reward.
4. **Mother Smolderweb party-pull**: rare black dragon at Lethlor Ravine. Engine should encode group-pull invariant.
5. **Theldurin chain cross-zone**: long adventurer-themed chain extending to Loch Modan + cross-zone setup.
6. **Uldaman dungeon prep**: at L41+, plan group queue. Reliquary chain should be active before entry.
7. **Cross-zone to Searing Gorge**: at L42-45, head east for L43-50 bracket. Standard route.
8. **Profession early grind**: Kargath sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman. Capital cities for advanced.
9. **Alliance limitation**: Badlands is **the first zone with no Alliance hub**. Engine should flag for Alliance alts and plan FP routing from Loch Modan/Refuge Pointe.
