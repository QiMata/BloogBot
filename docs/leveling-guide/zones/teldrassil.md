# Teldrassil — Night Elf Starter Zone (L1-10)

Kalimdor world-tree-island zone — **Night Elf only starter zone**. Sub-zones: **Shadowglen** (Lvl 1-5 NE intro), **Dolanaar** (Lvl 5-10 main hub), **Aldrassil**, **Lake Al'Ameth** (south), **Wellspring River**, **Rut'theran Village** (south coast, boat to Auberdine). Capital: **Darnassus** (atop world tree, accessed via portal at Rut'theran). Notable mobs: **Bloodfeather Harpies**, **Webwood Spiders**, **Grellkin**, **Mangy Nightsabers**. Class identity quests at L10: **Druid Bear Form** (NE-only Moonglade chain), **Priest Starshards** (NE racial). Cross-zone exit: **Rut'theran Village boat → Auberdine (Darkshore)** for L10-20 transition.

## Sub-Zone Breakdown

### Shadowglen (L1-5 Starter)

**Enclosed Night Elf-only starter area** — first 5 levels. Atop the world tree.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Conservator Ilthalaine** | Quest hub leader |
| **Athrikus Narassin** | Mage trainer (initial) |
| **Sentinel Kyra Starsong** | Hunter trainer (initial) |
| **Various class trainers** | Initial class instruction |

**Quests (Lvl 1-5):**
- **Webwood Spiders** — kill spiders in Shadowglen forest
- **Spider's Silk** — collect spider silk for Tailoring/Cooking pre-quest
- **Grellkin Threat** — kill grellkin (small dragonkin-like beings)
- **Lord Melenas** — kill Furbolg elite quest boss

**Decision-engine cue:** Shadowglen is the **enclosed L1-5 NE starter**. Engine should plan ~1-1.5 hour completion through to L5 + Dolanaar arrival.

### Dolanaar (L5-10 Main Hub)

**The Lvl 5-10 quest hub** — most quests originate here.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Innkeeper Keldamyr** | Hearthstone bind (Dolanaar Inn) |
| **Sentinel Selarin** | Main quest hub leader |
| **Tallonkai Swiftroot** | Quest giver (cross-zone setup) |
| **Various trainers** | Class trainers + profession trainers |

**Quests (L5-10):**
- **Wellspring Falls** chain — wendigo + grell kills
- **Bloodfeather Harpies** — kill harpies at Bloodfeather Hovel
- **Lake Al'Ameth Naga** — south quests
- **Tendris Warpwood prep** — pre-Darkshore content (cross-zone)
- **The Crystal Pylons** — Forest Spider/Wisp scout chain

### Aldrassil (Mid-Zone)

**Mid-zone quest hub** — between Shadowglen and Dolanaar.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Tarindrella** | Quest giver (rare spider quest) |
| **Various druid trainers** | Druid-themed |

### Lake Al'Ameth (South)

**Southern lake region** — Naga combat zone (mid-game prep).

| Mobs | Level | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Lake Al'Ameth Naga | 8-12 | Cross-zone Darkshore prep |
| Mountain Cougars | 4-7 | Standard fauna |

### Wellspring River (Mid-Center)

**Cross-zone river area** — wendigo + grell kills.

| Mobs | Level | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Wendigos | 6-9 | Quest target |
| Grellkin | 7-10 | Quest target |

### Rut'theran Village (South Coast — Boat Hub)

**The boat hub** to Darkshore (Auberdine) — required transition point.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Vesprystus** | Hippogryph master (FP) |
| **Goram** | Boat captain (sails to Auberdine) |

**Boat schedule:** boat to Auberdine arrives every ~5 minutes; transition is automatic.

**Decision-engine rule:** Rut'theran Village is the **only Teldrassil → Darkshore exit**. Engine should plan boat-timing during cross-zone travel.

## Class Quests (L1-10)

### Druid (Bear Form Moonglade Chain)

| Step | Source NPC | Reward |
|------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Druid trainer in Dolanaar | "Cleansed Spirit" mid-quest |
| 2 | Travel to **Moonglade** (port from Druid trainer) | Free port at L10+ |
| 3 | Defeat **Lunaclaw** (lvl 11 elite) | Soul of Lunaclaw item |
| 4 | Return to trainer | **Bear Form** spell trained |

**Decision-engine rule:** Druid Bear Form is **the** L10 NE class quest. Engine should plan Moonglade portal route + Lunaclaw kill.

### Mage / Hunter / Priest / Paladin (NE-only)

| Class | Trainer | Quest |
|-------|---------|-------|
| **Mage** | Darnassus / Dolanaar trainer | Polymorph rank 1 (auto-trained, no quest) |
| **Hunter** | Dolanaar / Darnassus | **Tame Beast** quest chain (lvl 10) |
| **Priest** | Dolanaar / Darnassus | **Starshards** racial spell (NE-only) |
| **Paladin** | NOT NE — Paladin is Alliance-only Human/Dwarf | N/A for NE characters |

**Decision-engine rule:** at lvl 10, NE character should travel to Darnassus for class trainer + class quest. Engine should plan Dolanaar→Rut'theran Village→Darnassus portal route.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Starter zone gate**: Shadowglen (L1-5) → Dolanaar (L5-10) → Darnassus (class quests at L10) → Rut'theran→Auberdine (Darkshore L10-20 transition).
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Dolanaar Inn (Innkeeper Keldamyr) is the bracket-1 default. Engine should bind here after first quest from Dolanaar.
3. **Druid Bear Form chain**: NE Druids must complete Moonglade chain at L10 (Lunaclaw kill). Engine should plan portal route from Dolanaar trainer.
4. **Class quest dispatch (L10)**: Mage/Hunter/Priest must travel to Darnassus for class trainer. Engine should plan Rut'theran portal path.
5. **Darkshore transition**: at L8-10, head to Rut'theran Village boat to Auberdine. Standard route.
6. **Profession early grind**: Dolanaar sufficient for L1-15. Darnassus for advanced + Druid-themed Tribal LW.
7. **Lord Melenas elite**: lvl 9 Furbolg quest boss in Shadowglen. Engine should party-pull or solo at L10+.
8. **Boat schedule**: Rut'theran Village → Auberdine is ~5 min boat. Engine should plan timing for transition.
