# Elwynn Forest — Human Starter Zone (L1-10)

Eastern Kingdoms forest zone — **Human (only) starter zone**. Sub-zones: **Northshire** (Lvl 1-5 starter area), **Goldshire** (Lvl 5-10 main hub), **Stone Cairn Lake** (south), **Brackwell Pumpkin Patch** (east). Capital adjacent: **Stormwind City** (north). Quest hubs lead to Westfall (lvl 10-15 transition) and Redridge Mountains (lvl 15+ transition). Notable mobs: **Defias Highwaymen** (cross-zone enemy faction), **Hogger** (lvl 11 elite gnoll), **Princess** (lvl 12 boar elite). Class identity quests at L10: Mage/Hunter/Warlock/Priest/Paladin all branch from Stormwind trainers.

## Sub-Zone Breakdown

### Northshire (L1-5 Starter)

**The Human starter area** — first 5 levels.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Marshal McBride** | Quest hub leader |
| **Drostan** | Quest giver |
| **Brother Sammuel** | Paladin class trainer (initial) |
| **Falkhaan Isenstrider** | Mage trainer (initial) |
| **Various race trainers** | Class-specific intro |

**Quests (Lvl 1-5):**
- **The Wendigo Threat** — kill wolves in Northshire forest
- **Eagan Peltskinner** chain — wolf pelts for Skinning intro
- **Barrows of Barbed Tongue** — basic murloc kills
- **A Threat Within** — Goldshire transition quest

**Decision-engine cue:** Northshire is the **enclosed L1-5 starter zone**. Engine should plan ~1-1.5 hour completion through to L5 + Goldshire arrival.

### Goldshire (L5-10 Main Hub)

**The Lvl 5-10 quest hub** — most quests originate here.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Innkeeper Farley** | Hearthstone bind (Goldshire Inn) |
| **Marshal Dughan** | Main quest hub leader |
| **Smith Argus** | Blacksmithing trainer (alt to Stormwind) |
| **Goldshire Defender** | Various quest givers |
| **Stitches** | Reference NPC (low-tier) |

**Quests (L5-10):**
- **Westfall expansion quest** — gates Westfall transition at lvl 8-10
- **Wanted: Hogger** — kill Hogger gnoll elite
- **Princess** — kill Princess boar elite (Brackwell)
- **Murloc Pearls** — Stone Cairn Lake murloc grinding
- **Defias Bandanas** — Highway Bandit kills

### Stone Cairn Lake (Murloc Coastal)

**The southern lake region** — murloc combat zone.

| Stone Cairn Murloc Sapper | 8-10 | Higher-tier murloc with bombs |

**Quests:** "Murloc Pearls" (Goldshire) requires this zone.

## Profession Trainers

### Goldshire (Lvl 5-15 Tier)

- **Smith Argus**: Blacksmithing (Apprentice 1-75)
- **Goldshire Cook**: Cooking (Apprentice)
- **Goldshire Skinner**: Skinning (Apprentice)
- **Goldshire Tanner**: LW (Apprentice)

### Stormwind (Apprentice through Artisan tiers)

- **All primary professions** (Alchemy, Blacksmithing, Enchanting, Engineering, Tailoring, LW, Mining, Herbalism, Skinning)
- **All secondary professions** (Cooking, First Aid, Fishing)
- Specialty trainers (BS Armorsmith, Eng Gnomish, etc.) at L40+ skill+

**Decision-engine rule:** Goldshire is sufficient for L1-15 profession leveling. Stormwind for L20+ + specialty.

## Cross-Zone Transition Routes

### To Westfall (L10-15 Bracket)

| Path | Difficulty |
|------|-----------|
| South road from Goldshire | Easy (lvl 8-10 mobs only); ~5-10 min walk |
| Through Sentinel Hill | Direct route; encounter Defias Highwaymen mid-walk |

### To Redridge Mountains (L15-25 Bracket)

| Path | Difficulty |
|------|-----------|
| East road from Goldshire | Through Brackwell Pumpkin Patch (lvl 12-15 mobs) |
| Via Stormwind East Gate | Stormwind → Lakeshire FP eventually |

### To Stormwind (L1+)

| Path | Difficulty |
|------|-----------|
| North road from Goldshire | Easy; flat terrain |
| Northshire → Stormwind direct | Quick |

### To Stranglethorn Vale (L30+)

| Path | Difficulty |
|------|-----------|
| Via Stormwind boat to Booty Bay | Long FP route from Stormwind |
| Land route via Westfall → Duskwood | Multi-zone travel; level-restricted |

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Starter zone gate**: Northshire (L1-5) → Goldshire (L5-10) → Stormwind (class quests at L10) → Westfall (L10-15 transition).
2. **Hogger kill priority**: Hogger is a lvl 11 elite at the pasture cliffs; engage with full party or solo at L12+. Engine should encode group-pull for Hogger.
3. **Hearthstone bind**: Goldshire Inn (Innkeeper Farley) is the bracket-1 default. Engine should bind here after first quest from Goldshire.
4. **Class quest dispatch (L10)**: Mage/Hunter/Warlock/Priest/Paladin must travel to Stormwind for L10 class quest. Engine should plan north road path.
5. **Westfall transition**: at L8-10, head south to Westfall via Sentinel Hill road. Defias Highwaymen mid-route are the main risk.
6. **Profession early grind**: Goldshire trainers sufficient for L1-15. Engine should batch profession-skill grinding with quest XP.
7. **Cross-zone preparation**: at L10, engine should plan multi-zone strategy (Westfall + Redridge + Loch Modan tour for L10-20 bracket).
