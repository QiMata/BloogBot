# Silverpine Forest — Horde Mid-Bracket Zone (L10-20)

Eastern Kingdoms forested zone south of Tirisfal — **Horde L10-20 zone** designed for Undead levelers (also accessible to Orc/Troll/Tauren via zeppelin Org→UC then Brill→Sepulcher). Sub-zones: **The Sepulcher** (main hub), **Pyrewood Village** (worgen-by-night curse zone), **Ambermill** (Dalaran mage tower — hostile), **Fenris Isle** (Thule Ravenclaw cultist island), **The Dawning Isles** (Dalar Dawnweaver hub), **North/South Tide's Run** (coast). **Shadowfang Keep** dungeon (lvl 22-30) at Pyrewood northwest. Notable mobs: **Worgen** (Pyrewood, Shadowfang), **Rot Hide Gnolls**, **Moonrage Worgen Pack**, **Dalaran Mages** (Ambermill), **Thule Ravenclaw cultists** (Fenris Isle).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Silverpine is the **L10-20 Horde Undead default zone**. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L18.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Sepulcher Inn is the bracket-2 default for Undead/Horde alts.
3. **Pyrewood day/night cycle**: in-game time changes Pyrewood mobs. Engine should track time invariant for quest progress.
4. **Sons of Arugal rare elites**: multiple worgen rare elites; engine should party-pull or solo at L18+.
5. **Cross-zone to Hillsbrad**: at L18-20, head south to Tarren Mill via south road. Standard route.
6. **Shadowfang Keep dungeon prep**: at L20+, plan group queue for SFK. Arugal's Folly attune chain is bonus content.
7. **Profession early grind**: Sepulcher sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman. Undercity for advanced.
8. **Apothecary Renferrel alchemy bias**: Forsaken alts particularly favor this trainer for early Alchemy + cross-Plaguelands setup.
