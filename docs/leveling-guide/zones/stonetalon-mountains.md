# Stonetalon Mountains — Horde-Leaning Mid-Bracket Zone (L16-27)

Kalimdor mountain zone — **Horde-leaning** (Sun Rock Retreat is Horde) but contested. Sub-zones: **Sun Rock Retreat** (Horde main hub), **Stonetalon Peak** (sacred Night Elf/Tauren shared site, Cliffwalker chain), **Krom'gar Fortress** (hostile to Alliance), **Windshear Crag** (Venture Co. goblins), **Charred Vale** (south — dragon corruption + harpies), **Mirkfallon Lake** (Kobolds), **Webwinder Path** (Ashenvale connection). No native dungeon. Notable mobs: **Kobolds** (Mirkfallon Lake), **Venture Co. Goblins** (Windshear Crag), **Harpies** (Charred Vale), **Black dragonkin** (Charred Vale), **Grimtotem Tauren** (Grimtotem Post), **Centaur** (Galak). Iconic **Cliffwalker chain** (Stonetalon Peak — daughter avenged).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Stonetalon is the **L16-27 Horde-leaning zone**. Engine should plan ~7-12 hour completion through L25 (less dense than Barrens/Ashenvale).
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Sun Rock Retreat Inn is the bracket-3 default for Horde alts.
3. **Cliffwalker chain priority**: iconic multi-step chain. Engine should pickup at L18+.
4. **Charred Vale Black Drake**: rare elite hunt. Engine should party-pull at L25+.
5. **Cenarion Circle parallel**: Charred Vale dragonscale + Cliffwalker chains give Cenarion rep. Engine should always-pickup.
6. **Cross-zone to Desolace**: at L25-28, head south for L28-38 bracket. Standard route.
7. **Profession early grind**: Sun Rock sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman. Thunder Bluff for advanced.
8. **Quest density warning**: Stonetalon is **less dense** than Barrens/Ashenvale. Engine should prefer Barrens or Ashenvale for L18-25 if Crossroads quests still available.
