# Dustwallow Marsh — Contested L35-45 Zone (Onyxia's Lair, Theramore, Nat Pagle)

Kalimdor swamp zone — **Contested** with major Alliance city (Theramore Isle) and Horde outpost (Brackenwall Village). Sub-zones: **Theramore Isle** (Alliance hub — Lady Jaina Proudmoore's city + boat from Menethil), **Brackenwall Village** (Horde main hub), **Tabetha's Farm** (mage class hub + Polymorph variants), **Nat's Landing** (Nat Pagle fishing + Cooking books), **Stonemaul Ruins** (ogre quest area), **Witchwing Lair** (harpies), **Wyrmbog** (Onyxia's Lair raid entrance, SW). **Onyxia's Lair** raid (lvl 60, 40-man) — major raid endpoint. Notable mobs: **Stonemaul ogres**, **Bluegill murlocs**, **Witchwing harpies**, **Theramore-loyal humans** (Horde-only target), **Black dragonkin** (Onyxia's brood, near Wyrmbog).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Dustwallow Marsh is the **L35-45 contested zone**. Engine should plan ~10-12 hour completion through L42.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Theramore (A) / Brackenwall (H) Inn is the bracket-4 default.
3. **Onyxia's Lair raid prep**: Wyrmbog area is L50+ raid-tier. Engine should defer to L60 raid week.
4. **Tabetha's Farm Mage class quests**: pre-flag Mage alts for Polymorph variants.
5. **Nat Pagle fishing chain**: Fishing skill +75 jump at L40+. Engine should plan visit during STV Fishing Extravaganza prep.
6. **Mok'Morokk overthrow (Horde)**: cross-faction faction-shift chain. Engine should plan multi-step.
7. **Theramore Guard chain (Alliance)**: cross-zone Burning Steppes setup. Engine should pickup at L40+.
8. **Cross-zone Theramore boat**: Alliance fastest cross-Kalimdor route to Eastern Kingdoms. Engine should plan boat-timing.
9. **Profession early grind**: Theramore/Brackenwall sufficient for advanced professions at L35-45.
