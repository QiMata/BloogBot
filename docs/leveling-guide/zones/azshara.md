# Azshara — Horde-Leaning Mid-Bracket Zone (L45-55, Quest-Thin)

Kalimdor coastal zone NE of Ashenvale — **Horde-leaning** (Valormok is Horde, Talrendis Point is Alliance, both small camps without innkeepers). **Quest density is the lowest of any L45+ zone**. Sub-zones: **Valormok** (Horde sub-camp), **Talrendis Point** (Alliance sub-camp), **Bay of Storms** (Loramus demon hunter chain + demonic whelps), **Hetaera's Clutch** (hydra elite group), **Eldarath Ruins** (Highborne ghosts), **Forlorn Ridge** (**Azuregos** Blue Dragon world boss spawn area), **Bitter Reaches** (coast), **Spitelash Cove** (naga coast). No native dungeon. **Hydraxian Waterlords rep** entry hub at Duke Hydraxis area. Notable elites: **Azuregos** (lvl 60 world boss), **Hetaera** hydra queen, **Razelikh the Defiler** (Loramus demon hunter chain). Best used for **Mining + Herbalism gathering** (Mithril/Truesilver veins, Khadgar's Whisker + Firebloom).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Quest-thin warning**: Azshara has **the lowest quest density** of any L45+ zone. Engine should NOT plan dedicated leveling here.
2. **Hearthstone bind warning**: Valormok and Talrendis Point have **NO innkeepers** — engine should NOT bind here.
3. **Best use**: **Mining + Herbalism gathering zone** — Mithril/Truesilver veins + Khadgar's Whisker/Firebloom dense.
4. **Loramus chain priority**: long demon hunter multi-zone chain. Engine should pickup at L48+ but defer Felwood/Outland completion.
5. **Hydraxian Waterlords entry**: Duke Hydraxis area for MC water rune douse. Engine should plan trip during MC raid prep.
6. **Azuregos world boss**: lvl 60 raid coordination. Engine should track spawn-timer (~16h respawn).
7. **Hetaera hydra elite**: multi-head hydra; engine should party-pull at L50+.
8. **Profession gathering**: prioritize Mining/Herbalism farming over questing in Azshara.
