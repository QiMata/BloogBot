# Burning Steppes — Contested L50-58 Zone (Blackrock Mountain Gateway, Vaelan Seal of Ascension)

Eastern Kingdoms volcanic zone — **Contested** with **Blackrock Mountain** as the **gateway for ALL endgame raids** (BRD/UBRS/LBRS/MC/BWL). Sub-zones: **Morgan's Vigil** (Alliance hub, SW), **Flame Crest** (Horde hub, NE), **Blackrock Mountain entrance** (shared 5-raid + 2-dungeon entry), **Dreadmaul Rock** (ogres), **Altar of Storms** (Twilight Cult ritual area), **Ruins of Thaurissan** (Dark Iron ruins), **Roguefeather Den** (harpies), **Pillar of Ash**. Notable mobs: **Black dragonkin** (Whelps, Drakes, Spawn — Onyxia attune chain), **Blackrock orcs** (Spirestone/Smolderthorn/Firegut), **Firegut + Smolderthorn ogres**, **Spirestone trolls**. **Vaelan Seal of Ascension chain** (UBRS attune) + cross-faction **Onyxia attune chain** + **John J. Keeshan special-ops endpoint** (Alliance multi-zone from Redridge).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Burning Steppes is the **L50-58 contested zone with raid attune chain content**. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L58.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Morgan's Vigil (A) / Flame Crest (H) Inn is the bracket-6 default for Blackrock Mountain proximity.
3. **Vaelan priority at L57+**: Seal of Ascension chain is **mandatory** for UBRS attune. Engine should pickup before Drakkisath kill plans.
4. **Onyxia attune chain priority**: faction-specific multi-zone (Marshal Maxwell A / Eitrigg+Rexxar H). Engine should plan multi-zone progression.
5. **Drakkisath kill bundling**: at UBRS, Drakkisath kill simultaneously gives MC+Ony+BWL attune progress. Engine should encode 4-attune-bundle invariant.
6. **John J. Keeshan endpoint**: Alliance multi-zone from Redridge — pickup at L25 but final at L50+.
7. **Cross-faction Blackrock orc kills**: shared kill quest pool. Engine should batch.
8. **Pillar of Ash dragonkin**: lvl 52-58; engine should party-pull or solo at L58+.
9. **Profession early grind**: Morgan's Vigil/Flame Crest sufficient for advanced primary professions at L50-58.
