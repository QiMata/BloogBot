# Redridge Mountains — Alliance Mid-Bracket Zone (L15-25)

Eastern Kingdoms mountain valley zone east of Elwynn Forest — **Alliance L15-25 zone**. Sub-zones: **Lakeshire** (main hub), **Three Corners** (southwest crossroads to Elwynn/Duskwood/Stonewatch), **Tower of Ilgalar** (Shadowhide gnoll fortress), **Stonewatch Keep** (Blackrock orc stronghold), **Render's Valley** (Blackrock orc camp + Black Dragon Whelps), **Lake Everstill** (central lake, murlocs). No native dungeon. Notable mobs: **Shadowhide Gnolls** (Tower of Ilgalar), **Blackrock Orcs** (Render's Valley, northern roads), **Murlocs** (Lake Everstill), **Black Dragon Whelps** (Render's Valley north), **Boars** (Snarlflare rare elite). Iconic **John J. Keeshan special-ops chain** (Alliance-only multi-zone Burning Steppes prep).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Redridge is the **L15-25 Alliance bridge zone** between Westfall/Elwynn (L1-15) and Stranglethorn (L30+). Engine should plan ~10-12 hour completion through L23.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Lakeshire Inn is the bracket-3 default for L15-25 Alliance.
3. **Black Dragon Whelp farming**: lvl 22-27 dragons in Render's Valley provide high XP + Hunter pet target. Engine should plan dragon farming for Hunter alts.
4. **John J. Keeshan chain**: Alliance-only multi-zone extension to Burning Steppes (L50+). Engine should pickup at L25 but defer completion.
5. **Cross-zone to Duskwood**: at L20-25, head south via Three Corners to Duskwood. Standard route.
6. **Stonewatch Keep raid**: lvl 22-25 Blackrock orc fortress. Engine should plan party-based clearing.
7. **Profession early grind**: Lakeshire sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman (1-150). Stormwind for advanced.
