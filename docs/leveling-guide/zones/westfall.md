# Westfall — Alliance Mid-Bracket Zone (L9-18)

Eastern Kingdoms farmland zone — **Alliance L9-18 zone**. The **Defias Brotherhood storyline** is the dominant theme. Sub-zones: **Sentinel Hill** (main hub), **Saldean's Farm**, **Moonbrook** (Defias-controlled, Deadmines entrance), **Furlbrow's Pumpkin Patch**. **Deadmines** dungeon (lvl 17-20) is the bracket-defining 5-man. Notable mobs: **Defias Highwaymen** (cross-zone Alliance enemy), **Riverpaw Gnolls**, **Harvest Watchers** (mechanical constructs), **Old Murk-Eye** (rare elite murloc, lvl 16). Cross-zone exits: north (Elwynn → Stormwind), east (Redridge), south (Stranglethorn boat from Lakeshire).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Westfall is the **L10-18 Alliance default zone**. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L18.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Sentinel Hill Inn is the bracket-2 default.
3. **Deadmines preparation**: at L17-18, plan group queue for Deadmines dungeon.
4. **Defias storyline cross-zone**: People's Militia chain crosses Stormwind → Westfall → Deadmines. Engine should track per-character chain progression.
5. **Old Murk-Eye rare elite**: lvl 16 elite murloc on southern coast. Engine should party-pull or solo at L18+.
6. **Cross-zone transition**: Westfall → Redridge at L15+ if Westfall quests exhausted; or Westfall → Loch Modan via Stormwind tram + Ironforge.
7. **Profession early grind**: Sentinel Hill sufficient for L10-15. Stormwind for advanced.
