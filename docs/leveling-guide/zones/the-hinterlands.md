# The Hinterlands — Contested L30-45 Zone (Wildhammer + Revantusk + Vilebranch)

Eastern Kingdoms wooded mountain zone — **Contested** with Wildhammer dwarven hub (Aerie Peak A) and Revantusk troll hub (Revantusk Village H). Sub-zones: **Aerie Peak** (Alliance Wildhammer Clan hub, NW), **Revantusk Village** (Horde Revantusk Forest Troll hub, SE coast), **Quel'Danil Lodge** (High Elf neutral outpost), **Jintha'Alor** (Vilebranch troll **major elite outdoor area**, central), **Skulk Rock** (ogre + Owlbeast), **Plaguemist Ravine** (Plaguelands prep), **Seradane** (Emerald Dragon spawn shrine). Notable mobs: **Vilebranch trolls** (Jintha'Alor), **Witherbark trolls** (cross-Arathi), **Wildhammer Gryphon Roosts** (lvl 40 elite — Horde-hostile), **Owlbeasts**, **Saltwater Snapjaws** (coast). Iconic **Jintha'Alor Vilebranch raid** (L42-45 culmination quest).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Hinterlands is the **L30-45 contested zone**. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L40.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Aerie Peak (A) / Revantusk Village (H) Inn is the bracket-4 default.
3. **Jintha'Alor priority**: bracket-defining elite multi-tier area. Engine should party-pull invariant for Vile Priestess Hexx.
4. **Falstad Wildhammer / Primal Torntusk faction chains**: long Alliance/Horde multi-zone chains. Engine should pre-flag faction-side.
5. **Quel'Danil Lodge neutral**: High Elf cross-faction quests; engine should always-pickup on first visit.
6. **Cross-zone to Western Plaguelands**: defer until L51+. Plaguemist Ravine is gear-restricted gateway.
7. **Seradane Emerald Dragon**: L60 raid week target. Engine should track for raid coordination.
8. **Profession early grind**: Aerie Peak/Revantusk sufficient for advanced professions at L30-45. Capital cities for specialty.
9. **Wildhammer Gryphon hostility**: Horde-only target; Alliance-friendly. Engine should pre-flag faction-aware NPC interaction.
