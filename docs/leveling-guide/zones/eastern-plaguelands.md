# Eastern Plaguelands — Contested L53-60 Zone (Light's Hope Chapel, Stratholme, Naxxramas)

Eastern Kingdoms post-Scourge zone — **Contested** with **Light's Hope Chapel** as the only major **neutral both-faction hub** on Eastern Kingdoms (equivalent to Booty Bay). Sub-zones: **Light's Hope Chapel** (Argent Dawn HQ, neutral both-faction), **Marris Stead** (Nathanos Blightcaller — Horde-only questgiver), **Tyr's Hand** (Scarlet Crusade fortress, lvl 60 elite), **Plaguewood** (most-diseased), 4 PvP-control towers (**Plaguemist/Eastwall/Crown Guard/Northpass**), **Stratholme entrance** (NE corner), **Quel'Lithien Lodge** (High Elf hostile), **Naxxramas** (floating necropolis above Stratholme, lvl 60 raid). **Stratholme** dungeon (lvl 58-63) at NE corner. Notable mobs: **Scourge** (Plaguewood + Plaguemist), **Scarlet Crusade** (Tyr's Hand + Stratholme Live), **Plagued Wildlife**, **Cauldron Mistress**. **Primary Argent Dawn endgame rep zone**.

## Sub-Zone Breakdown

### Light's Hope Chapel (Neutral Both-Faction Hub)

**The L55-60 endgame quest hub** — Argent Dawn HQ, both factions share.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Lord Maxwell Tyrosus** | Argent Dawn HQ leader |
| **Argent Quartermaster** | AD rep accelerator + repeatable Scourgestone turn-ins |
| **Duke Nicholas Zverenhoff** | Multi-step AD-aligned quests + repeatable rewards |
| **Argent Officer Pureheart** | **Naxxramas attune NPC** (Honored AD requirement) |
| **Innkeeper (Light's Hope)** | Hearthstone bind (CRITICAL — bracket-defining default) |
| **Hippogryph Master / Wind Rider Master** | Flight master (FP to Chillwind/Bulwark/Tarren Mill/Stormwind/Stranglethorn) |

**Quests (L53-60):**
- **Argent Dawn full quest set** — 20+ quests, multi-step AD rep grind
- **Repeatable Scourgestone turn-ins** — basic AD rep accelerator
- **Plaguewood Cult of the Damned** — Plaguewood multi-step
- **Tyr's Hand Crusader chain** — lvl 60 elite repeatable

- **Tirion Fordring chain endpoint** — Hearthglen→Sorrow Hill→Light's Hope reunion
- **Battle of Darrowshire continuation** — cross-zone WPL Pamela Redpath chain

**Decision-engine cue:** Light's Hope is **THE** L55-60 Argent Dawn rep hub. Bracket-defining default Hearthstone bind.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: EPL is the **L53-60 endgame zone with primary Argent Dawn rep grind**. Engine should plan ~15-25 hour completion through L60.
2. **Hearthstone bind PRIORITY**: **Light's Hope Chapel Inn is THE BRACKET-DEFINING DEFAULT BIND** for L55-60 AD farms. Engine should rebind here at L55+ for entire Plaguelands grind.
3. **AD rep priority**: Honored AD is **Naxx attune unlock**. Engine should plan ~10-30 hour AD rep grind via WPL+EPL questing + Scourgestone/Necromancer Skull turn-ins.
4. **Tyr's Hand party-pull**: lvl 60 Scarlet elite repeatable; engine should encode group invariant.
5. **Naxx attune at Honored**: Argent Officer Pureheart NPC + reagents (60g + Arcane/Nexus Crystal + Righteous Orb).
6. **Plaguewood Cult of the Damned**: lvl 56-60 Scourge mobs; standard kill quests.
7. **4-tower PvP system**: contested PvP control points; engine should track active battles + use FP-hop alternates.
8. **Stratholme dungeon prep**: at L58+, plan group queue. Both Live + UD entrances at NE corner.
9. **Naxxramas raid**: lvl 60 raid week target; coordinate via Necropolis Beacon summon.
10. **Profession early grind**: Light's Hope sufficient for limited primary professions; capital cities for advanced.
