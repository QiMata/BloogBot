# Un'Goro Crater — Contested L48-55 Zone (Devilsaurs, Linken's Adventure, Crystal Pylons)

Kalimdor jungle crater zone — **Contested** with neutral **Marshal's Refuge** hub. Sub-zones: **Marshal's Refuge** (neutral main hub), **Fire Plume Ridge** (central volcano), **Slithering Scar** (silithid hive south), **Lakkari Tar Pits** (tar beasts + dinosaur fossils), **Golakka Hot Springs** (gorillas), **The Marshlands** (croc/raptor), **Brontotherium Plateau** (stegodons). No native dungeon. Notable mobs: **Devilsaurs** (lvl 55 elite — multiple roaming, Hunter Devilsaur Leather farm target), Stegodons, Pterrordax, Plant elementals (Bloodpetal), Gorillas, Slime, Tar Beasts, Silithid. Iconic content: **Linken's Adventure** Zelda-parody multi-zone chain (ends in DM West), **Crystal Pylon decoder** (J.D. Collie chain), **Pristine Plant samples** (Williden Marshal expedition). **Highest XP-per-quest** zone in the L48-55 stretch.

## Sub-Zone Breakdown

### Marshal's Refuge (Neutral Main Hub)

**The Lvl 48-55 cross-faction quest hub** — adventurer expedition camp.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Williden Marshal** | Quest hub leader (Pristine Plant samples expedition) |
| **J.D. Collie** | Crystal pylon decoder chain |
| **Spraggle Frock** | Apothecary chain |
| **Linken** | **Linken's Adventure** Zelda parody chain (multi-zone, ends in DM West) |
| **Innkeeper (Marshal's Refuge)** | Hearthstone bind |
| **Hippogryph Master / Wind Rider Master** | Flight master (FP to Gadgetzan/Cenarion Hold/Silithus) |

**Quests (L48-55):**
- **Williden Marshal expedition** — Pristine Plant samples (multiple plant kills)
- **J.D. Collie crystal pylon decoder** — central pylon network with red/green/blue crystals
- **Linken's Adventure** — Zelda-parody multi-zone Easter-egg chain (Linken's Sword of Mastery in DM West)
- **Spraggle Frock apothecary** — herb/plant collection
- **Devilsaur scout / Devilsaur Eye** — pre-Hunter farming setup

**Decision-engine cue:** Marshal's Refuge is **the highest XP-per-quest density** for L48-55. Engine should always-pickup all hub quests on first visit.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Un'Goro is the **L48-55 contested zone with highest XP-per-quest density**. Engine should plan ~7-10 hour completion through L52.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Marshal's Refuge Inn is the bracket-5 default for cross-faction L48-55 questers.
3. **Williden Marshal expedition priority**: bracket-defining XP windfall. Engine should always-pickup at L48 entry.
4. **J.D. Collie crystal pylon**: 3-color decoder chain — engine should encode red/green/blue alignment sequence.
5. **Linken's Adventure**: pickup at L48+ but defer Linken's Sword final to DM West group queue.
6. **Devilsaur farming priority**: at L55+ Hunter alts (Dragonscale LW spec), plan dedicated Devilsaur farming. ~6-8 spawn points + 4-6h respawn timer.
7. **Cross-zone to Silithus**: at L52-55, head south to Cenarion Hold for L55+ bracket.
8. **Cross-zone Tanaris north**: Devilsaur road hazard — gear-restricted at L48-.
9. **Profession early grind**: Marshal's Refuge sufficient for advanced primary professions at L48-55.
