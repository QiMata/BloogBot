# Alterac Mountains — Contested L30-38 Zone (Quest-Light, Ravenholdt Rogue Hub)

Eastern Kingdoms cold mountain zone — **Contested** but **quest-light** (lowest quest density of any L30+ zone). No formal faction hub — Strahnbrad is Syndicate-controlled (hostile), Ruins of Alterac is Crushridge ogre-controlled (hostile). **Ravenholdt Manor** at NE corner is a **Rogue-only safe haven** with Lockpicking trainer + PvP gear path. **AV battleground entry zone** (Alterac Valley). Sub-zones: **Strahnbrad** (Syndicate hostile), **Ruins of Alterac** (Crushridge ogres hostile), **Ravenholdt Manor** (Rogue-only safe), **Yeti caves**, **Slaughter Hollow** (ogres). Notable mobs: **Syndicate** (Aliden Perenolde), **Crushridge Ogres** (Mug'thol), **Yetis**, **Mountain Lions**, **Ice Trolls**.

## Sub-Zone Breakdown

### Ravenholdt Manor (Rogue-Only Safe Haven)

**The bracket-defining Rogue-class hub** — NE corner of zone.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Lord Jorach Ravenholdt** | Rogue-friendly NPC (master of Ravenholdt) |
| **Lockpicking trainer** | **Rogue-mandatory L24+ Lockpicking trainer** |
| **Pickpocketing trainer** | Rogue-mandatory |
| **Rogue PvP gear vendor** | Mid-tier Rogue PvP gear (rep-locked) |

**Quests (L24+ Rogue-Only):**
- **Pick Lock training** — Rogue-mandatory at L24+
- **Pickpocket grind** — Rogue Pickpocket rep grind
- **Cartoon-themed Rogue quests** — multiple class-locked

**Decision-engine rule:** Ravenholdt Manor is **Rogue-only** for Lockpicking trainer + Pickpocketing rep grind. Engine should pre-flag Rogue alts at L24+ for travel here.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Quest-light warning**: Alterac Mountains has **the lowest quest density** of any L30+ zone. Engine should NOT plan dedicated leveling here.
2. **Ravenholdt Rogue priority**: at L24+ Rogue alt, Ravenholdt Manor is **mandatory** for Lockpicking trainer.
3. **Hearthstone bind**: NOT viable for non-Rogue characters (no innkeeper). Use Hillsbrad Foothills hubs instead.
4. **Crushridge Mauler / Mug'thol elite**: rare elite hunts; engine should party-pull at L36+.
5. **AV BG queue**: at L51+, queue AV via south road. See [../pvp/alterac-valley.md](../pvp/alterac-valley.md).
6. **Cross-zone routing**: Alterac is **transit zone** rather than dedicated questing. Engine should use FP-hop unless Rogue needs Lockpicking.
7. **Pickpocket rep grind (Rogue)**: Junkbox turn-ins at Ravenholdt for mid-tier Rogue PvP gear path.
