# Orgrimmar — Horde Orc Capital (Kalimdor Durotar)

Kalimdor Orc capital — **the Horde primary city**. All primary + secondary profession trainers, all class trainers, central Auction House + Bank. **Districts**: Valley of Strength (AH/Bank — central hub), The Drag (profession trainers), Valley of Honor (Warrior/Shaman + BS Armorsmith), Cleft of Shadow (Warlock/Mage), Valley of Spirits (Troll quarter), Valley of Wisdom (Tauren quarter), Hall of Legends (PvP rank 11+ vendor), Grommash Hold (Thrall atmospheric). **Zeppelin** to Undercity (~3 min ride) + zeppelin to Grom'gol Base Camp (Stranglethorn, after L35 chain). Iconic class quest hubs: **Eitrigg** (Onyxia attune chain Horde origin), **Borgosh Corebender** (BS Weaponsmith specialty), **Okothos Ironrager** (BS Armorsmith specialty).

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Cross-Horde hub**: Orgrimmar is the **Horde primary city**. Engine should plan trip for Horde alts at L10+ for class trainers + profession trainers + AH access.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Orgrimmar Inn is viable for L20-30+ Horde characters; not bracket-1 default but useful for cross-continent travel.
3. **Class quest dispatch**: per-class district routing (Cleft of Shadow for Warlock/Mage/Rogue, Valley of Honor for Warrior/Shaman, Valley of Spirits/Wisdom for Troll/Tauren racial trainers).
4. **Eitrigg priority**: Onyxia attune Horde chain origin. Engine should auto-route at L60 raid attune stage.
5. **Borgosh Corebender / Okothos Ironrager**: BS specialty trainers (Weaponsmith / Armorsmith). Engine should pre-flag Warrior/Paladin alts at L40+skill 200.
6. **Mage portal trainer Cleft of Shadow**: Teleports L20 + Portals L40.
7. **Zeppelin cross-continent**: Org→UC + Org→Grom'gol (after L35) primary Horde transit.
8. **Cross-continent transit hub**: zeppelins are Horde primary cross-continent. Engine should cache routes.
