# Ironforge — Alliance Dwarven Capital (Eastern Kingdoms)

Eastern Kingdoms dwarven capital — **the Alliance secondary city** (after Stormwind), built into the mountain south of Dun Morogh. **Districts**: The Commons (AH/Bank/profession central), Tinkertown (Engineering specialty), Mystic Ward (Mage/Warlock/portals), Hall of Arms (Warrior), Hall of Explorers (Explorer's League), Hall of Mysteries (Paladin/Priest), The Forlorn Cavern (Rogue/Hunter), High Seat (King Magni atmospheric), The Great Forge (central forge atmosphere). **Deeprun Tram** to Stormwind (~3 min ride). Iconic content: **Tinkmaster Overspark** Gnomish Engineering specialty trainer, **King Magni Bronzebeard** royal atmosphere, Hall of Mysteries Paladin trainers + class quest hub.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Cross-Alliance hub**: Ironforge is **the Alliance secondary capital** — Engineering/Mining/Blacksmithing-bias. Engine should plan trip for Dwarven/Gnome alts at L10+ for class trainers + profession trainers.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Ironforge Inn is preferred for Dwarven/Gnome alts L10-30. Cross-continent travelers should bind here vs Stormwind.
3. **Tinkmaster Overspark priority**: Gnomish Engineering specialty trainer at L30+skill 200. Engine should pre-flag Engineering alts.
4. **Hall of Mysteries Paladin/Priest hub**: alt to Stormwind Cathedral Square.
5. **Forlorn Cavern Rogue/Hunter hub**: alt to Stormwind Old Town/Dwarven District.
6. **Deeprun Tram cross-continent**: 3-min ride to Stormwind. Engine should cache route.
7. **Mage portal trainer**: Mystic Ward for Teleports + Portals.
8. **Profession early grind**: The Commons sufficient for all primaries (Apprentice through Artisan). Tinkertown for Engineering specialty.
