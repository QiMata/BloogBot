# Wetlands — Alliance Mid-Bracket Zone (L20-30)

Eastern Kingdoms swampy zone north of Loch Modan — **Alliance L20-30 zone** + **major cross-continent boat hub**. Sub-zones: **Menethil Harbor** (main hub + boats to Auberdine/Theramore), **Greenwarden's Grove** (Cenarion-aligned), **Whelgar's Excavation Site** (archaeology dig), **Black Channel Marsh** (southern, **black dragonkin** lvl 22-30 elite road hazard), **Dun Modr** (northern dwarven outpost), **Sundown Marsh** (south coast). No native dungeon. Notable mobs: **Mosshide gnolls**, **Daggerspine naga**, **Whelgar's troggs**, **Black Dragon Whelps + Drakes** (north road — significant hazard for L20-25 chars), **Vile Fin Murlocs**. **Wetlands road north to Loch Modan is dangerous due to Black Dragonkin** — boat or fly route preferred.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Wetlands is the **L20-30 Alliance zone + boat hub**. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L25.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Menethil Harbor Inn is the bracket-3 default for cross-continent travelers.
3. **Black Dragonkin road hazard**: Loch Modan → Menethil Harbor land route through Black Channel Marsh is **gear-restricted at L18-22**. Engine should prefer FP or boat route.
4. **Boat hub priority**: Menethil Harbor is **the Alliance cross-continent boat hub**. Engine should plan boat-timing for cross-Kalimdor transitions.
5. **Whelgar's Excavation chain**: pickup early; cross-zone Badlands extension (Uldaman L40+ defer).
6. **Greenwarden's Grove Cenarion rep**: parallel grind for Cenarion Circle. Engine should always-pickup on first visit.
7. **Cross-zone to Arathi Highlands**: at L25-30, head east via Thandol Span. Standard route.
8. **Profession early grind**: Menethil sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman. Ironforge for advanced.
