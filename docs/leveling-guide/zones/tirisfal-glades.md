# Tirisfal Glades — Forsaken Starter Zone (L1-10)

Eastern Kingdoms wooded zone — **Undead/Forsaken only starter zone**. Sub-zones: **Deathknell** (Lvl 1-5 enclosed starter), **Brill** (Lvl 5-10 main hub), **Cold Hearth Manor** (lvl 8-12 elite area), **Garren's Haunt** (lvl 5-9 Scarlet farm), **Agamand Mills** (lvl 8-12 ghost family), **Whispering Gardens / The Whisper Hill** (Cult of the Damned). Capital adjacent: **Undercity** (south via tunnel from Brill). Notable mobs: **Mottled Wolves**, **Vile Fin Murlocs**, **Scarlet Crusade** (cross-zone), **Cult of the Damned**, **Plague Beasts**, **Agamand Ghosts**. Class identity quests: **Priest Devouring Plague** (Undead racial L10), Hunter/Warlock/Mage L10 trainers in Undercity.

## Sub-Zone Breakdown

### Deathknell (L1-5 Starter)

**Enclosed Forsaken-only starter area** — first 5 levels.

| NPC | Role |
|-----|------|
| **Shadow Priest Sarvis** | Quest hub leader |
| **Mordo Thornblade** | Various class quest origin |
| **Various race trainers** | Class-specific intro |

**Quests (Lvl 1-5):**
- **A New Plague** — kill Wretched Zombies (Forsaken's own undead) for purification
- **Recovering the Cargo** — kill Vile Fin Murlocs for crashed ship cargo
- **Mistress Bloodmark** — Deathknell mid-quest chain
- **Departure** — exit chain to Brill

**Decision-engine cue:** Deathknell is the **enclosed L1-5 Undead starter**. Engine should plan ~1-1.5 hour completion through to L5 + Brill arrival.

### Brill (L5-10 Main Hub)

**The Lvl 5-10 quest hub** — most quests originate here.

|-----|------|

| **Apothecary Johaan** | Alchemy quests + plague-themed chain |
| **Smith Selina** | Blacksmithing trainer |

## Class Quests (L1-10)

### Priest (Devouring Plague — Undead Racial L10)

| Step | Source NPC | Reward |
|------|-----------|--------|

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Starter zone gate**: Deathknell (L1-5) → Brill (L5-10) → Undercity (class quests at L10) → Silverpine Forest (L10-20 transition).
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Brill Inn (Innkeeper Renee) is the bracket-1 default. Engine should bind here after first quest from Brill.
3. **Priest Devouring Plague**: race-locked Undead. Engine should pre-flag Undead Priest alts for Brill chain.
4. **Class quest dispatch (L10)**: Hunter/Warlock/Mage/Rogue must travel to Undercity for class trainer. Engine should plan Brill→Undercity tunnel path.
5. **Silverpine transition**: at L8-10, head east via Brill road to Sepulcher. Standard route.
6. **Profession early grind**: Brill sufficient for L1-15. Apothecary Johaan is Alchemy-bias. Undercity for advanced.
7. **Agamand Mills chain priority**: party-pull for Devlin Agamand elite. Standard route at L8-12.
8. **Cold Hearth Manor party-pull**: Hag of the Boughs elite. Engine should encode group-pull invariant.
9. **Cross-tunnel route**: Brill→Undercity tunnel is the Forsaken shortcut to capital. Engine should plan early visit.
