# Darkshore — Alliance Mid-Bracket Zone (L10-20)

Kalimdor coastal zone — **Alliance L10-20 zone** designed for Night Elf levelers (cross-faction accessible via Auberdine boat from Eastern Kingdoms). Sub-zones: **Auberdine** (main hub), **Master's Glaive**, **Cliffspring Falls**, **Mist's Edge**, **Bashal'Aran** (satyr-corrupted ruins), **Ameth'Aran** (naga-overrun ruins), **Blackwood Den** (corrupted furbolgs). No native dungeon — **Blackfathom Deeps** (lvl 20-30) accessed via Ashenvale Zoram'gar Outpost south. Notable mobs: **Naga** (Ameth'Aran), **Satyr** (Bashal'Aran), **Corrupted Furbolg** (Blackwood Den), **Grell**, **Wendigo** (Cliffspring), **Twilight Cultists**. The longest L10-20 zone in the game; can take a NE Druid from L10 to L18.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Bracket entry**: Darkshore is the **L10-20 NE default zone**. Engine should plan ~10-15 hour completion through L18.
2. **Hearthstone bind**: Auberdine Inn is the bracket-2 default for NE alts.
3. **Lord Sakrasis party-pull**: rare satyr elite at Bashal'Aran. Engine should encode group-pull invariant.
4. **Tharnariun Treetender chain**: long cross-zone Cenarion-aligned chain — pickup early but defer completion to L50+ Felwood.
5. **Cross-zone to Ashenvale**: at L18-20, head south to Astranaar via Auberdine south road. Standard route.
6. **BFD dungeon prep**: at L20+, plan group queue for Blackfathom Deeps via Ashenvale Zoram Strand.
7. **Profession early grind**: Auberdine sufficient for Apprentice + Journeyman. Darnassus for advanced.
