# Cooking — 1-300 Secondary Profession, Raid Food, Nat Pagle's Books

**Secondary profession** — does NOT count toward 2-primary cap. All characters should have it. **No specialty in 1.12.** Pair-feeds character buffs/raid food (HP/mana regen + stat-buff foods). Top raid foods: **Smoked Desert Dumplings** (+20 Strength, 1h), **Dirge's Kickin' Chimaerok Chops** (+25 Stamina, 1h), **Blessed Sunfruit Juice** (+5 Spirit, 30 min), **Runn Tum Tuber Surprise** (+10 Intellect, 30 min). Recipes from trainer, vendor, drops, **Nat Pagle's books** (Dustwallow), **Cenarion Circle** (Honored+).

## Cooking Fire Mechanic

Crafting Cooking recipes requires:

| Method | Source |
|--------|--------|
| **Open campfire / heat source** | Any campfire, oven, fire pit (capital cities + many outdoor zones) |
| **Makeshift Cooking Fire** | Crafted from `1 Simple Wood + 1 Flint + 1 Tinder` (vendor purchase ~3s) — usable anywhere |
| **Burning Item Cookfire** | Trainer-taught spell: `Basic Campfire` for Cooking 1+; `Travel Form` Druid: nope, no campfire spell |

**Decision-engine rule:** carry at least 5 Simple Wood + Flint + Tinder for makeshift cooking. Engine should auto-stock during AH/vendor visits.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Cooking is always-on**: secondary profession; engine should default Cooking on every character (no opportunity cost).
2. **Recipe-source coverage**: at L40+, plan Nat Pagle visit; at L55+, grind Cenarion Circle Honored for Runn Tum Tuber.
3. **Cookfire stockpile**: maintain 5+ Simple Wood + Flint + Tinder for makeshift cooking. Engine should auto-stock.
4. **Raid food prep**: at L60, stockpile **Smoked Desert Dumplings** (+20 Str melee) + **Dirge's Kickin' Chimaerok Chops** (+25 Sta tank) + **Runn Tum Tuber Surprise** (+10 Int caster). Engine should plan 30-50 of each food per raid week.
5. **Class-role food routing**:
   - Plate-DPS (Warrior/Pal Ret): Smoked Desert Dumplings (+20 Str)
   - Tank: Dirge's Kickin' Chimaerok Chops (+25 Sta)
   - Caster (Mage/Wlk/Pri/Sham Ele): Runn Tum Tuber Surprise (+10 Int)
   - Healer (Pri/Pal Holy/Sha Resto/Dru Resto): Blessed Sunfruit Juice (+5 Spirit) + Sagefish Delight
   - Hunter/Rogue/Sham Enh (mail/leather DPS): Mightfish Steak (+10 Sta)
6. **Cooking + Fishing pair**: secondary professions; engine should grind both in parallel for self-sustaining food/water.
7. **Cooking-while-questing**: kill mob → raw meat → nearby cookfire → cook food → eat or AH-flip. Most efficient grind path.
8. **AH-flipping cooked food**: at L60, raid foods (Dirge's, Smoked Dumplings, Runn Tum) are 30-100s per food on AH. Engine should AH-flip if guild raid-supply is filled.
9. **Hunter+Cooking synergy**: Hunters generate raw meat from beast kills naturally. Engine should bias Hunter alts toward Cooking 300 by L50.
