## Identity

**Alchemy is the highest-economic-impact profession in vanilla 1.12.1.** Pairs naturally with **Herbalism** (gathering profession that supplies the herbs Alchemy consumes). At lvl 60, an Alchemy alt can earn **50-200g/week** through:

- **Flask sales** (Flask of the Mongoose, Titans, etc.) — 50-150g per flask at AH
- **Major Healing/Mana Potion crafting** for raid stockpile
- **Arcanite Transmute** (1-2 day cooldown) — 1 Arcane Crystal + 1 Thorium Bar → 1 Arcanite Bar (worth ~30-50g)
- **Essence transmutes** (Earth → Water → Air → Fire transmute chains, each on cooldown)

| Skill range | Recipe focus | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 1-75 (Apprentice) | Minor Healing → Lesser Healing | Cheap herbs (Peacebloom, Silverleaf, Earthroot) |
| 75-150 (Journeyman) | Healing Potion + Lesser Mana | Mid-tier herbs (Briarthorn, Bruiseweed, Mageroyal) |
| 150-225 (Expert) | Greater Healing + Lesser Mana + first elixirs | Mid herbs (Liferoot, Kingsblood, Goldthorn) |
| 225-300 (Artisan) | Major Healing + Major Mana + Flasks + Transmutes | High-tier herbs (Sungrass, Mountain Silversage, Dreamfoil, Black Lotus) |

## Endgame recipes (lvl 300, raid BiS)

### Flasks (3-hour persist-through-death raid buffs)

**Require Alchemy Lab** in Scholomance (the Alchemy Lab is the room with the chemical apparatus — players use the lab to craft flasks). Flask recipes drop from various endgame mobs and bosses.

| Flask | Effect | Mats |
|---|---|---|
| **Flask of the Titans** | +1200 HP, 3h | 30 Mountain Silversage + 30 Plaguebloom + 1 Black Lotus + 1 Crystal Vial |
| **Flask of Distilled Wisdom** | +65 INT, 3h | 30 Dreamfoil + 30 Golden Sansam + 1 Black Lotus + 1 Crystal Vial |
| **Flask of Supreme Power** | +150 spell damage and healing, 3h | 30 Dreamfoil + 30 Mountain Silversage + 1 Black Lotus + 1 Crystal Vial |
| **Flask of the Mongoose** | +25 AGI / +2% crit, 3h | 30 Plaguebloom + 30 Mountain Silversage + 1 Black Lotus + 1 Crystal Vial |

**Per-flask cost**: ~50-150g (driven by Black Lotus availability — rare herb spawn). At AH, flasks sell for ~80-200g each.

### Major Healing/Mana Potions (raid stockpile)

| Potion | Mats | Bulk demand |
|---|---|---|
| **Major Healing Potion** | 1 Golden Sansam + 1 Mountain Silversage + 1 Crystal Vial | Every raider needs 5-10 per raid |
| **Major Mana Potion** | 3 Dreamfoil + 1 Icecap + 1 Crystal Vial | Healers/casters need 10-20 per raid |
| **Greater Stoneshield Potion** | 1 Stonescale Eel (fish) + 1 Mountain Silversage + 1 Crystal Vial | Tanks: 3-5 per raid |
| **Limited Invulnerability Potion** | 1 Plaguebloom + 1 Mountain Silversage + 1 Crystal Vial | Hunter Demon Stalker chain + niche raid use |
| **Greater Arcane Elixir** | 1 Dreamfoil + 1 Sungrass + 1 Crystal Vial | Caster pre-raid stack |

### Transmutes (1-2 day cooldown each)

**Transmute** spells convert one type of essence/material into another. Each transmute has a **cooldown** (~24-48 hours) — engine plans daily transmute action per character.

| Transmute | Recipe | Cooldown | Profit |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Transmute: Arcanite Bar** | 1 Arcane Crystal + 1 Thorium Bar → 1 Arcanite Bar | ~48h | **High** — Arcanite Bar = ~30-50g sell, mats ~15-25g; Thunderfury legendary requires many |
| **Transmute: Earth → Water** | 1 Essence of Earth → 1 Essence of Water | ~24h | Mid (varies) |
| **Transmute: Earth → Air** | 1 Essence of Earth → 1 Essence of Air | ~24h | Mid |
| **Transmute: Earth → Fire** | 1 Essence of Earth → 1 Essence of Fire | ~24h | Mid |
| **Transmute: Air → Fire** | 1 Essence of Air → 1 Essence of Fire | ~24h | Mid |
| **Transmute: Fire → Earth** | 1 Essence of Fire → 1 Essence of Earth | ~24h | Mid |

**Engine planning**: Run **Transmute: Arcanite Bar** daily on every Alchemist alt. The 24-48h cooldown means **a single character can craft ~1 bar/day** = **~30-50g/day passive income** at AH prices.

## Specialization notes

**There are NO specializations in vanilla 1.12.1.** Master Transmute (5x more transmutes per use), Master Potion, and Master Elixir specs were added in TBC. In 1.12.1, every Alchemist has equal access to all recipes.

## VMaNGOS / private server notes

- **All standard recipes** are correctly itemized on most VMaNGOS forks.
- **Transmute cooldowns** match retail 1.12.1 (24-48h).
- **Black Lotus spawns** are rare (Burning Steppes, EPL, Silithus, Winterspring) — VMaNGOS spawn rate is faithful.
- **Alchemy Lab in Scholomance** is correctly required for flask crafting.
- **Master Alchemist (Rogvar in Swamp of Sorrows)** is correctly placed for the lvl-225 trainer step.

## Decision-Engine Rules

- **id:** `prof.alchemy.daily-transmute-arcanite` — IF `Class.Alchemy >= 275 && Items.Contains(ArcaneCrystal AND ThoriumBar) && TransmuteArcanite.NotOnCooldown` THEN cast Transmute Arcanite Bar. Priority **600** (passive income; daily routine).
- **id:** `prof.alchemy.daily-transmute-essence` — IF `Class.Alchemy >= 250 && Items.Contains(EssenceOfEarth) && EssenceTransmute.NotOnCooldown` THEN cast Earth→Water/Air/Fire (rotate by AH demand). Priority **500**.
- **id:** `prof.alchemy.flask-pre-raid` — IF `Raid.Scheduled && Class.Alchemy >= 300 && Items.Contains(BlackLotus) && InAlchemyLab` THEN craft pre-raid flask. Priority **750**.
- **id:** `prof.alchemy.major-pot-stockpile` — IF `Class.Alchemy >= 275 && Items.MajorHealingPotionCount < 30` AND in safe area THEN craft Major Healing Pot batch. Priority **600** (raid prep stockpile).
- **id:** `prof.alchemy.skill-up-grind` — IF `Class.Alchemy < 300 && Items.Contains(<next-tier herbs>)` THEN advance recipe at trainer + craft. Priority **400** (background; bundle with herbalism farm).
