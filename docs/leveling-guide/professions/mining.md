# Mining — 1-300 Gathering, Ore Tiers, Smelting, Black Iron + Elementium

Pure gathering profession. **No specialty in 1.12.** Pair-feeds **Blacksmithing** + **Engineering** (both consumers of bars). Ore tiers: Copper → Tin/Silver → Iron/Gold → Mithril/Truesilver → Thorium → **Dark Iron** (BRD Black Forge only) → **Elementium** (MC alchemy lab only). Top zones: Searing Gorge / Burning Steppes (Mithril+Thorium), Un'Goro / Winterspring / Silithus (Rich Thorium dense). Smelt Dark Iron requires the **"Spectral Chalice"** quest in BRD.

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|

| Pair-supply | **Blacksmithing** + **Engineering** (both depend on Mining bars) |
| Tier caps | Apprentice 75 → Journeyman 150 → Expert 225 → Artisan 300 |
| Specialty | **None in 1.12** |
| Top tier ore | **Thorium** (Mining 245+) — common at Searing Gorge / Un'Goro / Winterspring |
| Special smelting | **Dark Iron** (BRD Black Forge); **Elementium** (MC alchemy lab) |
| Mining tool slot | Mining Pick → Mithril Mining Pick → Master Mining Pick |
| Trainer | Apprentice/Journeyman/Expert: capital cities; Artisan: **Galvan the Ancient** (Steamwheedle Port, Tanaris) |

## Skill Progression (1-300)

| Skill | Range | Ore tier | Notes |
|-------|-------|----------|-------|
| 1-50 | Apprentice | **Copper Ore** (Mining 1) | Every starter zone (Elwynn, Dun Morogh, Teldrassil, Durotar, Tirisfal, Mulgore) |
| 50-100 | Apprentice → Journeyman (75 cap) | Copper continues; **Tin Ore** at Mining 65 | Westfall, Loch Modan, Barrens, Silverpine, Stonetalon nodes |
| 100-125 | Journeyman | Tin continues; **Silver Ore** at Mining 75 (rare in Tin nodes) | Same zones |
| 125-175 | Journeyman → Expert (Expert quest at 125) | **Iron Ore** at Mining 125 | Hillsbrad, Ashenvale, Stonetalon, Wetlands, Arathi, Hinterlands |
| 175-225 | Expert | Iron continues; **Gold Ore** at Mining 155 (rare in Iron nodes) | Same Iron zones |
| 175-230 | Expert | **Mithril Ore** at Mining 175 | STV, Tanaris, Hinterlands, Badlands, Searing Gorge, Burning Steppes |
| 230-275 | Artisan (Artisan quest at 200) | **Truesilver Ore** at Mining 230 (rare in Mithril nodes); **Small Thorium Vein** at Mining 230 | Same Mithril zones; small Thorium starts here |
| 245-300 | Artisan | **Rich Thorium Vein** at Mining 245-275 | Silithus / Un'Goro / Winterspring / Felwood (highest density); Burning Steppes / Searing Gorge moderate |
| 230-300 | Artisan | **Dark Iron Ore** (Mining 230, requires "The Spectral Chalice" quest reward) | BRD only |
| 300+ | Master | **Elementium Ore** (BWL trash drops only) | Smelt at MC alchemy lab |

**Decision-engine rule:** Mining grind aligns with character level (~Skill = Level × 5). Engine should plan farming routes through ore-rich zones based on level bracket.

## Smelting Menu (Bar Crafting)

Smelting converts ore to bars at any forge (capital cities, Searing Gorge, etc.). Special smelting requires unique forges.

| Bar | Smelting skill | Recipe input | Notes |
|-----|---------------|---------------|-------|
| **Copper Bar** | Smelting 1 | 1 Copper Ore | Apprentice baseline |
| **Bronze Bar** | Smelting 65 | 1 Tin Bar + 1 Copper Bar | Mid-Apprentice |
| **Silver Bar** | Smelting 75 | 1 Silver Ore | Rare drop from Tin nodes |
| **Iron Bar** | Smelting 100 | 1 Iron Ore | Apprentice→Journeyman bridge |
| **Gold Bar** | Smelting 155 | 1 Gold Ore | Rare drop from Iron nodes |
| **Steel Bar** | Smelting 165 | 1 Iron Bar + 1 Coal (Coal from quartermaster vendors or mob drops) | Used in BS recipes |
| **Mithril Bar** | Smelting 175 | 1 Mithril Ore | Standard mid-tier |
| **Truesilver Bar** | Smelting 230 | 1 Truesilver Ore | Rare drop from Mithril nodes |
| **Thorium Bar** | Smelting 245 | 1 Thorium Ore | Late-tier baseline |
| **Dark Iron Bar** | Smelting 230 | 8 Dark Iron Ore + Black Forge | **BRD Black Forge only** + "Spectral Chalice" quest reward |
| **Enchanted Thorium Bar** | Smelting 290 | 2 Thorium Bar + 1 Lung Juice Cocktail (Engineering item) | Engineering BS-tier reagent |
| **Elementium Bar** | Smelting 300 | 10 Elementium Ore + Fiery Core (1) + Pyrium Bar | **MC alchemy lab only** |
| **Smelt Steel Bar** | 165 | Iron Bar + Coal | Common BS recipe input |

**Decision-engine rule:** Smelting menu is hidden under the Mining profession panel. Engine should batch-smelt bars whenever inventory is at risk of overflowing during dungeon runs.

## Dark Iron Smelting — "The Spectral Chalice"

Dark Iron Ore drops from Dark Iron Dwarves throughout BRD. Smelting Dark Iron requires:

| Step | Action | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Dark Iron Ore drops from BRD Dark Iron Dwarves | Open-world farming or AH |
| 2 | Reach BRD interior; find **the Pocket Watch / Spectral Chalice quest object** in the Domicile of Dwarven Magic | One-time chain |
| 3 | Complete "**The Spectral Chalice**" gambling event (mining trainer quest reward — drops 8 Dark Iron Bar to start) | One-time |
| 4 | Use Smelt Dark Iron at BRD's **Black Forge** (next to Lord Incendius) | Smelt 8 Ore → 1 Dark Iron Bar (8:1 ratio) |

**Decision-engine rule:** Spectral Chalice is **the gateway** for Dark Iron Smelting. Bots should always pickup the chain on first BRD visit. Sulfuron Hammer recipe uses Dark Iron Bars — engine should accumulate 50+ Dark Iron Bars for Sulfuron Hammer crafting.

## Elementium Smelting (MC-Only)

Elementium Bar is the highest-tier reagent in 1.12. Smelting requires:

| Step | Action | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Acquire **Elementium Ore** (BWL trash drops only) | BWL raid trash |
| 2 | Acquire **Fiery Core** (MC trash drop) and **Pyrium Bar** (smelting input or AH purchase) | MC raid |
| 3 | Travel to **MC alchemy laboratory** (between Domo and Ragnaros areas) | One-time per craft |
| 4 | Use Smelt Elementium → 1 Bar per recipe; high gold cost via Pyrium Bar reagent | — |

**Decision-engine rule:** Elementium is multi-month accumulated reagent for endgame BS recipes (Stormherald, Mind-Blade, etc.). Engine should track BWL trash drops as Elementium-source farming alongside boss progression.

## Mining + Engineering / Mining + Blacksmithing Pair Strategy

For pair-locked profession choice:

| Character class | Recommended pair |
|-----------------|------------------|
| Warrior / Paladin (plate-DPS) | Mining + **Blacksmithing** (Lionheart Helm, Imperial Plate set) |
| Hunter | Mining + **Engineering** (Master Engineer's Goggles, ammo, Hunter weapons) |
| Shaman / Hunter / Rogue (mail/leather) | Mining + **Engineering** (Mind Control Cap PvP, etc.) |
| Caster (Mage / Priest / Warlock) | Mining + Blacksmithing (mid-game Truesilver Champion) OR Tailoring solo (no pair) |

**Decision-engine rule:** Mining solo (without BS or Eng pair) is **only** worth it if character intends to AH-flip ores/bars rather than craft. Engine should prefer pair-locked combos for L60 endgame characters.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Profession pair-lock**: Mining feeds Blacksmithing + Engineering. Engine should default Mining as one of the 2 primaries on Warrior/Paladin/Hunter/Shaman/Rogue characters.
2. **Ore-tier-to-zone routing**: engine should plan gathering loops by zone:
   - Searing Gorge: Iron + Mithril + Thorium (full mid-to-late game grind)
   - Un'Goro / Winterspring / Silithus: Rich Thorium dense
   - Stranglethorn / Tanaris: Mithril dense (mid-game)
3. **Master Mining Pick equip**: at Skill 250+, engine should always-equip Master Mining Pick for +20 effective skill. Allows ore mining at lower tier.
4. **Smelting batch**: smelt ores immediately during downtime (between dungeon pulls, FP routes). Engine should never let ores accumulate beyond 60+ stacks in inventory.
5. **Spectral Chalice priority**: complete "The Spectral Chalice" quest in BRD on first visit. Required for Dark Iron Smelting.
6. **Sulfuron Hammer reagent**: 50+ Dark Iron Bars accumulated for BS partner. Engine should farm Dark Iron Ore in BRD trash runs.
7. **Elementium Bar accumulation**: long-term reagent for BWL/MC crafted recipes. Engine should track BWL trash drops as source.
8. **Thorium Brotherhood rep parallel**: Searing Gorge mining quests give Thorium rep. Engine should route through Searing Gorge for Mining + Rep simultaneously.
9. **Coal supply for Steel Bar**: Coal drops from BRD Dark Iron mobs + vendor at Thorium Point. Engine should stockpile Coal for Steel Bar smelting.
10. **AH-flipping vs crafting**: at L60, decision tree:
   - If `BS.Skill >= 250 || Eng.Skill >= 250`: prioritize crafting recipes
   - Else: AH-flip raw ore for gold
