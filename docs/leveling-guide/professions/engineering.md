# Engineering — 1-300 Grind, Goblin vs Gnomish Split, Raid Utility

The "explosive utility" profession. Pair-locks with **Mining**. **Specialty splits earliest** of any profession at **level 30 + skill 200** — Goblin (damage-focused) vs Gnomish (utility-focused). Specialty choice is **permanent in 1.12** (un-learn + re-grind to swap). Top utility items: **Goblin Sapper Charge** (AoE damage), **Gnomish Battle Chicken** (interrupt pet), **Gnomish Mind Control Cap** (PvP MC), **Goblin Jumper Cables XL** (raid combat-resurrect), **Field Repair Bot 74A** (raid repair). Engineering is **the** PvP-utility profession — most rank-14 grinders run Gnomish for Mind Control Cap.

| Skill | Range | Recipes / mats | Trainer |
|-------|-------|---------------|---------|
| 1-50 | Apprentice | Rough Blasting Powder + Rough Stone (Mining 1) → Copper Modulator → Crude Scope | Capital city Engineer trainer |

| 100-150 | Journeyman | Iron Strut + Bronze Tube + Heavy Blasting Powder; **Iron Grenade**; Mechanical Squirrel | Capital trainer |
| 150-200 | Journeyman → Expert (Expert quest at 125) | Mithril Tube + Mithril Casing; **Mithril Mechanical Squirrel** (lvl 25 vanity pet); Mithril Mechanical Dragonling; **Goblin Land Mine** | Capital trainer; **Specialty trainer at 200** |
| 200-225 | Expert | Mithril-tier specialty items (Goblin or Gnomish path-divergent) | Specialty trainer |
| 225-260 | Expert → Artisan (Artisan quest at 200) | Thorium Tube + Thorium Widget; **Thorium Grenade**; **Goblin Jumper Cables XL** | Specialty trainer (Goblin: Nixx Sprocketspring, Gadgetzan; Gnomish: Tinkmaster Overspark, Ironforge) |

**Decision-engine rule:** Engineering's earliest specialty split (lvl 30 + skill 200) means engine should plan path-divergence very early. Most levelers grind to skill 195 before lvl 30 ding to be ready for specialty pickup at lvl 30.

## Specialty Decision: Goblin vs Gnomish

### Goblin path (lvl 30 + skill 200)

**Best for:** PvP burst damage, raid utility (Jumper Cables raid resurrect), Sapper Charge AoE damage on adds, Goblin Rocket Boots speed-burst, group/raid-buff niche.

**Key Goblin-only recipes:**

| Recipe | Skill | Effect / use |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| **Goblin Sapper Charge** | 205 | AoE 1100-1700 damage in 5-yard radius; 1-min CD; consumed; raid-trash AoE clear |
| **Goblin Mortar** | 250 | Ranged AoE 600-1000 damage on target; 5-min CD |
| **Goblin Rocket Boots** | 250 | Boots; +5 movement speed boost (3-sec sprint); 5-min CD |
| **Goblin Bomb Dispenser** | 245 | Trinket; 4 throwable bombs (300-400 damage); 30-min CD |
| **Goblin Jumper Cables XL** | 285 | **Raid combat-resurrect** (50% chance to succeed at 50% target HP); BoP; 1-hour CD |
| **Goblin Mind Control Cap** | 280 | Helm; 25% chance to MC enemy for 20s on cast; 30-min CD `[verify pass 3]` (note: this is actually Gnomish, but listed in some Goblin variants) |
| **Goblin Land Mine** | 200 | 200-300 AoE damage on detonation; 1-min CD; consumed |
| **Goblin Construction Helmet** | 250 | Cosmetic helm with utility |
| **Goblin Battle Spear** | 250 | 2H weapon (rare, sometimes craftable) |

### Gnomish path (lvl 30 + skill 200)

**Best for:** PvP control, Vanish-like utility, MC Cap PvP rank-grinding, raid interrupt, repair-bot deployment.

**Key Gnomish-only recipes:**

| Recipe | Skill | Effect / use |
|--------|-------|--------------|
| **Gnomish Battle Chicken** | 205 | Trinket; summons mechanical chicken pet that interrupts spells (5-min duration, 30-min CD); raid CC |
| **Gnomish Cloaking Device** | 255 | Trinket; **Vanish for 5s + 50% movement speed for 5s** (PvP escape); 30-min CD; works in PvP/PvE |
| **Gnomish Mind Control Cap** | 270 | **Helm; 20% chance to Mind Control enemy for 20s** on use (PvP rank-grinding tool); 30-min CD; cloth helm |
| **Gnomish Death Ray** | 280 | Trinket; **300-400 damage instant on target** (medium AoE in 5-yard); 30-min CD |
| **Gnomish Rocket Boots** | 250 | Boots; ranged 3-sec sprint with 50% chance to fall over (lol-tier 1.12 mechanic); 5-min CD |
| **Gnomish Net-o-Matic** | 245 | Ranged trinket; immobilize 10s on target; 30-min CD |
| **Gnomish Universal Remote** | 245 | Trinket; control mechanical NPC for 20s; PvE niche |
| **Field Repair Bot 74A** | 285 | **Raid-anywhere repair bot**; summon NPC for 5 mins; raids cannot otherwise repair in instances; 1-hour CD |
| **Tranquil Mechanical Yeti** | 280 | Vanity pet (universal Goblin/Gnomish — both can craft) |

**Decision-engine rule:** Gnomish is the **PvP-utility** path; Goblin is the **raid-damage** path. Engine should pre-flag specialty based on `Snapshot.Profile.PvPGrinder == true` (Gnomish) vs `Snapshot.Profile.RaidDPS == true` (Goblin).

## PvP Rank-14 Grinding Synergy

Engineering is **the** PvP profession in 1.12. Gnomish path specifically:

| Item | PvP use |
|------|---------|
| **Gnomish Mind Control Cap** | Force-MC enemy player for 20s — uniquely powerful in BG |
| **Gnomish Cloaking Device** | Vanish + 50% sprint — best PvP escape tool, works on all classes |
| **Gnomish Net-o-Matic** | Immobilize enemy 10s — PvP root |
| **Gnomish Battle Chicken** | Interrupt-spam pet — caster-counter |
| **Gnomish Death Ray** | 300-400 AoE damage on target — burst damage |

**Decision-engine rule:** if `Snapshot.PvP.Rank.Target >= 12 (Lieutenant Commander)`, engine should pre-flag Gnomish Engineering on the PvP grinder character. Mind Control Cap alone is rank-14-tier utility.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Pair-lock with Mining**: `Profession.Mining.Skill >= Profession.Engineering.Skill - 50`. Engine should pause Engineering if Mining is too far behind.
2. **Specialty selection at lvl 30 + skill 200**: prompt user-config (`Snapshot.Profession.PreferredSpecialty`):
   - PvP rank-grinder → Gnomish (Mind Control Cap)
   - Raid DPS / Hunter → Goblin (Sapper Charge + Jumper Cables XL)
   - Raid healer / utility → Gnomish (Field Repair Bot 74A)
3. **Specialty change protocol**: requires un-learning + re-leveling 1-300; engine should refuse-by-default; require explicit user-confirm flag.
4. **Hunter+Engineering**: optimal pairing for ammo crafting. Engine should bias Hunter alts to Engineering + Mining.
5. **Master Engineer's Goggles BiS**: at L55+, engine should auto-craft class-specific Goggles helm.
6. **Goblin Jumper Cables XL** (raid combat-resurrect): the highest-impact raid-utility item in 1.12. Engine should flag for any L60 raider.
7. **Gnomish Mind Control Cap** PvP gate: at rank 12+, engine should ensure Cap availability for BG runs.
8. **Sapper Charge raid-trash**: AoE 1100-1700 damage on big mobs. Engine should auto-use during BRD/Strat/Scholo trash phases for AoE clear.
9. **Reagent stockpile (Heart of Fire, Black Diamond, Powerful Mojo)**: engine should accumulate during quest/dungeon runs; required for Master-tier recipes.
10. **Steamwheedle Cartel rep grind**: Engineering's primary rep faction; Honored is reachable from quest hubs (Booty Bay/Gadgetzan/Ratchet/Everlook); engine should round-trip ALL 4 ports for rep efficiency.
11. **Arcanite Dragonling at Revered**: raid-tier mechanical pet for trinket slot; ~5-10g per Arcanite Bar reagent.
12. **Thorium Grenade auto-bomb**: 1-min CD reagent-grade explosive; engine should default to Thorium Grenade as offhand-throwable for stuns/interrupts in PvP and trash AoE.
