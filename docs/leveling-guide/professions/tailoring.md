# Tailoring — 1-300 Grind, Mooncloth & Robe of the Archmage, Bag Crafting

The cloth-crafting profession. **No pair-lock** — cloth drops from any humanoid mob in the world. **No specialty in 1.12** (Mooncloth/Shadowweave/Spellfire spec system added in TBC). Pairs naturally with **Enchanting** for self-sustaining gear (disenchant tailored greens for materials). Top recipes: **Robe of the Archmage** (Mage class epic chest, BiS), **Truefaith Vestments** (Priest healer chest), **Bloodvine set** (ZG-themed PvP, +1 Spell Crit set), **Mooncloth Bag** (16-slot, Tailoring-only profession bag), **Felcloth Bag** (Warlock 30-slot soul shard bag).

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|

| Tier caps | Apprentice 75 → Journeyman 150 → Expert 225 → Artisan 300 |
| Specialty | **None in 1.12** (added in TBC: Mooncloth/Shadowweave/Spellfire) |
| Top BiS class items | **Robe of the Archmage** (Mage chest, BiS pre-raid); **Truefaith Vestments** (Priest healer chest, BiS pre-raid) |

| Bag specialty | **Mooncloth Bag** (16-slot, +2 vs base bag), **Felcloth Bag** (30-slot soul shard bag, Warlock specialty) |
| Class synergy | Mage/Priest/Warlock primary; Druid/Shaman/Rogue tertiary |

## Skill Progression (1-300)

| Skill | Range | Recipes / mats | Trainer |
|-------|-------|---------------|---------|
| 1-50 | Apprentice | Linen Bandage + Linen Boots + Linen Cloak; Bolt of Linen Cloth (2 Linen → 1 Bolt) | Capital city Tailor trainer |
| 50-100 | Apprentice → Journeyman (75 cap) | Wool Bandage + Wool items; Bolt of Woolen Cloth (3 Wool → 1 Bolt) | Same trainer + Journeyman quest at 50 |
| 100-150 | Journeyman | Silk Cloth tier; Bolt of Silk (4 Silk → 1 Bolt); **Cured Light Hide** (LW alternative) | Same |
| 150-225 | Journeyman → Expert (Expert quest at 125) | Mageweave Cloth tier; Bolt of Mageweave (4 Mageweave → 1 Bolt); **Mageweave Bandage** (First Aid synergy) | Same |
| 225-260 | Expert → Artisan (Artisan quest at 225) | Runecloth tier; Bolt of Runecloth (4 Runecloth → 1 Bolt); **Robe of Power** | Capital trainer + **Daryl Stack** (Stormwind A) / **Bombus** (Undercity H) for Artisan |
| 260-300 | Artisan | Felcloth → Mooncloth crafting (24h CD at Moonwell); **Truefaith Vestments**; **Robe of the Archmage**; **Bloodvine set** | Various Artisan trainers + reputation recipes + raid drops |

**Decision-engine rule:** Tailoring grind is **the cheapest profession to level** because cloth drops from world humanoids. Engine should always have Tailoring as one of the 2 primaries on a Mage/Priest/Warlock/Druid character (cloth/leather caster).

## Mooncloth Crafting (24h CD at Moonwell)

Mooncloth is the top-tier cloth reagent in 1.12. Crafted at:

| Step | Action | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Acquire 2 Felcloth (Felwood satyrs, Azshara demons, Jaedenar Shadow Council) | Open-world farming or AH |
| 2 | Travel to a **Moonwell** (Felwood Emerald Sanctuary, Moonglade, Darnassus, Hyjal Auberdine) | One-time travel |
| 3 | Use Tailoring crafting menu → Create Mooncloth (24h CD) | 1 Mooncloth per cast |
| 4 | Mooncloth used in: Mooncloth Bag, Truefaith Vestments, Robe of the Archmage, Cenarion gear | Stockpile for class epics |

**Decision-engine rule:** Mooncloth 24h CD is **the bottleneck** for top-tier Tailoring. Engine should mark "Mooncloth crafted" daily on calendar; multi-character coordination — multiple alts can each craft 1 Mooncloth/day.

## First Aid Synergy

**First Aid bandages are crafted FROM Tailoring-tier cloth** — every cloth tier produces a corresponding bandage:

| Cloth | Bandage tier | First Aid skill |
|-------|--------------|-----------------|
| Linen | Linen Bandage | 1 |
| Wool | Wool Bandage | 75 |
| Silk | Silk Bandage | 125 |
| Mageweave | Mageweave Bandage | 175 |
| Runecloth | Heavy Runecloth Bandage | 260 |

**Decision-engine rule:** Tailoring + First Aid combined produce bandage stockpile. Engine should reserve ~15-25% of cloth pile for First Aid bandage crafting; remaining for Tailoring leveling.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **No pair-lock**: Tailoring is fully self-sustaining via humanoid kills. Engine should default to Mage/Priest/Warlock/Druid characters as Tailoring primary.
2. **Cloth-tier matching**: cloth gathering routes should align with character level. Linen at L1-15; Wool at L16-30; Silk at L31-40; Mageweave at L41-50; Runecloth at L51-60.
3. **Mooncloth daily**: 24h CD at Moonwell location. Engine should track daily cooldown and queue crafting at Moonwell visits.
4. **Robe of the Archmage planning**: requires 8 Mooncloth (8 days) + Cured Rugged Hide (LW partner) + Pristine Hide (Druid quest or ST drop). Plan over 10-14 days.
5. **Truefaith Vestments planning**: similar to Robe but Priest-only equip.
6. **Bloodvine set planning**: requires ZG raid weekly clears for Bloodvine herb drops; ~5-10 weeks of clears for full 3-piece set.
7. **Mooncloth Bag baseline**: at L55+ with Tailoring 280+, engine should auto-craft 16-slot bags for inventory expansion.
8. **Felcloth Bag for Warlocks**: 30-slot soul shard bag is Warlock-locked equip. Engine should pre-flag for Warlock alts.
9. **First Aid synergy**: 15-25% cloth reservation for bandage crafting. Engine should batch-craft bandages during Tailoring leveling rather than skip them.
10. **Argent Dawn Argent Boots/Robe**: AD Honored recipe path; gates with Naxx attune chain.
11. **Cenarion Vestments at Revered**: top rep-locked Tailoring item; cross-profession with LW.
12. **Specialty for TBC**: in 1.12, no Mooncloth/Shadowweave/Spellfire spec exists. Engine should NOT prompt for spec selection (TBC-only feature).
