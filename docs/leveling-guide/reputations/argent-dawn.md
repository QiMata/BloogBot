## Identity

The **Argent Dawn** is the **most-impactful endgame faction** in vanilla 1.12.1 and the reputation engine should prioritize on at least one main raider per account. AD provides:

1. **Naxxramas attunement gate** — Honored required (Revered/Exalted reduce attune cost — see [naxxramas.md](../attunements/naxxramas.md))
2. **AD Insignia trinkets** at Honored tier — best-in-slot or near-BiS for several class roles pre-raid
3. **AD Tabard** — purchasable at Friendly, gives passive +5% rep gain in Plaguelands (Classic 2019 added a stronger version)
4. **Mograine's Might / similar EPL/WPL quest gear** — pre-raid BiS pieces from rep-gated quest hubs
5. **Stat-stick rings + cloaks** at Revered and Exalted tiers

| Tier | CP needed (cumulative) | Key reward unlock |
|---|---|---|
| Neutral | 0-2999 | Starting tier; faction quartermaster sells nothing |
| Friendly | 3000-8999 | AD tabard (+5% AD rep in Plaguelands), mid-tier crafted recipes |
| **Honored** | 9000-20999 | **Naxx attunement minimum** ; **AD insignia trinkets** + rings + cloak; PvE-relevant gear |
| Revered | 21000-41999 | Naxx attune cost reduced to 30g + 2 Arcane Crystals + 1 Nexus Crystal; better AD rings + epic accessories |
| Exalted | 42000+ | Naxx attune is **free** (no gold/mat cost); Argent Avenger trinket; full set bonuses |

Total reputation needed for **Exalted from Neutral**: **42,000 reputation**. With efficient farming, this is **20-40 hours /played** at lvl 60.

## Key NPCs and locations

- **Light's Hope Chapel** (Eastern Plaguelands, ~80,60) — neutral AD hub; quartermaster + main turn-in NPCs (Quartermaster Miranda Breechlock, Argent Officer Garush + Pruitt + Cleese for Scourgestone turn-ins)
- **Chillwind Camp** (Western Plaguelands, ~43,82) — Alliance hub for some AD-related quests; also Skeleton Key chain start
- **Plaguelands questgivers** scattered throughout EPL/WPL: Tirion Fordring (EPL — major chain), Crusader Lord Valdelmar, Argent Officers in WPL

## Reputation gain methods

### Method 1 — Scourgestone turn-ins (primary endgame grind)

Three Scourgestone tiers from undead mobs:

| Stone | Source | Turn-in ratio | Rep per stack |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Minion's Scourgestone** | EPL/WPL undead lvl 50+ | **20 in → 25 rep** | 1.25 rep per stone |
| **Invader's Scourgestone** | EPL/WPL undead lvl 53+ | **10 in → 25 rep** | 2.5 rep per stone |
| **Corruptor's Scourgestone** | Stratholme + Scholomance bosses | **1 in → 25 rep** | 25 rep per stone (rare drop) |

**Bonus**: Each Scourgestone turn-in **also gives 1 Argent Dawn Valor Token** (BoP, grants 25 rep on use; can be saved across alts via mail or used solo for additional rep).

**Rate analysis**: Invader's Scourgestone farming is the most efficient stack — Scarlet Crusade undead in Tyr's Hand (EPL) drop 2-3 stones per kill, allowing ~80-150 stones/hour = 200-375 rep/hour. Slow but reliable.

**Engine optimization**: Combine Scourgestone farming with Bone Fragment grinding (next method) since the same Tyr's Hand mobs drop both.

### Method 2 — Material turn-in quests (Friendly+ unlock)

Once **Friendly** with AD, **5 repeatable quests** at Light's Hope Chapel each grant **1000 rep per turn-in**:

| Material | 30× cost | Source |
|---|---|---|
| **30 Bone Fragments** | Cheap (drop from EPL/WPL undead) | Plaguelands skeletons + Stratholme + Scholomance |
| **30 Core of Elements** | Mid-cost (Burning Steppes/Searing Gorge mid-elite mobs) | |
| **30 Dark Iron Scraps** | Mid-cost (BRD/BRM Dark Iron mobs) | BRD farming or AH purchase |
| **30 Crypt Fiend Parts** | Higher cost (Stratholme UD spider mobs) | |
| **30 Savage Frond** | Easier (Felwood/Un'Goro plant mobs) | |

**Each is repeatable** — but the rep ratio decreases at higher tiers (1000 rep at Friendly drops to ~250 rep at Revered+ for the same 30-pile turn-in).

**Engine planning**: Stack one full set of all 5 (150 mats total) before going to Light's Hope Chapel; turn-in burst-style for 5000 rep in one trip. Do this 3-5 times to push from Friendly → Revered.

### Method 3 — One-time quest chains in EPL/WPL

| Chain | Reputation reward | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Tirion Fordring's chain** (EPL, starts at his cabin) | ~2000-3000 rep across multi-step | Includes elite-quality fights at end |
| **Argent Dawn Commission** chain | Trinket reward + ~500-1000 rep | Repeatable in a limited form |
| **Crusader Lord Valdelmar / Argent Officer chain** | EPL Plaguelands hub quests | Mostly ~250-500 rep each |
| **Various Light's Hope Chapel introductory quests** | Several at ~250-500 rep each | First-time bonuses |

Total one-time quest rep available in EPL/WPL: **~5000-8000 rep** without repeatables — gets a fresh-60 from Neutral to Honored just by completing the EPL/WPL quest hub.

### Method 4 — Stratholme + Scholomance dungeon kills (mob + boss)

Mob and boss kills inside Strat/Scholo grant AD rep:
- **Trash mobs**: ~5-15 rep per kill until Honored, drops off after
- **Boss kills**: ~75-150 rep per boss, contribute up to Exalted
- **Corruptor's Scourgestone drops** from named bosses

Estimate: A Stratholme UD clear (5 bosses + ~50 trash) grants ~750-1000 rep + 4-6 Corruptor's Scourgestones (= 100-150 additional rep).

**Engine optimization**: Combine Strat/Scholo runs with **Skeleton Key acquisition** ([attunements/5-man-keys.md](../attunements/5-man-keys.md)), **T0 set farming**, **Mor'Ladim trinket farming**, and **Headmaster's Charge staff farming** (Scholo) — single dungeon run progresses 4-5 character objectives simultaneously.

## Tabard mechanics (1.12 version)

The **Argent Dawn Tabard** (purchase at Friendly+ from quartermaster Miranda Breechlock for ~50s) provides:
- Visual: Argent Dawn crusader emblem
- **+5% rep gain on AD-related kills/turn-ins in Plaguelands** (1.12.1)
- **Worn-tabard reputation gain** for kills against undead in Plaguelands

**Classic 2019 delta**: Changed to "wearing tabard during dungeon clear gives bonus rep" — this is **NOT the 1.12.1 mechanic**. Engine should not assume tabard-active dungeon rep buffs in vanilla.

## Engine planning rule — multi-character AD strategy

Naxx attune at Honored costs ~135-210g per character (60g + mats). For an account with 2-3 raid alts:
- **Push 1 main to Revered or Exalted first** → reduces attune cost on subsequent alts
- **Stockpile Argent Dawn Valor Tokens** (BoP, can't transfer) — each toon's tokens stay with that toon
- **Material gathering (Bone Fragments, Core of Elements, etc.) is tradeable** — main can mail mats to alt for rep grind
- **Scourgestones are tradeable** — main can farm + mail to alt for rep boost

**Cross-account amortization**: Engine plans the main raider's AD push to Exalted to unlock Argent Avenger + free Naxx attune; subsequent alts grind only to Honored using mailed mats.

## Time investment table

Starting from Neutral at lvl 60:

| Goal | /played time |
|---|---|
| Neutral → Friendly | 2-4 hours (one-time EPL/WPL quest hub completion) |
| Friendly → Honored | 4-8 hours (5×1000-rep material turn-ins + Strat/Scholo runs) |
| Honored → Revered | 8-15 hours (Scourgestone farming + repeat quest turn-ins) |
| Revered → Exalted | 15-25 hours (heavy Scourgestone grinding; rep gain rate drops at Revered) |
| **Total Neutral → Exalted** | **30-50 hours** /played |

**Engine optimization**: Bundle AD grind with all other lvl-60 endgame objectives (T0 farming, Skeleton Key chain, Naxx attune mat gathering, Headmaster's Charge runs).

## VMaNGOS / private server notes

- **Light's Hope Chapel quartermaster + Argent Officer turn-in NPCs** are scripted reliably.
- **Argent Dawn Valor Token** mechanic works.
- **Tabard +5% rep buff** is correctly Plaguelands-only on most VMaNGOS forks (1.12.1-faithful).
- **AD insignia trinkets** are correctly itemized at Honored tier.

## Decision-Engine Rules

- **id:** `rep.argent-dawn.start-quest-hub` — IF `Level==60 && Reputation[ArgentDawn]<Friendly && Account.RaiderPlanIncludesNaxx` THEN run EPL/WPL quest hub at Light's Hope Chapel. Priority **600** (~5000-8000 rep one-time bump).
- **id:** `rep.argent-dawn.tabard` — IF `Reputation[ArgentDawn]>=Friendly && !Items.Contains(ArgentDawnTabard)` AND in Light's Hope Chapel THEN buy tabard from Miranda Breechlock. Priority **500** (low cost; passive +5% Plaguelands rep boost).
- **id:** `rep.argent-dawn.scourgestone-stack` — IF `Reputation[ArgentDawn]<Honored && Player.InEPLOrWPL` THEN passive-farm Scourgestones during questing/grinding. Priority **400** (background).
- **id:** `rep.argent-dawn.material-turnin` — IF `Reputation[ArgentDawn]>=Friendly && Items.Contains(30+Bone Fragments OR 30+Core of Elements OR 30+Dark Iron Scraps OR 30+Crypt Fiend Parts OR 30+Savage Frond) && in Light's Hope Chapel` THEN turn in for 1000 rep. Priority **600** (efficient burst rep).
- **id:** `rep.argent-dawn.honored-target-for-naxx` — IF `Account.RaiderPlanIncludesNaxx && Reputation[ArgentDawn]<Honored && Level==60` THEN prioritize AD push to Honored over secondary endgame goals. Priority **750** (gates Naxx attunement).
- **id:** `rep.argent-dawn.revered-target-for-naxx-savings` — IF `Account.RaiderPlanIncludesNaxx && Reputation[ArgentDawn]<Revered && Account.HasMultipleRaidAlts` THEN push lead char to Revered for cheaper Naxx attune cost on alts. Priority **decision-request** (account-level cost-benefit).
- **id:** `rep.argent-dawn.bundle-with-strat-scholo` — IF `Reputation[ArgentDawn]<Exalted && Strat OR Scholo run scheduled` THEN passively claim AD rep from boss/trash kills. Priority **300** (always-on background).
- **id:** `rep.argent-dawn.exalted-trinket-target` — IF `Reputation[ArgentDawn]>=Revered && PvERoleTrinketSlot.NotBiS` THEN push to Exalted for Argent Avenger / Argent Defender. Priority **400** (gear improvement; less critical than Naxx access).
- **id:** `rep.argent-dawn.tirion-chain` — IF `Level==60 && !QuestsCompleted.Contains(TirionFordringChain)` THEN run the chain (~2000-3000 rep + lore + flavor pieces). Priority **500**.
