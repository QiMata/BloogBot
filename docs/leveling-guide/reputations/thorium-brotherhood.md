# Thorium Brotherhood — Sulfuron Hammer Plans, Dark Iron Crafting, Searing Gorge + BRD + MC

Neutral faction representing the Dark Iron dwarf splinter group at Thorium Point, Searing Gorge. **Lokhtos Darkbargainer is the BRD-locked quartermaster** (deep inside dungeon near Bael'Gar). **Honored unlocks the Sulfuron Hammer plans** — the BS recipe to craft the base item for **Sulfuras, Hand of Ragnaros** legendary mace. Revered unlocks Mithril armor recipes (Ornate Mithril, Wildthorn Mail). Exalted unlocks BiS rep-only items including Bloodsoul Embrace + Stronghold Gauntlets variants. Rep zones: **Searing Gorge** (Thorium Point quests + outdoor mob kills) + **BRD** (boss kills + reagent turn-ins) + **MC raid** (Core Leather + Lava Core turn-ins post-clear).

## Sulfuron Hammer (Honored Recipe)

Sulfuron Hammer is a Blacksmithing 1H mace recipe; **the base item for Sulfuras, Hand of Ragnaros** (legendary mace).

| Step | Action | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Reach Thorium Brotherhood **Honored** | Searing Gorge + BRD farming (~10-25 hours) |
| 2 | Buy **Plans: Sulfuron Hammer** from Lokhtos Darkbargainer (10g, Honored discount) | BRD-locked quartermaster |
| 3 | Craft **Sulfuron Hammer**: 8 Sulfuron Ingot + 20 Arcanite Bar + 50 Dark Iron Bar + 20 Blood of the Mountain | Most expensive recipe in 1.12 |
| 4 | Acquire **Eye of Sulfuras** (~1% drop from Ragnaros, MC) | MC raid weekly |
| 5 | Combine Sulfuron Hammer + Eye of Sulfuras → **Sulfuras, Hand of Ragnaros** (legendary 2H mace) | Final combination |

**Decision-engine rule:** Sulfuras chain spans Thorium Brotherhood Honored + multiple MC raid weeks. Engine should plan 4-8 month chain for melee alts.

See [../professions/blacksmithing.md](../professions/blacksmithing.md#sulfuron-hammer-pre-raid-legendary-step) for crafting detail.

## Reputation Grinding Sources

### Searing Gorge (Quests + Outdoor Farming)

Thorium Point is the entry hub. Quests:

| Quest source | Rep per quest | Notes |
|--------------|---------------|-------|
| **Master Smith Burninate** quests (fiery flux chain) | ~75-150 rep | Mining-themed |
| **Hansel Heavyhands** quests (hostile fauna) | ~50-100 rep | Beast-kill quests |
| **Velarok Windblade** quests (Dark Iron sabotage) | ~75-150 rep | Espionage chain |
| **Dark Iron Residue** turn-in | ~5 rep per residue | Drops from Dark Iron mobs at Cauldron + BRD |

### BRD (Boss Kills + Outdoor Farming)

BRD provides high-rep turn-ins:

| Source | Rep gain |
|--------|----------|
| **BRD boss kill** | ~100-300 rep per kill |
| **Plagued Lung** turn-in (rare drop) | ~250 rep |
| **Black Iron Ore** turn-in | ~10 rep per ore |
| **Dark Iron Residue** stockpile (high volume) | ~5 rep per |

### MC Raid Reagent Turn-Ins (Post-Clear)

After MC raid clears, turn in reagents to Lokhtos:

| Reagent | Rep gain |
|---------|----------|
| **Core Leather** (Skinning material from MC bosses) | ~25 rep per leather |
| **Lava Core** (drops from MC bosses) | ~250 rep per turn-in |
| **Fiery Core** (drops from MC bosses) | ~250 rep per turn-in |
| **Sulfuron Ingot** (rare MC trash drop) | ~250 rep per turn-in (also Sulfuron Hammer reagent) |

**Decision-engine rule:** MC raid clears = ~750-1500 Thorium rep per week from reagent turn-ins. Engine should always-loot MC reagents and turn in to Lokhtos.

## Lokhtos Darkbargainer (BRD Quartermaster)

**Lokhtos Darkbargainer** is Thorium Brotherhood's quartermaster, located in BRD (Domicile of Dark Iron Magic, near Bael'Gar). To reach him:

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Enter BRD via Blackrock Mountain shared entrance |
| 2 | Navigate through Detention Block (left wing) |
| 3 | Pass through Domicile of Dark Iron Magic |
| 4 | Lokhtos Darkbargainer is on a balcony in Bael'Gar's chamber area |

**Decision-engine rule:** Lokhtos visit requires deep BRD penetration (~30-45 min from BRD entrance). Engine should batch reagent turn-ins per BRD trip — collect 5-10+ items before traveling.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Friendly priority**: Searing Gorge quest dump on first entry. Engine should pickup all 6+ Thorium Point quests.
2. **Sulfuron Hammer chain priority**: at Honored, immediate purchase Plans: Sulfuron Hammer for BS alt. Engine should track per-character reagent count.
3. **BRD batching**: Lokhtos requires deep BRD penetration. Engine should batch 5-10+ reagent items per BRD trip.
4. **MC weekly turn-in**: post-clear, deliver Core Leather + Lava Core + Fiery Core to Lokhtos for sustained rep gain.
5. **Bloodsoul Embrace planning**: Exalted goal for Plate-tank Warriors. Multi-month grind.
6. **Dark Iron Residue stockpile**: 5 rep per residue; engine should always-loot from Dark Iron mobs (Searing Gorge + BRD).
7. **Sulfuron Ingot priority**: 250 rep + Sulfuras chain reagent. Engine should always-loot MC trash drops.
8. **Cross-Mining sync**: Mining + Smelt Dark Iron unlock cross-quest. Engine should pickup Spectral Chalice in BRD.
9. **Time-to-Honored estimate**: ~15-20 hours active. Engine should plan 2-3 weekly BRD trips for stockpile + Lokhtos delivery.
