# Timbermaw Hold — Furbolg Faction, Bracer Mana Regen Enchant, Felwood↔Winterspring Tunnel

Neutral faction representing the Timbermaw Furbolg tribe. Cross-faction (both factions grind same way). **Starts at Hostile** (Timbermaw guards attack on sight at first); clear the early hostile-attack threshold via Deadwood Furbolg kills. **Honored unlocks the BiS mana regen bracer enchant** (Enchant Bracer - Mana Regeneration). Revered unlocks crafted Cenarion Reagent components. Exalted unlocks BiS rep-only items including bracers, boots, and crafted gear. Rep zones: **Felwood** (Deadwood Furbolgs hostile, Timbermaw quests at the tunnel entrance) + **Winterspring** (Winterfall Furbolgs hostile, alternative quests). The **Timbermaw Hold tunnel** is the **Felwood ↔ Winterspring shortcut** — accessible at Friendly+.

## Initial Hostile Standing — Clearing the Threshold

At first encounter, **Timbermaw Furbolg guards attack on sight**. The standing must be moved past Hostile via:

| Step | Action | Effect |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Kill ~25 Deadwood Furbolgs (Felwood Deadwood Village) | Each kill = ~5-10 rep with Timbermaw |
| 2 | Reach Hated → Unfriendly transition | Furbolgs start to ignore you |
| 3 | Pickup the first quest at Timbermaw Hold tunnel entrance NPC | One-time chain |
| 4 | Complete the first quest (turn-in Deadwood Headdresses) | Reach Neutral standing |
| 5 | Continue rep grind to Friendly | Tunnel passage unlocked |

**Decision-engine rule:** initial hostility-clearing requires ~30-45 minutes of active Deadwood Furbolg farming. Engine should always pre-grind to Unfriendly before tunnel approach.

## Reputation Tier Rewards

### Friendly (Tunnel Pass + Basic Quests)

- **Tunnel passable**: Timbermaw Hold cave allows Felwood ↔ Winterspring rapid travel (~3-4 minute shortcut vs. 10+ minute outdoor route)
- Basic quest hub access at Timbermaw Hold; ~10 quests available
- Vendor access (Meilosh sells low-tier items)

### Honored (1 BoP Recipe per Profession)

| Profession | Honored recipe |
|------------|----------------|
| **Enchanting** | **Enchant Bracer - Mana Regeneration** (+4 mp/5 — **top healer/caster bracer enchant pre-raid**) |
| **Tailoring** | **Mooncloth Bag** (16-slot, BoP) — alternate to Tailoring 280 trainer-taught variant |
| **Leatherworking** | **Cenarion Reagent component** (Tribal LW); alternate Wolfshead Helm crafting path |
| **Alchemy** | **Major Mana Potion** (already trainer-taught, but Timbermaw Honored gives discounted recipe) |
| **Cooking** | **Cenarion Carrot Cake** (rare food, mid-tier) |
| **Engineering** | **Tranquil Mechanical Yeti** alternate source (cross-Cenarion Circle) |

### Revered (Mid-Tier Crafted)

| Profession | Revered recipe |
|------------|----------------|
| **Tailoring** | **Mooncloth Robe pattern** (alt source); Cenarion Reagent crafted components |
| **Leatherworking** | Cenarion-rep component for Cenarion Vestments (Tailor + LW partner) |
| **Enchanting** | **Enchant Boots - Greater Stamina** (+7 Stamina, alt-source) `[verify pass 3]` |

### Exalted (BiS Rep-Locked)

| Profession | Exalted recipe |
|------------|----------------|
| **Tailoring** | **Mooncloth Bracers** (BiS pre-raid healer/caster bracer slot — rep-locked) |
| **Leatherworking** | **Bramblewood Bracers** (BiS pre-raid Druid Resto bracer slot — rep-locked) |
| **Enchanting** | Cenarion-flavor enchant patterns (cross-rep, may overlap with Cenarion Circle) |

**Decision-engine rule:** Mooncloth Bracers (Tailoring) + Bramblewood Bracers (LW) at Exalted are **bracket-defining** for healer/caster slot. Engine should plan multi-month grind for these.

## Reputation Grinding Sources

### Felwood — Deadwood Furbolgs (Primary)

Deadwood Village in Felwood has the highest density of Furbolg mobs:

| Source | Rep gain | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Deadwood Furbolg kill** | +5 to +10 rep per kill | Open-world, lvl 50-55 elites |
| **Deadwood Ritual Mask** turn-in | +150 rep per mask | Mask drops from Deadwood Furbolgs (~25-50% per kill); turn in at Timbermaw Hold tunnel NPC |
| **Deadwood Headdress** turn-in | +150 rep per turn-in (alt name) | Same as Ritual Mask |
| **Felwood corruption quests** | ~50-100 rep per quest (cross-Cenarion) | Cenarion Hold quests give Cenarion + Timbermaw rep |

### Winterspring — Winterfall Furbolgs (Secondary)

Winterspring has the second hostile Furbolg tribe:

| Source | Rep gain | Notes |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Winterfall Furbolg kill** | +5 to +10 rep per kill | Open-world, lvl 53-58 |
| **Winterfall E'ko / Spirit Beads** turn-in | +150-250 rep per turn-in | Drops from Winterfall Furbolgs |
| **Winterspring quests** (Wintersaber Trainers / general questing) | ~50-100 rep per quest | Cross-Wintersaber rep simultaneously |

### Timbermaw Hold Tunnel Quest Hub (Tertiary)

| Source | Rep gain |
|--------|----------|
| **Timbermaw Quartermaster Meilosh quests** | ~50-200 rep per quest (one-time) |
| **Felpaw Village quests** (advanced) | Mid-tier rep gain |
| **Northern Felwood quests** (Cenarion-aligned with Timbermaw rep) | ~50 rep per |

**Decision-engine rule:** Timbermaw rep grind is **Felwood-primary** (Deadwood Ritual Masks 150 rep each = best rate). Engine should plan 2-4 hour blocks of Deadwood farming + tunnel-trip turn-ins.

## Standing Threshold Map

| Tier | Rep needed (cumulative) | Time estimate |
|------|--------------------------|---------------|
| Hostile → Hated | 0 → 36000 (default starting "buffer") `[verify pass 3]` | ~30-45 min Deadwood farming |
| Hated → Unfriendly | 36000 → 39000 | Quick after first quest turn-in |
| Unfriendly → Neutral | 39000 → 42000 | Initial quest chain completion |
| Neutral → Friendly | 42000 → 48000 | ~5-10 hours Deadwood + Winterfall turn-ins |
| Friendly → Honored | 48000 → 60000 | ~10-25 hours sustained farming |
| Honored → Revered | 60000 → 78000 | ~25-40 hours |
| Revered → Exalted | 78000 → 102000 | ~30-60 hours |

**Note:** The starting "Hostile" tier requires ~36000 rep to clear (since faction starts at Hated by default in 1.12.1). This is approximate — exact threshold may vary.

**Decision-engine rule:** Mooncloth Bracers is a long-term Exalted goal. Engine should plan ~30-60 hour grind for healer/caster alts.

## Tunnel Passage (Felwood ↔ Winterspring)

The Timbermaw Hold tunnel is the **fastest cross-zone shortcut** between Felwood and Winterspring.

### Pre-Friendly (Hostile/Unfriendly)

- Furbolg guards attack on sight
- Tunnel is dangerous — not recommended for travel

### Friendly+

- Furbolg guards ignore neutral/friendly characters
- Tunnel becomes safe rapid-travel route (~3-4 min cross-zone vs. 10+ min outdoor)

**Decision-engine rule:** at Friendly+, engine should always-prefer Timbermaw tunnel for Felwood ↔ Winterspring cross-zone routes.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Hostile-clearance priority**: at first encounter, plan ~30-45 minute Deadwood Furbolg pre-grind before tunnel approach.
2. **Deadwood Ritual Mask farming**: ~150 rep per mask + ~25-50% drop rate = ~2-4 hour per Honored push.
3. **Tunnel passage**: at Friendly+, engine should always-route through Timbermaw tunnel for Felwood ↔ Winterspring.
4. **Mana Regen bracer enchant priority**: Honored unlock; engine should plan for healer/caster alts.
5. **Mooncloth Bracers (Exalted)**: long-term BiS Tailoring bracer goal. Engine should plan 30-60 hour multi-week grind.
6. **Cross-Cenarion rep parallel**: Felwood Cenarion quests give both Timbermaw + Cenarion rep simultaneously. Engine should batch.
7. **Cross-Wintersaber rep parallel**: Winterspring Wintersaber Trainers quests overlap with Timbermaw farming routes. Engine should batch (NE Alliance only for Wintersaber mount).
8. **Turn-in batching**: collect 5+ Deadwood Ritual Masks at a time before traveling to Timbermaw tunnel for turn-in. Engine should batch trips.
9. **Reagent farming**: Timbermaw is a stockpile zone for Mooncloth-tier reagents (Felcloth, Mooncloth). Engine should round-trip with reagent gathering.
