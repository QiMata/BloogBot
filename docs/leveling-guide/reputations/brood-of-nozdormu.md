# Brood of Nozdormu — AQ40-Locked Faction, Atiesh Chain Step, Class Rings

Bronze dragonflight faction, **exclusively grindable via AQ40 boss kills** (no outdoor questing path — must be in raid). Hub: **Anachronos** at Caverns of Time entrance, eastern Tanaris (next to Silithus border). **Neutral required** for Atiesh chain step 3 (Frame of Atiesh turn-in to Anachronos). **Friendly→Exalted** unlocks class-specific rings + Mark of the Champion trinkets. Top reward: **Band of Earthen Wrath / Band of Servitude / Don Julio's Band** at Exalted (BiS rep-locked rings). Time-to-Exalted is **multi-month** — 60-100+ hours of AQ40 weekly clears.

| Time to Honored | ~5-15 hours (multiple AQ40 clears) |
| Time to Exalted | ~60-100+ hours (multi-month multi-clear) |

- Cost: ~10g per Vintage from Anachronos at Exalted
- Use case: pre-raid stat buff stack (alongside flasks)

## Reputation Grinding Sources

### AQ40 (Primary — only major source)

AQ40 boss kills give Brood Nozdormu rep:

| Boss | Approximate rep gain |
|------|----------------------|
| **Skeram** | ~250 rep |
| **Bug Trio** (Yauj/Vem/Kri) | ~250 rep (combined) |
| **Sartura** | ~250 rep |
| **Fankriss the Unyielding** | ~250 rep |
| **Viscidus** (optional) | ~250 rep |
| **Princess Huhuran** | ~250 rep |
| **Twin Emperors** | ~500 rep (combined) |
| **Ouro** (optional) | ~250 rep |
| **C'Thun** | ~750 rep |
| **Full clear total** | ~3000-5000 rep per week |

### AQ20 (Tertiary — minor contribution)

AQ20 boss kills give ~50-150 rep per kill, ~500-1000 per full clear. Negligible vs AQ40 but every bit helps.

### Twilight Trappings (Brood-rep accelerator)

**Twilight Trappings** drop from AQ40 trash (specifically Twilight Cultist mobs within the raid):

| Reagent | Rep gain at Anachronos |
|---------|------------------------|
| **Twilight Trappings** (cloth/leather/mail/plate) | ~50-100 rep per piece |
| **Cultist Insignia** | Higher rate per turn-in (+200 rep) |

**Decision-engine rule:** Twilight Trappings inside AQ40 = **rep accelerator**. Engine should always-loot trash drops within AQ40 + turn in to Anachronos post-clear.

## Anachronos Atiesh Chain (Step 3)

For Druid/Mage/Priest/Warlock pursuing **Atiesh, Greatstaff of the Guardian** legendary:

| Step | Action | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Reach **Brood of Nozdormu Neutral** (Anachronos requires Neutral standing for Atiesh quest acceptance) | Achieved at first Atiesh quest pickup |
| 2 | Have **Frame of Atiesh** (40 Splinters from Naxxramas) | See [../raids/naxxramas.md](../raids/naxxramas.md#atiesh-greatstaff-of-the-guardian-legendary) |
| 3 | Bring Frame to Anachronos | Cross-zone travel to Caverns of Time |
| 4 | Anachronos sends to Naxxramas → Kel'Thuzad → **Staff Head of Atiesh** | Naxxramas final boss kill |
| 5 | C'Thun in AQ40 → **Base of Atiesh** (drops while quest active) | AQ40 final |
| 6 | Stratholme Festival Lane → kill demon Atiesh → **Atiesh, Greatstaff of the Guardian** | Final purification |

**Decision-engine rule:** Atiesh chain step 3 requires Brood Nozdormu Neutral. Engine should track per-character chain step + Anachronos position.

## Standing Threshold Map

| Tier | Rep needed (cumulative) | AQ40 clears estimated |
|------|--------------------------|------------------------|
| Neutral → Friendly | 0 → 3000 | ~1-2 full AQ40 clears |
| Friendly → Honored | 3000 → 9000 | ~3-5 full AQ40 clears |
| Honored → Revered | 9000 → 21000 | ~6-10 full AQ40 clears |
| Revered → Exalted | 21000 → 42000 | ~10-15 full AQ40 clears |

**Note:** With AQ40 weekly lockout, **time-to-Exalted is 10-15+ weeks** of consecutive raid attendance.

**Decision-engine rule:** Brood Nozdormu Exalted is **the slowest rep grind** in vanilla 1.12 because:
- AQ40-locked source (no outdoor accelerator)
- 7-day raid lockout
- ~3000-5000 rep per clear

Engine should plan 3-4 month timeline for raid teams pursuing Exalted.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Source-locked**: Brood Nozdormu rep is **AQ40-only**. Engine should not attempt outdoor grinding.
2. **Atiesh chain step 3 priority**: Druid/Mage/Priest/Warlock pursuing Atiesh require Neutral. Engine should track per-character chain.
3. **Twilight Trappings always-loot**: AQ40 trash drops; ~50-100 rep per piece. Engine should always-loot.
4. **Class ring tier-tracking**: per-class ring acquisition by tier (Friendly/Honored/Revered/Exalted). Engine should plan AQ40 schedule.
5. **Mark of the Champion priority**: Plate-tank ring at Honored. Engine should ensure tank alts grind to Honored first.
6. **Brood Vintage consumable at Exalted**: pre-raid stat buff (+5 all stats). Engine should stock for raid teams.
7. **Multi-month timeline**: Exalted requires 10-15 weekly AQ40 clears. Engine should not block on Exalted until late raid weeks.
8. **Cross-Cenarion sync**: AQ40 clears advance both Brood Nozdormu + Cenarion Circle. Engine should batch.
