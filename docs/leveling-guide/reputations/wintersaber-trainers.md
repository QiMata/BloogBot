# Wintersaber Trainers — Alliance Night Elf Only Mount Grind, Frostsaber Rock

**Alliance Night Elf race-locked faction**. The only Alliance race-locked mount in vanilla 1.12. Hub: **Frostsaber Rock** (NW Winterspring), with **Donova Snowden** at Everlook as the chain initiator. Reward: **Reins of the Winterspring Frostsaber** (BoP, NE-only, white tiger mount) at Exalted. Rep grind is **single-source dedicated farming** — Frostsaber Cub kills + token turn-ins to **Rivern Frostwind**. **Time-to-Exalted is ~30-60 hours**, one of the longest grinds in vanilla 1.12. Pairs poorly with raid prep (no overlap); recommend running on a dedicated NE alt.

## Reputation Grinding Sources

The grind is **strictly single-source dedicated farming** in Winterspring. There is no rep accelerator from quests or other zones.

### Step 1: Donova Snowden Chain (Initial Unlock)

| Step | Action | Source |
|------|--------|--------|
| 1 | Speak to **Donova Snowden** at Everlook (Winterspring goblin port) | One-time chain |
| 2 | Receive **"The Wintersaber Trainers"** quest | Quest acceptance |
| 3 | Travel to **Frostsaber Rock** (NW Winterspring, ~5-10 min ride from Everlook) | One-time travel |
| 4 | Meet **Rivern Frostwind** (faction quartermaster) | First contact |
| 5 | Receive first repeatable Frostsaber-related quests | Daily/repeatable turn-ins |

### Step 2: Rivern Frostwind Repeatable Turn-Ins

Frostsaber Rock has **multiple repeatable quests** that give Wintersaber rep:

| Quest type | Action | Rep gain |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| **Frostsaber Pelt** turn-in | Kill Frostsaber Cubs + Adolescents (lvl 53-58); collect 5 Frostsaber Pelts | ~250 rep per 5-pelt turn-in |
| **Frostsaber Hunting** | Kill specific Winterspring frostsabers (named pulls) | ~25-100 rep per kill |
| **Frostsaber Egg / Cub** chains | Multi-step; collect Frostsaber Cub items + bring to Rivern | ~100-250 rep per chain |
| **Cat Meat** turn-in | Cooking-tier reagent from Cubs; Rivern accepts | ~50 rep per stack |

**Decision-engine rule:** **Frostsaber Pelt** is the highest-rep-per-time turn-in. Engine should plan 4-6 hour farming blocks at Frostsaber Pride / Den of Frostsabers (Winterspring NW area).

### Step 3: Frostsaber Pride Den (Primary Farm Zone)

The **Frostsaber Pride** is a clustered den of Frostsabers in NW Winterspring near Frostsaber Rock:

| Mob density | Per-pelt drop rate |
|-------------|---------------------|
| ~30-50 Frostsaber Cubs + Adolescents per zone respawn cycle | ~50-75% pelt drop rate |
| Respawn timer | ~5-10 minutes per pull cluster |

**Farming pace estimate:**
- 5 Frostsaber Pelts ≈ 30-45 minutes of solo farming (clear 6-10 mobs)
- Each pelt-set turn-in = ~250 rep
- Honored to Revered: ~30-60 turn-ins (~30-50 hours)
- Revered to Exalted: ~60-100+ turn-ins (~30-50 hours)

**Decision-engine rule:** **Frostsaber farming is mathematically slow** — the rep grind is the longest single-source-locked rep in 1.12.1. Engine should reserve 4-6 hour weekly windows on dedicated NE alts.

## Cross-Reputation Synergy (None)

Wintersaber Trainers is **single-source-locked**. No synergy with:
- Cenarion Circle (Silithus is far away)
- Timbermaw Hold (Winterspring overlap, but Timbermaw quests don't give Wintersaber rep)
- Argent Dawn (Plaguelands far away)
- Brood of Nozdormu (AQ40-locked)
- Other vanilla rep grinds

**Decision-engine rule:** plan Wintersaber as **dedicated grind** — no parallel rep chains apply.

## Optimal Grinding Strategy (Decision-Engine)

### Class-Specific Farming Tips

| Class | Strategy |
|-------|----------|
| **Druid** (NE) | Travel Form between Frostsaber Pride pulls; Mangle/Maul for fast clears; bonus skinning if LW alt |
| **Hunter** (NE) | Pet tank Frostsaber Cubs; Concussive Shot kite escape; Aspect of the Cheetah for inter-cluster movement |
| **Priest** (NE) | Slow grind without DPS spec; Mind-Controlled Bear Spirit (cross-Druid) for support |
| **Rogue** (NE) | Stealth + Vanish for solo escape; Eviscerate burst on cubs |
| **Warrior** (NE) | Slowest grind; tank-cleave clusters; recover via bandage between pulls |

### Daily Cadence (Dedicated Grind)

- **Day 1**: Initial Donova Snowden chain (1-2 hours) — reach Friendly
- **Days 2-7**: Daily 1-2 hour Frostsaber Pelt farming + turn-ins → Honored (~5-10 hours)
- **Weeks 2-4**: Daily 1-2 hour grind sessions → Revered (~10-20 hours)
- **Weeks 5-8**: Sustained grind to Exalted (~20-30 hours)

**Decision-engine rule:** Wintersaber daily cadence works best as **1-2 hour evening farming** alongside other character activities.

## Decision-Engine Rules

1. **Race-locked**: only Alliance Night Elf can begin the chain. Engine should fail-fast for non-NE characters.
2. **Class-priority**: Druid (Travel Form between pulls) is fastest farmer; Rogue (Stealth) for safety; Hunter (pet) for sustained pulls.
3. **Donova Snowden initiator**: at Everlook (Winterspring); always-pickup on first Winterspring visit.
4. **Frostsaber Pelt prioritization**: 250 rep per 5-pelt turn-in; engine should target Frostsaber Pride / Den area.
5. **Single-source-locked**: no parallel rep accelerators. Engine should plan dedicated grind sessions.
6. **Time-to-Exalted estimate**: 30-60 hours active. Engine should set realistic timeline.
7. **Mount value vs time**: cosmetic-only reward (white tiger). Engine should de-prioritize for raid-focused players.
8. **Pelt drop tracking**: ~50-75% drop rate; engine should track per-session pelt count + turn-in cadence.
9. **Multi-character coordination**: only NE alts can grind. Engine should coordinate which alt is the dedicated farmer.
