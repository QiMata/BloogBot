# Heal Task — Heal-Target Selection → Heal-Cascade by HP-Deficit + MP Cost → Predictive / Pushback-Aware Cast → Threat-Throttle on Healers → HoT Cycling → Group-Heal Triggers → Cleanse / Dispel / Combat-Rez → Emergency-Cooldown Chain

Group-aware healing sub-cycle. Where [melee-rotation.md](./melee-rotation.md) drives the swing/cooldown ladder, [cast-spell.md](./cast-spell.md) drives any single cast, [pull-target.md](./pull-target.md) drives pre-engage, and [threat-management.md](./threat-management.md) governs who the mob chases, **heal-task** is the cross-bot policy that decides *who* the healer casts on, *which* heal flavour (Flash / Greater / HoT / Group), *when* it lands (pre-emptively vs reactively), and how to spend mana across a fight without pulling aggro. This doc picks up exactly where [threat-management.md R6](./threat-management.md) defers: the healer-side wait-for-threat gate, the heal-threat formula, and per-spec heal-cascade priorities — and where [threat-management.md R12](./threat-management.md) leaves off: the snapshot-field contract for healer state (`heal.target.health_pct`, `heal.tank.threat_value`, `heal.player.mana_pct`).

The shipped runtime split is: the shared **heal-target selection** + **single-heal dispatch** lives on `CombatRotationTask` ([`Exports/BotRunner/Tasks/CombatRotationTask.cs:627-652`](../../../Exports/BotRunner/Tasks/CombatRotationTask.cs) `GetHealTarget` + [`:686-706`](../../../Exports/BotRunner/Tasks/CombatRotationTask.cs) `TryCastHeal` — temporarily targets the heal target, casts, then re-targets the enemy via `AssignGroupTarget`). The shared **wait-for-threat** gate at [`:660-676`](../../../Exports/BotRunner/Tasks/CombatRotationTask.cs) is currently used by DPS specs but is the canonical surface healer-side R5 should consume. **Per-spec heal-cascade priority** lives in each healer profile: PaladinHoly at [`BotProfiles/PaladinHoly/Tasks/PvERotationTask.cs`](../../../BotProfiles/PaladinHoly/Tasks/PvERotationTask.cs) (single `HolyLight` cascade — emergency self-heal `< 40%` HP at line 13-18, group heal at 60% threshold line 42, otherwise DPS prologue Devotion + Seal + Judgement), PriestHoly at [`BotProfiles/PriestHoly/Tasks/PvERotationTask.cs`](../../../BotProfiles/PriestHoly/Tasks/PvERotationTask.cs) (PWS + InnerFire prologue, Renew HoT @ 80%, Heal @ 60% — line 27-43; HolyFire + Smite DPS fillers), DruidRestoration at [`BotProfiles/DruidRestoration/Tasks/PvERotationTask.cs`](../../../BotProfiles/DruidRestoration/Tasks/PvERotationTask.cs) (Rejuvenation HoT @ 80%, HealingTouch @ 55% — line 47-55; Moonfire + Wrath DPS fillers), ShamanRestoration at [`BotProfiles/ShamanRestoration/Tasks/PvERotationTask.cs`](../../../BotProfiles/ShamanRestoration/Tasks/PvERotationTask.cs) (HealingWave @ 60% line 38; ManaSpringTotem maintenance + GroundingTotem reactive interrupt; LightningBolt + FlameShock DPS fillers). All four derive from `CombatRotationTask` (interface `IBotTask`) and share the `TryCastHeal` dispatch path.

The server-side heal-threat formula is `gain × classThreatModifier × spell_threat.multiplier`, split across **every mob currently in combat with the heal target** via `HostileRefManager::threatAssist`. Class threat modifier is **`0.25f` for Paladin** (per-cite below) and **`0.5f` for all others** (Priest / Druid / Shaman). The single ingress is at [`D:/MaNGOS/source/src/game/Spells/Spell.cpp:1587-1593`](D:/MaNGOS/source/src/game/Spells/Spell.cpp):

```cpp
int32 gain = pCaster->DealHeal(unitTarget, addhealth, m_spellInfo, crit);

if (pRealUnitCaster)
{
    float classThreatModifier = pRealUnitCaster->GetClass() == CLASS_PALADIN ? 0.25f : 0.5f;
    unitTarget->GetHostileRefManager().threatAssist(pRealUnitCaster, float(gain) * classThreatModifier * sSpellMgr.GetSpellThreatMultiplier(m_spellInfo), m_spellInfo);
}
```

`threatAssist` at [`D:/MaNGOS/source/src/game/Threat/HostileRefManager.cpp:62-76`](D:/MaNGOS/source/src/game/Threat/HostileRefManager.cpp) divides the resulting threat by the heal target's hostile-ref count (`size = pSingleTarget ? 1 : getSize()`) and adds `threat / size` to each mob's `ThreatManager`. The `SPELL_ATTR_EX4_NO_HELPFUL_THREAT` attribute (line 64) opts a heal out entirely (e.g., self-cast HoTs on out-of-combat tanks). This means **a single Greater Heal landing on a tank engaged with 4 mobs splits 0.5 × heal across all 4 mobs equally** — and the client-side `ThreatTracker.RecordHealing` at [`Exports/BotRunner/Combat/ThreatTracker.cs:30-35`](../../../Exports/BotRunner/Combat/ThreatTracker.cs) collapses this to one number per healer without the per-mob split (the R17 per-mob accounting gap from [threat-management.md](./threat-management.md)).

The live test anchor is `BotRunner.Tests.LiveValidation.CombatLoopTests` at [`Tests/BotRunner.Tests/LiveValidation/CombatLoopTests.cs:49`](../../../Tests/BotRunner.Tests/LiveValidation/CombatLoopTests.cs) — solo combat scenario that does NOT exercise heal-task. Planned anchors `HealTaskTests::Heal_PaladinSelfHealsHolyLightToFullHp`, `HealTaskTests::Heal_PriestHealsPartyTankLowestHpFirst`, `HealTaskTests::Heal_DruidRejuvenationCyclesAcrossMultipleTargets`, `HealTaskTests::Heal_ShamanWaitsForTankThreatBeforeFirstHeal` are `not-started` per [Plan/Activities/combat.md §HealTask](../../Plan/Activities/combat.md).

## Overview

Healing in 1.12.1 is **purely cast-driven** — no auto-heal, no proc-heal channels, and no shipped server-side "lowest-HP-first" oracle. Every heal is one explicit `CMSG_CAST_SPELL` with the heal-target's GUID in the `SpellCastTargets` payload (`TARGET_FLAG_UNIT` per [cast-spell.md](./cast-spell.md) — same protocol as a damage spell; the spell's `SPELL_EFFECT_HEAL` (id 10) effect resolves on the server via `Spell::EffectHeal` at [`D:/MaNGOS/source/src/game/Spells/SpellEffects.cpp:2480`](D:/MaNGOS/source/src/game/Spells/SpellEffects.cpp), then `Unit::DealHeal` clamps `gain = min(addhealth, maxHealth - currentHealth)` and the threat-assist branch at `Spell.cpp:1587-1593` fires. Per-spell threat is loaded from the `spell_threat` DB row at [`D:/MaNGOS/source/src/game/Spells/SpellMgr.cpp:839`](D:/MaNGOS/source/src/game/Spells/SpellMgr.cpp) and queried via `SpellMgr::GetSpellThreatMultiplier` at the cast site.

`GetHealTarget(healthThreshold = 70)` at [`CombatRotationTask.cs:627-652`](../../../Exports/BotRunner/Tasks/CombatRotationTask.cs) is the canonical selection helper. Its policy:

1. **Self-emergency short-circuit** — if `Player.HealthPercent < healthThreshold - 20` (default `< 50%` HP), return `Player` immediately. The healer must not be dead.
2. **Iterate `PartyMembers`**, pick the lowest-HP-%-alive party member below `healthThreshold`. Ties resolve by iteration order (no marked-target preference today; future SCo.4 enhancement).
3. **Self-fallback** — if `Player.HealthPercent < lowestPartyHp`, prefer self over party (covers the case where the healer is below the chosen party member's HP%).
4. **Return null** when no party member or self is below the threshold — caller falls through to DPS rotation.

`TryCastHeal(spellName, healthThreshold = 70, maxRange = 40f)` at [`:686-706`](../../../Exports/BotRunner/Tasks/CombatRotationTask.cs) is the dispatch helper: (a) call `GetHealTarget`; null → return false; (b) if self-heal, route through `TryCastSpell(spellName, 0f, float.MaxValue, true, castOnSelf: true)` (single-cast cast-on-self path; `CMSG_CAST_SPELL` with `TARGET_FLAG_SELF = 0x0000`); (c) party heal: save `prevTarget = GetTarget(Player)`, `SetTarget(healTarget.Guid)`, dispatch `TryCastSpell(spellName, 0f, maxRange)`, then restore the enemy target (`SetTarget(prevTarget.Guid)` if alive, else `AssignGroupTarget`). The target switch sends `CMSG_SET_SELECTION` ([`Exports/WoWSharpClient/SpellcastingManager.cs:307-328`](../../../Exports/WoWSharpClient/SpellcastingManager.cs)) before the `CMSG_CAST_SPELL`.

All four share the same skeleton via `TryCastHeal`: (1) **target selection** via `GetHealTarget(threshold)`; (2) **temporary target switch** to heal target (`SetTarget`); (3) **dispatch** via `TryCastSpell(spellName, 0f, maxRange)` which routes through [cast-spell.md](./cast-spell.md) R1-R6 gates (resource / cooldown / range / cast-time / movement); (4) **enemy re-target** to the prior victim or `AssignGroupTarget`. The cast-time gates from [cast-spell.md](./cast-spell.md) R6 apply: **the healer must `StopAllMovement()` before a cast-time heal** or the server cancels at `>0.5y` displacement. Today PriestHoly and DruidRestoration's `PerformCombatRotation` calls `StopAllMovement()` explicitly at body entry; PaladinHoly's `ExecuteRotation` does NOT (open SCo.1).

**Heal-threat aggregation**. The client-side `ThreatTracker.RecordHealing(healerGuid, healAmount)` aggregates `healAmount × 0.5f` to the healer's running total across ALL mobs (no per-mob split). For Paladins specifically, the server-side multiplier is `0.25f` not `0.5f` — the client estimator over-reports Paladin heal threat by 2× ([`ThreatTracker.cs:16`](../../../Exports/BotRunner/Combat/ThreatTracker.cs) constant is fixed to `0.5f`; class-aware variant is open SCo.3). Net effect: a Paladin can safely heal earlier and harder than the client estimator suggests, but the rotation does not exploit this today.

**Group-heal triggers** (Chain Heal / Circle of Healing / Wild Growth / Prayer of Healing). All four ship in 1.12.1 EXCEPT Wild Growth (Wrath+) and Circle of Healing (TBC+): **only Chain Heal (Shaman) and Prayer of Healing (Priest) exist in 1.12.1**. Neither is in any shipped rotation today — group-heal-on-2+-low-HP is an open SCo.1 surface. The trigger predicate is `PartyMembers.Count(m => m.HealthPercent < <threshold>) >= 2` — see R7 below.

**Cleanse / Dispel / Decurse**. Paladin `Cleanse` (Poison + Disease + Magic), Priest `DispelMagic` (Magic only, friendly+self), Priest `AbolishDisease` / `CureDisease`, Druid `RemoveCurse` (Curse only), Shaman `CurePoison` / `CureDisease` (Tremor Totem for Fear is passive). The `Spellbook.cs` ships `DispelMagic` (line 166), `AbolishDisease` (line 153), `CureDisease` (line 154), `RemoveCurse` (line 32). **`Cleanse`, `CurePoison`, `PurifyDisease`, `AbolishPoison`-as-dispel are NOT defined as constants today** (open Spellbook gap). No shipped rotation dispatches a dispel — see R9 + Failure modes.

**Combat-rez**. Only **Druid Rebirth** (10 min CD, target-selectable, in-combat) and **Shaman Reincarnation** (60 min CD, self-only, requires Ankh reagent — NOT really combat-rez of others) qualify. Paladin Redemption + Priest Resurrection require out-of-combat. The combat-rez constant is **not in `Spellbook.cs` today**; not dispatched by any rotation. See R10.
