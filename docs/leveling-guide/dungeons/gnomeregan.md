# Gnomeregan (Gnomer) — 5-Man Dungeon Guide

Sixth file in the dungeons/ family, authored to the `ragefire-chasm.md` template contract. Gnomeregan is the **Alliance-natural L29-38 dungeon** and the dungeon-side climax of the fallen-gnome storyline that begins with the gnomes' exodus from their irradiated capital. **Two entrances** in the **Dun Morogh (zone 1)** mountains: the **main entrance** opens off the rim around (-5163.5, 925.4) on the surface and the **Workshop back entrance** (item-key-gated) on the south side. Inside is a multi-tier industrial dungeon: clockwerk halls → workshop → engineering labs → trogg-infested lower halls → **Mekgineer Thermaplugg's bomb arena**. **5 named bosses** (Grubbis + Viscous Fallout + Electrocutioner 6000 + Crowd Pummeler 9-60 + Mekgineer Thermaplugg) **+ Dark Iron Ambassador (6228) rare-spawn boss and Chomper (6215) escort-event add**. **Level band 29-38 optimal**, ~1-2h full clear depending on Workshop/Clockwerk path. Gnomeregan has a **dedicated C++ script** at `D:/MaNGOS/source/src/scripts/eastern_kingdoms/dun_morogh/gnomeregan/` (`gnomeregan.cpp` + `gnomeregan.h` + `boss_thermaplugg.cpp` — script-name `instance_gnomeregan` + `npc_blastmaster_emi_shortfuse` + `npc_kernobee` + `boss_thermaplugg`), making it the **highest scripting-risk class** sub-L40 dungeon in vanilla — three separate C++ scripts driving the **Blastmaster Shortfuse 41-phase escort + Grubbis ambush** (cave-in doors, 33 mob-summon waypoints, 4 explosive charges), the **Kernobee Alarm-A-Bomb follower-AI quest event** (post-death detonation), and the **Thermaplugg 6-face bomb-dispenser arena** (gnome-face GOs 142208-142213 + button GOs 142214-142219 + walking bomb 7915 spawn-and-fall pathing).

## Geography & Sub-Zones

Gnomeregan is **branching** with two main loops — the **Clockwerk Run** (linear corridor from main entrance to engineering core) and the **Workshop / Engineering Quadrant** (parallel branch accessible via back entrance OR via Clockwerk-side cutthrough). The only sub-zone in `world_full_14_june_2021.sql` is **Gnomeregan (721)** itself; the floor-by-floor named sub-rooms below come from AreaTable.dbc client data and wiki and are flagged **TBD**.

1. **Main Entrance Hall** [TBD AreaTable name — wiki calls it "The Entrance"] — first room past WSL `324`. Trash: **Leper Gnome (1211, lvl 8-10)** + **Irradiated Pillager (6329, lvl 25-26)** packs. **Petrified Trogg trash** (entries not extracted) in the entrance ramps. Friendly NPCs: **Pristine Tinker (rare)**, **Kernobee (7850)** is the escort-event NPC for quest 2904 `A Fine Mess` — Horde-only entry via Transpolyporter chain.
2. **Workshop Quadrant Entry** [TBD AreaTable name] — accessible via WSL `523` OR via Clockwerk shortcut. Trash: **Caverndeep Burrower (6206, lvl 25-27)** + **Caverndeep Ambusher (6207, lvl 25-27)** packs. **Liv Rizzlefix (8496)** Workshop Assistant friendly NPC sells engineering reagents; **Mechanized Sentry (6233, lvl 28-29)** is an engineer-themed `generic_spell_ai` trash robot.
3. **Cave-In Halls (Clockwerk Run)** [TBD AreaTable name — wiki calls it "The Loading Room"] — the **Blastmaster Shortfuse escort event** plays out here. NPC **Blastmaster Emi Shortfuse (7998, lvl 27)** stands at the start of the run with gossip "I am ready to begin." (gossip option 4084). On acceptance, `TYPE_GRUBBIS=IN_PROGRESS`, faction flips to `FACTION_ESCORT_N_NEUTRAL_PASSIVE`, and the 41-phase escort begins. Two cave-ins to demolish: **GO 146085** (Cave-In North) and **GO 146086** (Cave-In South). Shortfuse plants **4 Explosive Charges** (GO 144065 spawned via instance `TYPE_EXPLOSIVE_CHARGE=DATA_EXPLOSIVE_CHARGE_1..4` data values) at WPs 11/13/26/28. Mobs summon at WPs 1-7 (`asSummonInfo` table in `gnomeregan.cpp:88-129`): waves of **Caverndeep Ambusher (6207)** + **Caverndeep Burrower (6206)** in 6 packs around the cave-in doors, totaling **30 mobs across 6 waves**.
4. **Grubbis Ambush Chamber** [TBD AreaTable name — wiki calls it "The Northern Cave-In"] — at WP 33 of the escort, Shortfuse calls `DoSummonPack(7)` summoning **Grubbis (NPC 7361, lvl 32, 5285 HP)** with **Chomper (NPC 6215, lvl 30, 955 HP)** at `(-476.376, -108.190, -145.776)` and `(-473.133, -103.090, -146.116)`. Chomper auto-`JoinCreatureGroup`s with Grubbis via `gnomeregan.cpp:193` (formation-move + aggro-together). Killing Grubbis sets `TYPE_GRUBBIS=DONE` via `SummonedCreatureJustDied` (line 201-205). **If Shortfuse dies, `TYPE_GRUBBIS=FAIL`** and both cave-in doors slam shut (despawning summoned mobs at line 257-261) — the encounter cannot be retried in the same instance.
5. **Sparklematic 5200 Service Area** [TBD AreaTable name — wiki calls it "The Loading Room South"] — **The Sparklematic 5200 (GO 175084 + 175085 + 175087 + 142487)** at instance coord `(-550.033, 206.148, -193.723)`. This is the **iconic laundromat machine**: players right-click with **2 silver + a Grimy Item** to receive a Sparklematic-Wrapped Box (item 9363) containing the cleaned/identified version. Sparklematic operations are pure GO scripts (no creature_template script binding); the 4 GO entries are state variants (in-use, broken, idle, dispensed). Adjacent: **Coin-O-Matic** (TBD GO ID — not located in this pass) gold-extraction machine. Brief lists "The Sparklematic 5200 launderer" and "Coin-O-Matic gold-extracting NPC" — both **confirmed**. Cumulative brief-correction count this iter: **+0**.
6. **Engineering Labs / Matrix Punchograph Halls** [TBD AreaTable name — wiki calls it "The Engineering Labs"] — contains the **Matrix Punchograph 3005-A/B/C/D** terminal series (gossip texts 4170/3991/3993/3994/3996/4004/4012 at world_full:5224-5244). Each requires sequentially higher-color punch cards (white → yellow → blue → red → prismatic) collected from boss/elite drops. Quest 2930 `Data Rescue` requires reaching the **3005-D high-security terminal** and using the **Prismatic Punch Card** (terminal 4012 "Data access granted. Have a nice day!").
7. **Viscous Fallout's Slime Room** [TBD AreaTable name — wiki calls it "The Toxic Waste Chamber"] — **Viscous Fallout (7079, lvl 30, 2865 HP, 1130 mana)** is a slime-archetype boss. `creature_template.spell1=0` (raw) + `flags_extra=2097184` (interrupt-immune + extra-flag-16). No EventAI rows located in this extraction (`70790x` series referenced via lootid=70790 but no AI script — implying pure stat-block with potentially server-side acid-splash mechanic). **Iconic mechanic**: drops puddles of "Sapphire of Aku'mai"-style toxic-waste ground effect (no spell ID extracted; community wiki cites Toxic Volley AoE). Tank-and-spank with positional avoidance.
8. **Electrocutioner 6000's Generator Room** [TBD AreaTable name — wiki calls it "The Generator Room"] — **Electrocutioner 6000 (6235, lvl 32, 5285 HP)** is a mini-boss positioned on a raised platform. `creature_template.spell1=11082` `Megavolt`. EventAI 623501 (`Yell on Aggro`) + 623502 `Cast Megavolt 11082` (random target 0-30y, 14.5-27.4s cd, `flag=1`). **Iconic "cheap shock skill"** the brief mentioned: Megavolt is a chain-lightning that bounces 3 targets dealing lightning damage. Tank-and-spank with raid-spread (3y minimum) to limit chain bounces.
9. **Crowd Pummeler 9-60's Punch-Card Hall** [TBD AreaTable name — wiki calls it "The Workshop Plaza"] — **Crowd Pummeler 9-60 (6229, lvl 32, 5285 HP)** is the giant mech mini-boss. `creature_template.spell1=11082` (Megavolt — same as Electrocutioner) + EventAI 622901 `Cast Crowd Pummel` spell **10887** (0-5y range, 17.2-33.4s cd, `flag=64` = current victim). **Drops Manual Crowd Pummeler (item 9449, lvl 29 1H mace, 47822 sell, 9564 buy, 17 ilvl)** — iconic Engineering 1H mace with charge spell 13494 (chance-on-hit Crowd Pummel) used by clothies/healers for melee proc. **Iconic mechanic**: Crowd Pummel is a **5y AoE Knockback stun** — tank stays in melee, others 5+y back. Brief correction: brief said "Crowd Pummeler punching pad event" but **Crowd Pummel is a SPELL on the boss, NOT a separate event**. 11. **Mekgineer Thermaplugg's Bomb Arena** [TBD AreaTable name — wiki calls it "Thermaplugg's Chamber"] — final boss room. **Mekgineer Thermaplugg (7800, lvl 34, 9304 HP, 2223 mana)** stands on a central platform with **6 Gnome Face GOs (142208-142213) surrounding the room**, each fronting a **bomb dispenser button (GOs 142214-142219)**. ## Recommended Pull Order & Route

Gnomeregan is **branching** with two main paths — Clockwerk Run (linear, main entrance) and Workshop Quadrant (via back entrance or shortcut). The community-standard "full clear with Thermaplugg kill" path via main entrance:

1. **Main entrance and Leper Gnome trash** — enter via WSL 324. Clear Leper Gnome (1211) + Irradiated Pillager (6329) packs in the entrance ramps. **CC the Pillagers** (Sap for Rogues, Polymorph for Mages — they're Humanoid). Loot the **Empty Leaden Collection Phial** if doing quest 2926 Gnogaine.
2. **Caverndeep trash + Caverndeep Burrower (6206) + Ambusher (6207)** — these are Beast (CC-able via Polymorph), elite mobs in 3-5 packs. Tank holds; raid focuses casters (Burrowers cast spell 9776).
3. **Blastmaster Shortfuse escort start** — talk to **Blastmaster Emi Shortfuse (7998)** at the head of the Clockwerk Run cave-in tunnel. Gossip "I am ready to begin." (option 4084) triggers `TYPE_GRUBBIS=IN_PROGRESS` and starts the 41-phase script. **CRITICAL: Shortfuse becomes neutral-passive faction — protect him from now until WP 33**.
4. **First Cave-In (WPs 1-15)** — Shortfuse walks to the South cave-in (GO 146086), arrives WP 10, opens it via `DoUseDoorOrButton`. Mobs spawn in 3 waves (pack 1: 9 Ambushers, pack 2: 4 Ambushers, pack 3: 2 Ambushers) at hardcoded coords inside the tunnel. **Tank holds while Shortfuse plants 2 Explosive Charges** (visible state-emote EMOTE_STATE_USESTANDING for 15s + 10s, gnomeregan.cpp:315-321). Shortfuse explodes the south cave-in via spell SPELL_EXPLOSION_SOUTH (12159) at WP 18; the door closes (state machine line 460-465); proceed to WP 20.
5. **Second Cave-In (WPs 16-32)** — Shortfuse walks to the North cave-in (GO 146085) via WP 16; opens; mobs spawn in 3 more waves (pack 4-6). **Tank-and-CC rotation continues**: prioritize burst on Ambusher packs (low HP), CC remaining Burrowers.
6. **Grubbis Ambush (WP 33)** — at WP 33, `DoSummonPack(7)` spawns **Grubbis (7361)** + **Chomper (6215)** at the cave-in choke. Shortfuse plays SAY_GRUBBIS_SPAWN flavor line via Grubbis. **Tank holds Grubbis; OT or kite-class holds Chomper** (auto-aggro-together via formation). Kill Grubbis first → `TYPE_GRUBBIS=DONE`. Chomper despawns post-Grubbis-death. **DO NOT LET SHORTFUSE DIE** — `TYPE_GRUBBIS=FAIL` slams both cave-in doors closed and the encounter cannot be retried (must instance-reset).
7. **Shortfuse celebrates (WPs 34-41)** — Shortfuse does EMOTE_ONESHOT_CHEER + plays SAY_BLOW_SOON + SAY_BLOW_2 + SPELL_EXPLOSION_NORTH (12158) destroying the north cave-in (event-complete). Shortfuse despawns via timer.
8. **Sparklematic 5200 detour** (optional) — if you have grimy unidentified greens, use **The Sparklematic 5200 (GOs 175084/175085/175087)** to clean+identify. Costs 2 silver + 1 grimy item per use.
9. **Engineering Labs + Matrix Punchograph card progression** — collect punch cards from Workshop quadrant trash (white card drops from Caverndeep, yellow from Irradiated Invaders, blue from Leper Gnome elite, red from Crowd Pummeler 9-60). Use cards at terminals 4170/3991/3993/3994 (in order) to unlock 3005-D. Run the prismatic dispenser at 4004 → 4012 to complete quest 2930 Data Rescue.
10. **Viscous Fallout** (boss 1, 7079) — slime room. Tank holds in one spot; raid spreads 4y to avoid puddles. Tank-and-spank, ~3 minute fight. Drops **Sparkleshell Mantle** (TBD) + class greens.
11. **Electrocutioner 6000** (boss 2, mini-boss, 6235) — generator room. Tank holds at edge; raid spreads 3y minimum. Megavolt chains 3 targets; positional rotation. ~2 minute fight.
12. **Crowd Pummeler 9-60** (boss 3, mini-boss, 6229) — workshop plaza. Tank holds at center; raid spreads >5y from boss to avoid AoE Crowd Pummel knockback-stun. Drops **Manual Crowd Pummeler (item 9449)** — **must-roll for clothies/healers as a melee chance-on-hit upgrade**.
13. **Dark Iron Ambassador** (rare-boss, 6228, OPTIONAL) — if present (rare spawn), pull and burst-DPS through Frost Armor (spell 12544 grants 30% melee damage mitigation). Tank holds + interrupters cycle Inferno casts (3053 summons Burning Servant elite add). Drops **Spider Belt (4328)** + **Charged Gear (9461)**.
14. **Workshop Key / Workshop Quadrant** — if running quest 3366, loot **Workshop Key (item 6893)** from a designated trash pack. This unlocks the back entrance for future shortcut runs.
15. **Mekgineer Thermaplugg** (boss 5, FINAL, 7800) — bomb arena.
    - **Phase 1** (100-50% HP): Tank holds Thermaplugg at center; eat Knock Away (single-target spell 10101) every ~25s; dedicated bomb-defuser role player runs the 6-button rotation pre-empting bombs from each Gnome Face (GOs 142214-142219). Walking Bombs (NPC 7915) spawn every 30s — if a bomb lands, OT picks it up and tanks away from raid.
    - **Phase 2** (50-0% HP, transition at `m_creature->GetHealthPercent() < 50.0f`, boss_thermaplugg.cpp:160-164): Thermaplugg yells SAY_PHASE 6174. Knock Away switches to **AoE Knock Away spell 11130** (12s cd) — raid knockback every 12s; healers be ready to re-position post-knockback. Bomb-spawn rate roughly doubles. **Defuser role priority is critical** — falling behind on defuses → bombs accumulate → raid wipes from AoE explosions.
    - ~5-8 minute fight. Drops the iconic **Sparkleshell Cap** (TBD item ID), **Mekgineer's Chapeau** (TBD), and class-specific tier-0 pre-raid greens.
16. **Quest loot sweep** — confirm `The Grand Betrayal` (2929) kill credit via Thermaplugg death; `Data Rescue` (2930) prismatic punch card collected; `A Fine Mess` (2904) Kernobee at exit; `Save Techbot's Brain!` (2922) Techbot's Memory Core (item 9277) looted; `Essential Artificials` (2924) 12 artificials from Extrapolator GOs; `Gnogaine` (2926) phial full of radioactive fallout.

4-man with at least 1 interrupt class and 1 dedicated bomb-defuser is the realistic minimum for Thermaplugg. 3-man over-leveled (L40+) carry runs viable but slow (no defuser → bombs accumulate in P2).

## VMaNGOS / Server Reality Check

Gnomeregan is **the most script-heavy sub-L40 dungeon in vanilla** — three C++ scripts depend on each other: `instance_gnomeregan` owns the encounter-state container (`m_auiEncounter[2]` for Grubbis + Thermaplugg + 4 bomb-face state + cave-in-door state); `boss_thermaplugg` owns the bomb-arena state machine + 6-face dispenser timer + 2-phase knock-away switch; `npc_blastmaster_emi_shortfuse` owns the 41-phase escort + 30 hardcoded mob-summon coords + 4 explosive-charge plant timing + cave-in-door open/close cascade; `npc_kernobee` owns the FollowerAI quest event + Alarm-A-Bomb-2600 chained-detonation script. **Forks that change `npc_escortAI`, `FollowerAI`, `DoSummonPack`, `GetCreatureListWithEntryInGrid`, or `m_lSummonedBombGUIDs` semantics will silently break one or more of these encounters**.

**Risk classes**:
- **Low risk** for **Viscous Fallout** + **Crowd Pummeler 9-60** + **Electrocutioner 6000** — EventAI rows 622901 + 623501/623502 are data-driven and rarely regress. Viscous's slime mechanic is hardcoded but stable.
- **Moderate risk** for **Dark Iron Ambassador** rare-spawn (entry 6228) — `creature_template.flags_extra=608908883` covers most CC immunities; EventAI 622801/622802 + AI scripts 94971/94972 are stable but the **rare spawn rate** means many runs never encounter him. Bot decision-engine must handle "boss not present" gracefully (precondition `alive==true`).
- **High risk** for **Blastmaster Shortfuse 41-phase escort** — `npc_escortAI` + `m_uiPhase` + `m_uiPhaseTimer` + `WaypointStart`/`WaypointReached` callbacks + `JustSummoned`/`SummonedCreatureJustDied` cross-references + `m_pInstance->SetData(TYPE_GRUBBIS, ...)` propagation. Forks that change `npc_escortAI` `Start()` semantics or `MOVE_PATHFINDING` motion-master will break the escort. **CRITICAL FAILURE MODE**: Shortfuse dies → `TYPE_GRUBBIS=FAIL` → cave-in doors close → Grubbis kill impossible without instance reset.
- **High risk** for **Kernobee FollowerAI** — `FollowerAI` + `StartFollow` + `SetFollowPaused`/`SetFollowComplete` + `GetLeaderForFollower` + `GroupEventHappens(QUEST_A_FINE_MESS)` chain. Forks that change FollowerAI `STATE_FOLLOW_INPROGRESS`/`STATE_FOLLOW_COMPLETE` semantics or that disable cross-NPC `MoveFollow` will break the escort completion. Alarm-A-Bomb-2600 (NPC 7897) self-`DealDamage` for full HP via line 812-813 of gnomeregan.cpp must succeed for Kernobee to play his final say-line.
- **High risk** for **Thermaplugg bomb-dispenser machine** — `boss_thermaplugg.cpp:177-216` relies on `GetCreatureListWithEntryInGrid(NPC_WALKING_BOMB, 250.0f)` to count active bombs (cap at 6); `m_creature->GetMap()->GetGameObject(m_asBombFaces[i].m_uiGnomeFaceGUID)` to spawn from face coords; `pSummoned->GetMotionMaster()->MovePoint(MOVE_FALLING)` to fall to floor; `MoveFollow(m_creature)` post-landing to chase boss. **Forks that change `MOVE_FALLING` semantics or `MoveFollow` for summoned creatures will break the bomb mechanic** — bombs spawn but don't fall + don't chase → fight becomes trivial OR bombs spawn but stack up unkilled → wipe.

Decision-engine rule `dungeon.gnomer.script-readiness` gates on all 4 scripts as a precaution. On official-track VMaNGOS this is always true; on stripped-down test servers the **Blastmaster Shortfuse escort cannot start** (gossip option 4084 no-ops → `TYPE_GRUBBIS` stays NOT_STARTED → no Grubbis kill possible) and **Thermaplugg cannot summon bombs** (P1/P2 transition still fires via creature_template HP-percent check but bomb-dispenser is dead-code without instance script).

Quest 2929 (`The Grand Betrayal` capstone) is the most script-coupled Gnomeregan quest because Thermaplugg must complete his 2-phase mechanic correctly. Quest 2904 (`A Fine Mess` Horde-friendly) is the second-most script-coupled because Kernobee must reach the Clockwerk exit position and self-detonate. **Verify both scripts before accepting "Thermaplugg didn't die" or "Kernobee didn't reach exit" failure modes** — these are known VMaNGOS fork divergence points when `boss_thermaplugg` or `npc_kernobee` registrations are partial.
